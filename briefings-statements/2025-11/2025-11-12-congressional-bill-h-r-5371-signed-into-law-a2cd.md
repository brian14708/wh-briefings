title: Congressional Bill H.R. 5371 Signed into Law
---
 
On Wednesday, November 12, 2025, the President signed into law:

H.R. 5371, the “Continuing Appropriations, Agriculture, Legislative
Branch, Military Construction and Veterans Affairs, and Extensions Act,
2026,” which makes continuing appropriations and extensions for fiscal
year 2026, and for other purposes.
