title: Congressional Bill S. 269 Signed into Law
---
 
On Tuesday, February 10, 2026, the President signed into law:

S. 269, the “Ending Improper Payments to Deceased People Act,” which
permanently allows sharing of death data to prevent and recover improper
payments.
