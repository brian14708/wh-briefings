title: S. 254 Signed into Law
---
 
On Friday, June 12, 2026, the President signed into law:

S. 254, the “Alaska’s Right to Ivory Sales and Tradition Act” or the
“ARTIST Act,” which amends the Marine Mammal Protection Act of 1972 to
prohibit States from enacting State-level prohibitions on the interstate
commerce, importation, sale, transfer, trade, barter, or possession of
marine mammal ivory, bone, or baleen produced by an Alaska Native as an
authentic Alaska Native article of handicrafts and clothing.
