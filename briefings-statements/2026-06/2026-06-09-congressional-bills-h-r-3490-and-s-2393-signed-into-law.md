title: Congressional Bills H.R. 3490 and S. 2393 Signed into Law
---
 
On Tuesday, June 9, 2026, the President signed into law:

H.R. 3490, the “Gerald E. Connolly Esophageal Cancer Awareness Act of
2025,” which requires the Government Accountability Office to produce a
report on esophageal cancer; and

S. 2393, the “Fiscal Year 2025 Veterans Affairs Major Medical Facility
Authorization Act,” which authorizes a major medical facility project
for the Department of Veterans Affairs in St. Louis, Missouri.
