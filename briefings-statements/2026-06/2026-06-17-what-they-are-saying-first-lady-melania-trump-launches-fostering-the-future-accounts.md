  Accounts'
---
 
Last week, First Lady Melania Trump launched Fostering the Future
Accounts, a new financial resource established to empower foster youth
to be fiscally autonomous in adulthood. Developed in partnership with
the Department of the Treasury, these accounts will provide eligible
youth in foster care with tax-advantaged investment and savings accounts
to help build long-term financial stability and expand opportunities for
future success.
