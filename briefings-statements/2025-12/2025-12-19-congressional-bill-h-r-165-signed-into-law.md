title: Congressional Bill H.R. 165 Signed into Law
---
 
On Friday, December 19, 2025, the President signed into law:

H.R. 165, the “Wounded Knee Massacre Memorial and Sacred Site Act,”
which directs the Secretary of the Interior to take actions necessary to
place certain land in South Dakota into restricted fee status for the
Oglala and Cheyenne River Sioux Tribes.
