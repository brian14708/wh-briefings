title: Congressional Bills H.J. Res 133 and H.R. 1512 Signed into Law
---
 
On Tuesday, December 2, 2025, the President signed into law:

H.J. Res 133, which requests the Secretary of the Interior to authorize
unique and one-time arrangements for displays on the National Mall and
the Washington Monument during the period beginning on December 31,
2025, and ending on January 5, 2026; and

H.R. 1512, the “Taiwan Assurance Implementation Act,” which permanently
extends existing requirements for the Department of State to review and
report on its guidance to Executive Branch agencies and offices on
relations with Taiwan.
