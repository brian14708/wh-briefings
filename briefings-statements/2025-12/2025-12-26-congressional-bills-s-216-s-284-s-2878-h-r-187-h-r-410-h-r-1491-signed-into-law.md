title: Congressional Bills S.216, S.284, S.2878, H.R. 187, H.R. 410, H.R. 1491 Signed
  into Law
---
 
On Friday, December 26, 2025, the President signed into law:

S. 216, the “Save Our Seas 2.0 Amendments Act,” which authorizes
appropriations for the Marine Debris Program through Fiscal Year (FY)
2029 and the Marine Debris Foundation for FY 2025; and modifies the
administrative requirements of the Program and Foundation;

S. 284, the “Congressional Award Program Reauthorization Act,” which
retroactively reauthorizes the Congressional Award Board, which
administers the Congressional Awards Program to recognize the
achievements of young people, effective as if enacted on October 1,
2023, through October 1, 2028;

S. 2878, the “Great Lakes Fishery Research Reauthorization Act,” which
reauthorizes funding to monitor, assess, and research fisheries in the
Great Lakes Basin through Fiscal Year 2030;

H.R. 187, the “Modernizing Access to our Public Waters Act of 2025” or
the “MAPWaters Act of 2025,” which directs the Department of Agriculture
and the Department of the Interior to standardize and publish date
relating to public outdoor recreational access of Federal waterways and
Federal fishing restrictions;

H.R. 410, the “Alaska Native Vietnam Era Veterans Land Allotment
Extension Act of 2025”, which extends the Alaska Native Vietnam Era
Veterans Land Allotment Program through December 29, 2030; and

H.R. 1491, the “Disaster Related Extension of Deadlines Act,” which
requires the Department of the Treasury to treat the postponement of the
tax return deadline to a federally-declared disaster or certain other
events as an extension of such deadline for purposes of calculating the
limit on a tax refund.
