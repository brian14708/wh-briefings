title: Presidential Message on Holi
---
 
Melania and I send our best wishes to all who celebrate the Hindu
festival of Holi—a day welcoming the arrival of spring and rejoicing in
the victory of good over evil.

Today, as communities gather around the world to celebrate this festival
of colors, I wish all Americans who observe Holi a day filled with hope,
peace, and unity.
