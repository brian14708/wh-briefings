title: Presidential Message on Eid al-Fitr
---
 
The First Lady and I send our best wishes to every American celebrating
Eid al-Fitr.

Eid al-Fitr, also known as the Festival of Breaking the Fast, unites
Muslim families, friends, and communities in honor of their spiritual
devotion at the conclusion of Ramadan.  It also serves as a reminder of
the foundational American principle of religious liberty, which my
Administration is fighting for every day.

We offer our warmest greetings for a blessed Eid al-Fitr to all who
celebrate.
