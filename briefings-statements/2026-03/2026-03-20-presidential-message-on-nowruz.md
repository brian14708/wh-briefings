title: Presidential Message on Nowruz
---
 
Today, I send my best wishes to every American celebrating Nowruz.

Nowruz marks the start of the Persian New Year, celebrates the arrival
of spring, and acknowledges the eternal triumph of light over darkness. 
This ancient holiday also serves as a yearly reflection of the power of
new beginnings.

As we continue our righteous mission in the Middle East, I pray that
this Nowruz will promote the causes of peace and human dignity on
American shores and all across the world, and that the year ahead will
be filled with many blessings.

Nowruz Pirouz!
