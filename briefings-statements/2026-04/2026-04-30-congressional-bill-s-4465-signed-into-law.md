title: Congressional Bill S. 4465 Signed into Law

On Thursday, April 30, 2026, the President signed into law:

S. 4465, which amends the FISA Amendments Act of 2008 to extend the
authorities of title VII of the Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Act of
1978, and for other purposes.
