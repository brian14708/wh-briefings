title: "First Lady Melania Trump\u2019s Elegant State Dinner and Groundbreaking VR\
  \ Educational Experience Create a Historic State Visit for Their Majesties King\
  \ Charles III and Queen Camilla"

“The visit by Their Majesties King Charles III and Queen Camilla on the
250th anniversary of our nation transcends ceremony. It symbolizes our
modern society’s enduring commitment to freedom and human potential,”
exclaimed First Lady Melania Trump. The First Lady created a memorable
experience for Their Majesties, blending regard for the history of both
nations, plus a look ahead for the next generation.  

First Lady Melania Trump led a cross-cultural educational tech
experience for American students, which aligned with her Fostering the
Future Together’s mission. The First Lady stated, “The students enjoyed
a groundbreaking educational experience which brought them to Buckingham
Palace through the use of VR, while physically standing on the White
House Tennis Pavilion grounds.” The participants, many of whom were
Presidential AI Challenge State Champions, learned more about the iconic
places they visited through discussions with Queen Camilla and First
Lady Melania Trump, after removing their Meta Quest headgear.

“I was honored to host Their Majesties for a State Dinner that reflected
the warmth and history shared between our countries,” exclaimed Mrs.
Trump. The First Lady organized a white tie dinner, the most formal
evening dress code, in honor of Their Majesties King Charles III and
Queen Camilla.

Mrs. Trump’s refined design signature included floral arrangements
inspired by overflowing British gardens, cascading candelabras,
sophisticated live music performances, and a menu featuring a delightful
beehive shaped gâteau made with honey harvested directly from the White
House bees. The dinner brought together distinguished guests from across
government, technology, business, sports, and culture—showcasing
American hospitality, craftsmanship, and culinary excellence.

Throughout the visit, the President and First Lady hosted Their
Majesties for a series of engagements that reflected the enduring
friendship between the United States of America and the United Kingdom
of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, grounded in deeply shared
history, values, and a commitment to future generations. Among these was
a pleasant visit to the newly expanded White House Beehive on Monday and
a historic State Arrival Ceremony on Tuesday featuring the first ever
military pass in review at the White House, a 21-gun cannon salute, and
full military honors with the involvement of nearly 500 members of the
United States Armed Forces.

From the arrival ceremony to the final engagements, the State Visit
reaffirmed the strength of the U.S.–U.K. partnership and highlighted the
importance of continued collaboration across diplomacy, education, and
innovation. The First Lady is proud to have led the preparations for the
visit—honoring tradition while embracing the promise of the future.
