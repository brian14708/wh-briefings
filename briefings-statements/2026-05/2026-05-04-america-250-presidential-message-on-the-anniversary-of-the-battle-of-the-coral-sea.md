title: 'America 250: Presidential Message on the Anniversary of the Battle of the
  Coral Sea'
---
 
Today, our Nation commemorates the 84th anniversary of the Battle of the
Coral Sea, honoring the brave Americans and Australians who joined
forces to turn the tide of World War II in the Pacific against tyranny. 
Through their shared sacrifice and courage, an enduring bond was forged
between our peoples—one that has weathered the trials of history and
remains a cornerstone of security and prosperity in the Indo-Pacific and
beyond.

For 4 grueling days in the Coral Sea, Americans and Australians fought
alongside one another, launching relentless air strikes against the
invasion fleet.  It was the first ever carrier-to-carrier naval
engagement in military history, with opposing fleets never seeing or
firing directly on one another but rather carrying out the mission from
the sky.  After enduring days of intense combat, Allied forces struck
with precision, halting a major enemy advancement through the Pacific
Theatre and delivering a critical blow to its naval strength, ultimately
paving the way for the decisive victory at Midway a month later.

Out of the ashes of that hard-won triumph emerged an alliance between
the United States and Australia that remains stronger than ever
before—and my Administration is fully committed to deepening our
strategic partnership in the years to come.  Last October, I was proud
to secure a landmark agreement with Australia to expand our shared
access to rare earth and critical minerals—an essential component of
America’s ability to deter threats, confront our adversaries, and secure
our economic prosperity for generations to come.

As we reflect on this solemn anniversary of the Battle of the Coral Sea,
we honor the unwavering grit of our service members and remember those
heroes who willingly laid down their lives in the defense of freedom. 
Standing shoulder to shoulder with our Australian partners, we will
continue to champion the cause of peace and stability throughout the
Indo-Pacific, ushering in a new Golden Age of security and prosperity
for our Nation and our allies around the world.
