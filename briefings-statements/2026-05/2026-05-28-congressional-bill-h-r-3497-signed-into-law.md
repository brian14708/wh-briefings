title: Congressional Bill H.R. 3497 Signed into Law
---
 
On Thursday, May 28, 2026, the President signed into law:

H.R. 3497, the “Medal of Sacrifice Act of 2025,” which establishes the
Medal of Sacrifice to honor certain eligible law enforcement officers
and first responders who are killed in the line of duty.
