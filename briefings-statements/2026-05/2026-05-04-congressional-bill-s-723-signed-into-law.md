title: Congressional Bill S. 723 Signed into Law
---
 
On Monday, May 4, 2026, the President signed into law:

S. 723, the “Tribal Trust Land Homeownership Act of 2025,” which
codifies and establishes certain deadlines for the Bureau of Indian
Affairs to process residential and business leasehold mortgages on trust
or restricted Indian land.
