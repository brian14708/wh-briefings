title: Presidential Message on Loyalty and Law Day, U.S.A.
---
 
For 250 glorious years, the rule of law and the blessings of
freedom have stood as the bedrock of our Republic and
the fount of our greatness.  On Loyalty and Law Day, we
reaffirm our solemn commitment to defending our unalienable
rights and preserving our freedoms.

Since our founding on July 4, 1776, the righteous principles of equal
justice, law and order, and the dignity of every human
person have been faithfully defended by generations of patriots.  In
fact, from the very beginning of our national story, responsible
citizenship has meant obeying our laws, defending our flag, honoring our
heritage, protecting our homeland, and safeguarding our constitutional
way of life.  These foundational truths—and our steadfast commitment to
them—have made our Nation the greatest beacon of freedom and liberty the
world has ever known.  From the Revolutionaries who fought to secure our
independence to the brave Americans who stood firm against communist
threats during the Cold War, our history is defined by a citizenry with
an unyielding devotion to God, an abiding loyalty to the Constitution,
and a deep love of country. 

My Administration is proudly carrying forth this enduring legacy as
we restore our justice system, uphold law and order,
and eliminate threats to our freedom.  Since I returned to office, we
have ended the era of lawlessness and the weaponization
of our justice system and reclaimed our
constitutional heritage. America’s borders are secure.  Our cities
are reemerging safe, vibrant, and beautiful.  And our
precious liberties are once again being safeguarded.

Under my leadership, we are in the new Golden Age of America—one that
remains firmly rooted in respect for our laws, reverence for our
Constitution, and an unwavering commitment to the eternal truths that
have guided our Nation for two and a half centuries. 

Today, we celebrate the revolutionary framework of governance enshrined
in our Constitution, and we pledge to defend it
with unshakeable courage and devotion.  As we commemorate 250 years of
American independence, we renew our resolve to secure a future where the
rule of law prevails, liberty endures, and the forces of tyranny and
evil remain forever defeated.
