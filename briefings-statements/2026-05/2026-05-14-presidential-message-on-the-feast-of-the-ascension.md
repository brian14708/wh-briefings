title: Presidential Message on the Feast of the Ascension
---
 
Today, Melania and I join with many Christians in the United States and
all around the world who are celebrating the memorial feast day of the
Ascension of Jesus into Heaven.

Forty days ago, the world celebrated the miracle of Easter, when Jesus
rose from the dead following His death on the Cross.  On the Feast of
the Ascension, we celebrate yet another great miracle:  His bodily
departure from Earth and return to His Father in Heaven.

On this special day, we are reminded that, for millions of Americans,
the love of Christ gives our lives—and our country—the deepest meaning,
purpose, and identity.  As we celebrate 250 years of American freedom
and independence, we remember that America remains a great Nation
because the United States has always been a Nation blessed by God.
