title: First Lady Melania Trump to Ring Opening Bell of the New York Stock Exchange
---
 
First Lady Melania Trump will ring the opening bell of the New York
Stock Exchange on Wednesday, January 28, 2026, following an impactful
first year of leadership as First Lady of the United States during the
second term of the Trump Administration.

Over the past year, Mrs. Trump has taken action to advance a
results-driven agenda that is centered on the well-being of children in
the United States and around the world. Some of her achievements
include:

-   Championing the [***TAKE IT DOWN
    Act***](https://whitehouse.us10.list-manage.com/track/click?u=dace49741569f7585670378b3&id=a732159e10&e=e7ee628798),
    a new law that enhances federal online protections for children from
    online deepfakes.

<!-- -->

-   Leading an [**Executive Order on Fostering the
    Future**](https://whitehouse.us10.list-manage.com/track/click?u=dace49741569f7585670378b3&id=570a81deea&e=e7ee628798),
    which unites federal resources with innovative public- and
    private-sector partnerships to provide more pathways to success for
    young people who have experienced foster care.

<!-- -->

-   Launching the [**Presidential Artificial Intelligence (AI)
    Challenge**](https://whitehouse.us10.list-manage.com/track/click?u=dace49741569f7585670378b3&id=ec8335a0cc&e=e7ee628798)
    and hosting a meeting of the [**White House AI Task
    Force**](https://whitehouse.us10.list-manage.com/track/click?u=dace49741569f7585670378b3&id=262c38ca8d&e=e7ee628798)
    to leverage the power of the private sector to empower the next
    generation to responsibly explore and innovate with AI alongside
    parents and educators.

<!-- -->

-   Securing [**$25 million for housing
    supports**](https://whitehouse.us10.list-manage.com/track/click?u=dace49741569f7585670378b3&id=d8a2cbab59&e=e7ee628798)
    in President Trumpâ€™s Fiscal Year 2026 Budget for youth transitioning
    out of foster care.

<!-- -->

-   Leading [**reunification efforts in Ukraine and
    Russia**](https://whitehouse.us10.list-manage.com/track/click?u=dace49741569f7585670378b3&id=39fcfa8522&e=e7ee628798)
    to bring together children and families separated by the ongoing
    conflict.

<!-- -->

-   Establishing [**Fostering the Future
    Together**](https://whitehouse.us10.list-manage.com/track/click?u=dace49741569f7585670378b3&id=12cab03b69&e=e7ee628798)
    during the United Nations General Assembly, a global coalition of
    first spouses of nations to help children learn, grow, and thrive
    through the safe and innovative use of advanced technology.

More information for media on the New York Stock Exchange opening bell
