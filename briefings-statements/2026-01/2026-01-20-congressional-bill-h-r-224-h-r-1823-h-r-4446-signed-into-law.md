title: Congressional Bill H.R. 224, H.R. 1823, H.R. 4446 Signed into Law
---
 
On Tuesday, January 20, 2026, the President signed into law:

H.R. 224, the “Disabled Veterans Housing Support Act,” which amends
section 102(a)(20) of the Housing and Community Development Act of 1974
to require the exclusion of service-connected disability compensation
when determining whether a person is a person of low and moderate
income, a person of low income, or a person of moderate income, and for
other purposes;

H.R. 1823, the “VA Budget Shortfall Accountability Act,” which requires
a report on certain funding shortfalls in the Department of Veterans
Affairs; and H.R. 4446, the “Focused Assistance and Skills Training for
Veterans’ Employment and Transition Success Act” or the “FAST VETS Act,”
which specifies the conditions under which a veteran’s vocational
rehabilitation plan must be redeveloped.
