title: Congressional Bill S. 222 Signed into Law
---
 
On Wednesday, January 14, 2026, the President signed into law:

S. 222, the “Whole Milk for Healthy Kids Act of 2025,” which modifies
the requirements for milk provided by schools participating in the
National School Lunch Program.
