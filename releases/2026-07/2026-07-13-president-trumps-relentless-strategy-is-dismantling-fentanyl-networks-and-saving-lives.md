  \ and Saving Lives"

**This week, the Trump Administration convenes the inaugural**
[**Fentanyl Free America
Summit**](https://www.dea.gov/fentanyl-free-summit)**, bringing together
partners from across the country to lock in and accelerate the historic
progress achieved against the deadliest drug threat in history.**

Thanks to the Trump Administration’s relentless, multi-front campaign —
securing the border, targeting transnational gangs, and applying maximum
pressure on foreign suppliers and narcoterrorists — fentanyl deaths have
collapsed and continue falling sharply.

### **Under President Trump, the fentanyl death toll has plunged:**

- Synthetic opioid (fentanyl)
  deaths [dropped](https://www.cdc.gov/nchs/pressroom/releases/20260513.html) 22%
  in a single year — from 48,913 in 2024 to 38,084 in 2025.
- Total drug overdose
  deaths [fell](https://www.cdc.gov/nchs/pressroom/releases/20260513.html) 14%
  — from 81,313 in 2024 to 69,973 in 2025.
- America recorded its
  [lowest](https://blogs.cdc.gov/nchs/2026/07/02/7934/) overall death
  rate ever in 2025, driven [in large
  part](https://www.wsj.com/health/u-s-death-rate-falls-to-its-lowest-point-on-record-9b7777c5#:~:text=The%20main%20driver%20of%20the%20record%20low%20was%20a%20continued%20drop%20in%20drug%20overdose%20deaths%2C%20said%20Farida%20Ahmad%2C%20a%20health%20scientist%20at%20the%20CDC%20and%20one%20of%20the%20report%E2%80%99s%20co%2Dauthors.)
  by these [sharp
  declines](https://www.newsweek.com/why-us-death-rate-has-fallen-record-low-12154744#:~:text=Although%20the%20provisional%20mortality%20report%20does%20not%20identify%20a%20single%20cause%20for%20the%20record%2Dlow%20death%20rate%2C%20Ahmad%20pointed%20to%20falling%20overdose%20deaths%20as%20a%20significant%20factor.)
  in drug fatalities.

### **President Trump is dismantling the fentanyl supply chain at every link:**

- President Trump
  [designated](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/designating-cartels-and-other-organizations-as-foreign-terrorist-organizations-and-specially-designated-global-terrorists/) major
  cartels — including Tren de Aragua, MS-13, and Sinaloa — as Foreign
  Terrorist Organizations and Specially Designated Global Terrorists,
  unlocking powerful
  [sanctions](https://ofac.treasury.gov/media/934096/download?inline),
  asset seizures, and enhanced prosecutorial tools to crush their
  operations.
- President
  Trump [signed](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2025/07/president-trump-signs-halt-fentanyl-act-into-law/) the
  [HALT Fentanyl
  Act](https://www.congress.gov/bill/119th-congress/senate-bill/331),
  permanently classifying fentanyl analogs as Schedule I drugs and
  closing the chemical loopholes traffickers had exploited for years.
- President
  Trump [designated](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/12/designating-fentanyl-as-a-weapon-of-mass-destruction/) illicit
  fentanyl and its core precursors as Weapons of Mass Destruction,
  enabling harsher penalties, expanded seizure authorities, and treating
  this poison with the full force of national security tools.

### **President Trump is applying maximum pressure on foreign suppliers and enablers:**

- President Trump
  [imposed](https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2025/02/07/2025-02408/imposing-duties-to-address-the-synthetic-opioid-supply-chain-in-the-peoples-republic-of-china) targeted
  fentanyl tariffs on China —
  later [expanded](https://www.whitehouse.gov/fact-sheets/2025/02/fact-sheet-president-donald-j-trump-imposes-tariffs-on-imports-from-canada-mexico-and-china/) to
  Canada and Mexico — forcing Beijing to [tighten
  controls](https://www.nytimes.com/2025/11/10/world/asia/china-fentanyl-united-states-trade.html) on
  13 key precursor chemicals.
- President
  Trump [closed](https://www.cbp.gov/newsroom/national-media-release/cbp-ready-enforce-end-de-minimis-loophole-securing-borders-and) the
  de minimis loophole, ending the flow of millions of uninspected
  packages that traffickers used to smuggle fentanyl precursors and
  finished product into the country.

### **President Trump is disrupting the supply of fentanyl at its source:**

- U.S. military forces have
  [conducted](https://www.nytimes.com/interactive/2025/10/29/us/us-caribbean-pacific-boat-strikes.html)
  strikes on dozens of narcotrafficking vessels in the Pacific and
  Caribbean, severely degrading maritime supply routes.
- Nationwide fentanyl
  seizures [fell](https://homeland.house.gov/2025/12/22/border-brief-christmas-comes-early-as-the-trump-effect-leads-to-another-month-of-record-low-border-encounters/#:~:text=Nationwide%20fentanyl%20seizures%20in%20FY25%C2%A0decreased%2045%20percent%20compared%20to%20FY24%2C%20highlighting%20deterrence) 45%
  in FY2025 as upstream disruption starved the flow reaching U.S.
  borders.
- DEA testing
  [confirms](https://abc7news.com/post/disruption-fentanyl-supply-chain-is-working-purity-levels-take-drastic-drop-dea-says/18296991/)
  President Trump’s sustained pressure is forcing traffickers to dilute
  product; only 29% of seized fentanyl pills now contain a potentially
  lethal dose — down from 76% just two years ago.

**President Trump’s ironclad commitment to border security and
relentless pressure on every node of the supply chain is delivering
these lifesaving results — and America is finally winning the fight
against fentanyl.**
