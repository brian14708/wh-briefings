Democrat New Jersey Gov. Mikie Sherrill
[admitted](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/07/21/nyregion/noncitizen-voting-new-jersey-error.html)
today that a “software glitch” registered approximately 6,600
noncitizens to vote in her state between 2023 and 2024 — and hundreds of
them actually cast ballots.

**This is the direct result of Democrat policies that refuse basic
citizenship checks — and exactly why Congress must pass the** [**SAVE
America Act**](https://www.whitehouse.gov/saveamerica/) **immediately.**
The bill
[requires](https://www.congress.gov/crs-product/IF12902#:~:text=The%20bill%20would%20require%20states%2C%20within%2030%20days%20of%20enactment%2C%20to%20submit%20voter%20lists%20to%20the%20USCIS/DHS%20SAVE%20system%20to%20verify%20citizenship%20for%20voter%20eligibility.%20As%20indicated%20previously%2C%20states%20currently%20may%20use%20the%20system%20to%20verify%20voter%20eligibility.)
states to use the Department of Homeland Security’s [SAVE
system](https://www.uscis.gov/save/about-save/about-save) to confirm
citizenship before registering anyone to vote in federal elections, a
safeguard New Jersey has [refused to
implement](https://www.uscis.gov/save/about-save/save-agency-search-tool?%252Fviews%252Fajax%253F%252Fviews%252Fajax%253F%252Fviews%252Fajax%253F%252Fviews%252Fajax%253F%252Fviews%252Fajax%253Fbenefit_category%5B0%5D=97&items_per_%252Fviews%252Fajax%253Fbenefit_category%5B0%5D=97&%252Fviews%252Fajax%253F%252Fviews%252Fajax%253Fbenefit_category%5B0%5D=97&%252Fviews%252Fajax%253F%252Fviews%252Fajax%253F%252Fviews%252Fajax%253Fbenefit_category%5B0%5D=97&%252Fviews%252Fajax%253Fbenefit_category%5B0%5D=97&topic_id=&agency_state_code%5B0%5D=34&agency_zip_code=&benefit_category%5B0%5D=97&benefit_category%5B1%5D=98&markerVal=&items_per_page=10).

The legislation also mandates voter ID for federal elections, a reform
supported by the [overwhelming
majority](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2026/03/the-save-america-act-is-the-most-popular-election-reform-in-decades/)
of Americans, among other [popular
initiatives](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2026/03/another-new-poll-shows-massive-support-for-save-america-act/).

Every illegal ballot dilutes the voice of a lawful American citizen and
undermines election integrity.These incidents are not random errors;
they are the predictable result of Democrat policies that refuse to
verify citizenship.

**The American people demand secure elections. Congress must pass the
SAVE America Act without delay.**
