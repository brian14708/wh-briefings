**Last night, President Donald J. Trump released** [**newly declassified
intelligence**](https://www.whitehouse.gov/election-integrity/) **that
had been hidden from the American people for years.** The documents
reveal that foreign adversaries, led by China, have compromised U.S.
voter data on a massive scale and that America’s election infrastructure
remains dangerously vulnerable to hacking and manipulation —
intelligence that officials deliberately suppressed.

Radical Left Democrats and their Fake News allies immediately moved to
discredit, downplay, and distract from the shocking disclosures. **Here
are the facts:**

**CLAIM: “President Trump’s address contained no new information.”**

- **FACT:** President Trump released a substantial body of newly
  declassified intelligence that had never been made public — or even
  shared with the President at the time. This includes assessments
  [confirming](https://www.whitehouse.gov/election-integrity/#:~:text=As%20one%20assessment%20states%3A%20%E2%80%9CWe%20judge%20that%20U.S.%20adversaries%2C%20including%20at%20a%20minimum%20Russia%2C%20China%2C%20Iran%2C%20and%20North%20Korea%2C%20as%20well%20as%20non%2Dstate%20groups%2C%20have%20the%20capability%20to%20compromise%20U.S.%20election%20infrastructure.%E2%80%9D)
  that China, Russia, Iran, North Korea, and other malign actors have
  the capability to compromise U.S. election infrastructure, as well as
  [evidence](https://www.whitehouse.gov/election-integrity/#:~:text=Many%20people%20have,hacked%20or%20compromised.)
  that the Maduro regime in Venezuela successfully developed methods to
  digitally alter vote totals in undetectable ways — information that
  has major implications in our own elections.

**CLAIM: “We already knew foreign adversaries try to interfere in our
elections.”**

- **FACT:** The point is to expose the extent to which foreign
  adversaries were attempting to interfere in our election. For example,
  President Trump
  [exposed](https://www.whitehouse.gov/election-integrity/#:~:text=Over%20a%20period,the%20information%20hidden.)
  how China obtained the personal data of 220 million American voters
  — names, addresses, phone numbers, and party affiliations — during the
  2020 election cycle, representing the largest known compromise of U.S.
  voter information in history. Intelligence agencies knew about this
  breach in at least 18 states and kept it hidden from the President and
  Congress.

**CLAIM: “President Trump was in office in 2020, so he was undermined by
his own team.”**

- **FACT:** That is exactly the point: Deep State elements within the
  government withheld important assessments from President Trump. One
  analyst admitted to “deliberately massaging” the President’s Daily
  Brief to keep election-related intelligence out, while an FBI official
  even described running “a shadow government” to prevent this
  information from reaching the President.

**CLAIM: “The voter data China obtained is already public information
anyway.”**

- **FACT:** When China obtained similar data on British voters, the
  Biden Administration
  [responded](https://www.wsj.com/world/europe/u-k-to-blame-china-for-hacking-details-of-millions-of-voters-a94697fe?st=HEoHxt)
  with sanctions and criminal charges. When Iran hackers obtained just
  100,000 American voter files, the Biden Department of Justice brought
  [indictments](https://www.justice.gov/archives/opa/pr/two-iranian-nationals-charged-cyber-enabled-disinformation-and-threat-campaign-designed#:~:text=In%20September%20and,1%E2%80%99s%20voters.).
  Yet when China acquired data on 220 million Americans, these same
  officials stayed silent and covered it up — an indefensible double
  standard.

**CLAIM: “There is no evidence they were able to change the outcome of
votes.”**

- **FACT:** This is a deliberate attempt to change the subject. In his
  remarks, President Trump did not make a claim about past elections; he
  exposed dangerous, ongoing vulnerabilities in America’s election
  systems that foreign adversaries have both the intent and capability
  to exploit, and revealed that senior intelligence officials
  deliberately hid this intelligence from the President and the American
  people.

**CLAIM: “The number of non-citizens on voter rolls is insignificant.”**

- **FACT:** A Department of Homeland Security review
  [identified](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/2078136512978305046?s=20)
  approximately 278,000 non-citizens registered to vote in federal
  elections across just four states that cooperated with the
  Department’s request. Because many Democrat-led states refused to
  provide their voter files, the actual number is significantly higher.

**CLAIM: “There is no evidence of fraud or irregularities in our
elections.”**

- **FACT:** The declassified documents detail how clear evidence of
  fraud was deliberately buried. In [one
  case](https://www.whitehouse.gov/election-integrity/#:~:text=PILLAR-,Michigan%20Voter%2DRegistration%20Investigation,-Even%20when%20significant),
  an investigation
  [discovered](https://thefederalist.com/2026/07/17/trumps-declassified-docs-detail-massive-michigan-voter-registration-fraud/)
  Democrat-aligned canvassers in Michigan forged voter registrations,
  submitted applications for nonexistent people, and received gift cards
  based on the volume of fraudulent registrations they produced in the
  weeks leading up to the 2020 election. Despite the evidence, the Biden
  Justice Department slow-walked the investigation for years.

**CLAIM: “Mail-in voting is safe and secure.”**

- **FACT:** Multiple independent analyses have
  [warned](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2026/03/the-save-america-act-is-the-most-popular-election-reform-in-decades/#:~:text=FACT%3A%20Voting%20by%20mail%20increases%20the%20risk%20of%20fraud.)
  for years about the heightened risks of mail-in ballots. The
  bipartisan Commission on Federal Election Reform
  [concluded](https://web.archive.org/web/20200419202148/https:/legislationline.org/download/id/1472/file/3b50795b2d0374cbef5c29766256.pdf)
  that absentee ballots are “the largest source of potential voter
  fraud.” The MIT Election Data + Science Lab has
  [noted](https://electionlab.mit.edu/research/voting-mail-and-absentee-voting#:~:text=However%2C%20even%20many%20scholars%20who%20argue%20that%20fraud%20is%20generally%20rare%20agree%20that%20fraud%20with%20VBM%20voting%20seems%20to%20be%20more%20frequent%20than%20with%20in%2Dperson%20voting.)
  that fraud appears more frequent with mail voting than in-person
  voting. A Heartland Institute/Rasmussen poll
  [found](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/heartland-rasmussen-poll-one-five-161100197.html?guccounter=1&guce_referrer=aHR0cHM6Ly93d3cuZ29vZ2xlLmNvbS8&guce_referrer_sig=AQAAAJbZs8elb6GMCLjC15uMUtqR1b-C5xruLe6z7YvGydqnR6xl9DJzGPGDO4Qso2co4Za_NNjKOB0GJeMtn70xSq_qAdhDhebuwjlRgJsVNvv-uly9jHsk6rGV1Tl9UCm-oprRCZZNWDZ0Wiqa7MWKfSF7uxfAdzKKNoc6qb6Y0Bjo)
  that 21% of mail-in voters admitted to at least one form of fraud in
  the 2020 election. The Heritage Foundation has
  [documented](https://www.heritage.org/election-integrity/report/four-stolen-elections-the-vulnerabilities-absentee-and-mail-ballots#:~:text=All%2050%20states,mail%2Din%20voting.)
  widespread vulnerabilities to theft, forgery, and coercion.
