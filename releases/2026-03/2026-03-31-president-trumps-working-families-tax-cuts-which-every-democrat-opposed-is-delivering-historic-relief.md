### **New data shows the law is delivering record-breaking benefits:**

-   The average tax refund is up significantly this year ([nearly
    11%](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/27/average-irs-tax-refund.html)),
    with average refunds exceeding $3,700 — while millions more
    Americans will see an immediate boost in their paychecks as they
    adjust withholding for the lower tax rates made permanent by the
    legislation.
-   Nearly 20 million taxpayers have
    [claimed](https://www.politico.com/news/2026/03/31/trump-tax-break-overtime-democrats-00848495?utm_content=politico/magazine/Politics&utm_source=flipboard)
    the No Tax on Overtime provision, giving these essential workers —
    from factory employees and linemen to police officers and nurses — a
    powerful new break on their hard-earned extra hours.
-   Over 4.6 million taxpayers have
    [benefited](https://home.treasury.gov/news/press-releases/sb0427#:~:text=More%20than%204.6%20million%20taxpayers%20have%20claimed%20the%20No%20Tax%20on%20Tips%20deduction)
    from No Tax on Tips, providing meaningful relief to service workers
    who rely on gratuities.
-   Nearly
    [half](https://home.treasury.gov/news/press-releases/sb0427#:~:text=In%20fact%2C%20nearly%20half%20of%20all%20filers%20so%20far%20have%20benefited%20from%20at%20least%20one%20of%20the%20President%E2%80%99s%20signature%20provisions%20of%20the%20Working%20Families%20Tax%20Cuts.)
    of all filers so far have claimed at least one of the law’s landmark
    new tax cuts.
-   Nearly 12 million small business owners are
    [seeing](https://home.treasury.gov/news/press-releases/sb0427#:~:text=For%20small%20businesses%2C%20the%20impact%20has%20been%20especially%20meaningful.%20On%20average%2C%20the%20law%20is%20reducing%20taxes%20for%20roughly%2012%20million%20small%20business%20owners%20by%20nearly%20%247%2C000.)
    an average tax reduction of nearly $7,000, while the permanent
    extension of the 20% Qualified Business Income deduction is
    [delivering](https://home.treasury.gov/news/press-releases/sb0427#:~:text=The%20permanent%20extension%20of%20the%2020%20percent%20Small%20Business%20Deduction%20alone%20is%20delivering%20about%20%244%2C600%20in%20average%20tax%20relief%20to%208%20million%20entrepreneurs%20around%20the%20country.)
    $4,600 in average relief to eight million entrepreneurs.
-   Restoration of immediate deductibility for research and development
    expenses — applied retroactively — is [freeing
    up](https://home.treasury.gov/news/press-releases/sb0427#:~:text=We%20have%20also%20taken%20important%20steps%20to%20support%20innovation%20and%20investment.%20Immediate%20deductibility%20for%20R%26D%20expenses%20has%20been%20restored%2C%20and%20applied%20retroactively%2C%20unlocking%20an%20estimated%20%24100%20billion%20in%20prior%2Dyear%20deductions%20for%20tens%20of%20thousands%20of%20small%20businesses.%C2%A0)
    $100 billion in prior-year deductions for tens of thousands of
    businesses, while full expensing allows companies to write off
    investments immediately, improving cash flow and fueling expansion.
-   Parents of [more than four
    million](https://www.washingtonexaminer.com/news/white-house/4505768/parents-4-million-children-claimed-trump-account/)
    children have already claimed [Trump
    Accounts](https://trumpaccounts.gov/) on their returns.
