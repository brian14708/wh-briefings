title: Trump Administration Delivers Another Crushing Blow to Antifa Terrorist Network
---
 
**Last year, President Donald J. Trump**
[**designated**](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/09/designating-antifa-as-a-domestic-terrorist-organization/)
**Antifa a domestic terrorist organization and**
[**directed**](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/09/countering-domestic-terrorism-and-organized-political-violence/)
**the full power of the federal government to hunt down, disrupt, and
dismantle the**
[**violent**](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2025/09/president-trump-isnt-backing-down-from-crushing-radical-left-violence/)
**anarchist network.** This week, the Department of Justice struck
another decisive blow by
[charging](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/2066939193176060098) 15
Antifa-linked terrorists in a conspiracy to violently obstruct lawful
immigration enforcement operations in Minneapolis.

**These charges are the latest in the Trump Administration’s relentless
campaign to eradicate Antifa’s domestic terrorism threat:**

- **Minnesota:** Yesterday, 15 Antifa operatives were
  [charged](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/2066939193176060098)
  with [coordinating violent
  attacks](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/2066941531919912997?s=20)
  on ICE agents and facilities during Operation Metro Surge in
  Minneapolis, where they deployed blockades, vehicle tracking,
  intimidation, and other militant tactics to sabotage the operation. In
  February, a self-described Antifa member was
  [charged](https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/anti-ice-antifa-member-arrested-federal-charges-cyberstalking-and-threatening-communications)
  with cyberstalking and threatening ICE officers.
- **Oregon:** Since mid-2025, dozens of [Antifa
  militants](https://thepostmillennial.com/exclusive-inside-of-portland-antifas-ice-facility-occupation-individuals-seen-delivering-supplies-riot-gear)
  have been
  [arrested](https://www.oregonlive.com/crime/2025/09/arrests-outside-portland-ice-building-have-dropped-throughout-summer.html) [and](https://www.justice.gov/usao-or/pr/four-defendants-charged-assaulting-federal-law-enforcement-officers-other-offenses)
  [charged](https://www.justice.gov/usao-or/pr/six-defendants-charge-assaulting-federal-offices-other-offenses-during-protests-near)
  — with multiple
  [convictions](https://abcnews.com/US/wireStory/anti-ice-protester-portland-sentenced-30-months-prison-133802888)
  already secured — following their prolonged
  [siege](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2025/09/president-trump-deploys-federal-resources-to-crush-violent-radical-left-terrorism-in-portland/)
  against the Portland ICE facility. Rioters repeatedly assaulted
  officers, lobbed incendiary devices, and engaged in sustained attacks
  on federal personnel and property.
- **Texas:** In March, nine members of a North Texas Antifa cell were
  [convicted](https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/antifa-cell-members-convicted-prairieland-ice-detention-center-shooting)
  on terrorism-related charges — in addition to seven others who pleaded
  guilty — for their violent, armed
  [ambush](https://www.fox4news.com/news/texas-ice-detention-center-attack-alvarado)
  on an ICE detention facility.
- **Washington:** In May, multiple Antifa insurgents were
  [convicted](https://www.justice.gov/usao-edwa/pr/eastern-washington-jury-convicts-three-defendants-conspiring-impede-federal-officers)
  on federal conspiracy charges for their roles in a violent attack at a
  Spokane ICE facility.
- **New Jersey:** In May, an Antifa thugs
  [violently](https://www.breitbart.com/immigration/2026/06/01/new-jersey-police-arrest-antifas-rioters-at-ices-delaney-hall/)
  [laid](https://nypost.com/2026/05/25/us-news/nj-gov-mikie-sherrill-slammed-for-stunt-at-newark-ice-facility-amid-tense-protests/)
  [siege](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/06/04/nyregion/delaney-hall-protests-charges.html)
  to a Newark ICE detention center, resulting in dozens of
  [arrests](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/06/04/nyregion/delaney-hall-protests-charges.html)
  and federal
  [charges](https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/new-jersey-rioter-delaney-hall-charged-kicking-and-biting-ice-officers).
- **California:** In December, Antifa extremists were
  [charged](https://www.justice.gov/usao-cdca/pr/grand-jury-charges-four-members-anti-government-group-terrorism-felonies-stemming-new)
  in a coordinated bombing plot targeting multiple businesses, ICE
  agents, vehicles, and other sites across Southern California.
- **Indiana:** An
  [Antifa-linked](https://www.washingtonexaminer.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/Thrams-complaint.pdf)
  terrorist was
  [sentenced](https://www.justice.gov/usao-ndin/pr/milford-man-sentenced-time-served)
  for threats to bomb government buildings and assassinate prominent
  politicians.

**The Trump Administration has made it clear: Antifa terrorists and
their networks will be investigated, disrupted, prosecuted, and
neutralized with the full force of federal law.** There will be no safe
harbor for those who attack law enforcement, obstruct lawful immigration
enforcement, or wage campaigns of political violence against the
American people.

**President Trump’s leadership has already produced results — and this
is only the beginning.**
