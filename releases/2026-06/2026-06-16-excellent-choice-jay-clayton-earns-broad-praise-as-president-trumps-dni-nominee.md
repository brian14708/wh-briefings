**President Donald J. Trump’s**
[**nomination**](https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/116732777898985789)
**of Jay Clayton as Director of National Intelligence is drawing strong
bipartisan acclaim across Capitol Hill and beyond.** A former U.S.
Attorney for the Southern District of New York — where he aggressively
prosecuted terrorists, cartels, and major national security threats —
and former Chair of the Securities and Exchange Commission, Mr. Clayton
brings unmatched experience, sharp judgment, and proven leadership in
both the public and private sectors, making him exactly the right leader
to strengthen America’s intelligence community.
