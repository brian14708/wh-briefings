title: "President Trump\u2019s America First Agenda Scores Major Supreme Court Win\
  \ on TPS Termination"
---
 
**The Supreme Court has delivered a major victory for American
sovereignty, ruling that the Trump Administration has full authority to
terminate Temporary Protected Status (TPS) for Haitian migrants.** The
Biden Administration weaponized TPS as a backdoor amnesty to flood the
country with millions of unvetted migrants — and that loophole is
finally closed.

This is the latest decisive win in President Donald J. Trump’s
relentless effort to end the border invasion and put American citizens
first.

**Here are 60 key actions the Trump Administration has taken as part of
its historic America First border agenda:**

1.  The Supreme Court
    [affirmed](https://abcnews.com/Politics/supreme-court-makes-easier-border-agents-deport-green/story?id=134141121)
    the Trump Administration’s authority to remove green card holders
    who threaten American communities.
2.  The Supreme Court
    [upheld](https://www.scotusblog.com/2026/06/justices-side-with-trump-administration-in-border-dispute-over-asylum-seekers/)
    the Trump Administration’s policy to swiftly turn back illegal
    aliens seeking asylum before they set foot on U.S. soil.
3.  The Supreme Court
    [affirmed](https://www.dhs.gov/news/2026/06/25/dhs-issues-statement-following-multiple-supreme-court-wins#:~:text=In%C2%A0Mullin%20v.%20Doe,are%20due%20to%20racial%20discrimination)
    the Trump Administration’s full discretion over Temporary Protected
    Status designations and terminations.
4.  The Supreme Court
    [allowed](https://www.scotusblog.com/2025/06/supreme-court-pauses-district-court-order-preventing-immigrants-from-being-deported-to-third-party-countries/)
    the Trump Administration’s Safe Third Country deportation program to
    continue.
5.  A federal appeals court
    [affirmed](https://www.npr.org/2026/06/24/nx-s1-5869125/court-allows-trump-speedy-deportations)
    the Trump Administration’s expedited removal policy, accelerating
    deportations of illegal aliens.
6.  The Trump Administration
    [reformed](https://www.uscis.gov/newsroom/news-releases/uscis-unveils-first-changes-to-naturalization-test-in-multi-step-overhaul-of-american-citizenship)
    the citizenship test to better assess aliens’ understandings of
    American history, government, and values, and better ensure aliens
    meet all eligibility requirements.
7.  The Trump Administration dramatically
    [expanded](https://www.dhs.gov/news/2026/01/03/ice-announces-historic-120-manpower-increase-thanks-recruitment-campaign-brought)
    the immigration enforcement workforce, including more than
    [doubling](https://www.dhs.gov/news/2026/01/03/ice-announces-historic-120-manpower-increase-thanks-recruitment-campaign-brought)
    the number of ICE agents.
8.  The Trump Administration
    [restored](https://www.uscis.gov/newsroom/news-releases/dhs-strengthens-integrity-in-nations-immigration-system-returns-to-commonsense-legal-immigration#:~:text=We%20restored%20the%20practice%20of%20conducting%20neighborhood%20investigations%20of%20potential%20new%20citizens%20to%20verify%20their%20claims%20and%20ensure%20we%20get%20a%20full%20picture%20of%20their%20character.)
    the practice of conducting neighborhood investigations of potential
    new citizens to verify their claims and ensure we get a full picture
    of their character.
9.  The Trump Administration dramatically
    [accelerated](https://www.npr.org/2026/05/26/nx-s1-5830474/trump-immigration-courts-mega-masters)
    immigration court hearings.
10. The Trump Administration
    [restricted](https://news.bloomberglaw.com/daily-labor-report/dhs-advances-rule-nixing-automatic-renewal-of-work-permits)
    access to immigrant work permits, prioritizing U.S. workers and
    tightening security.

    [moved](https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2026/06/23/2026-12542/naturalization-application-fee-adjustments)
    to raise naturalization fees so American taxpayers are no longer
    subsidizing the process.
12. The Trump Administration
    [moved](https://home.treasury.gov/news/press-releases/sb0523) to
    secure our financial system against illegal aliens.
13. The Trump Administration
    [ended](https://www.hud.gov/news/hud-no-25-048) government-backed
    mortgage financing for illegal aliens and H-1B visa holders.

    [suspended](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/realigning-the-united-states-refugee-admissions-program/)

    [reduced](https://www.npr.org/2025/10/30/g-s1-95823/trump-administration-sets-lowest-ever-cap-on-refugee-admissions)

    [banned](https://www.sba.gov/article/2026/03/09/sba-bans-foreign-nationals-accessing-sba-backed-loans)
