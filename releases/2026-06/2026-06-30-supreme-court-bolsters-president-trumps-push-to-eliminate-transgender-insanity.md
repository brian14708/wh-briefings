  \ Insanity"

Today, the Supreme Court affirmed President Donald J. Trump’s
longstanding commitment to protect women and girls, ruling that states
may reserve female athletic competition for biological females.

The Court’s decision is a landmark victory for common sense, biological
reality, and for the millions of women and girls who deserve a level
playing field. By upholding laws protecting female athletic competition,
the Court confirmed that states may preserve the fairness, safety, and
equal opportunities that Title IX was enacted to guarantee.

This ruling is the latest affirmation of President Trump’s efforts to
restore biological reality throughout the federal government and protect
the rights of women and girls. Since returning to office, President
Trump has taken a series of decisive actions to advance those goals,
including:

- [Barring](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/02/keeping-men-out-of-womens-sports/)
  men from competing in women’s sports, resulting in athletic
  organizations such as the NCAA, International Olympic Committee, and
  World Athletics Council realigning their policies with biological
  reality.
- [Cracking](https://www.ed.gov/about/news/press-release/us-department-of-educations-office-civil-rights-initiates-18-title-ix-investigations)
  down on athletic organizations that refuse to comply with President
  Trump’s commonsense Executive Order by launching Title IX
  investigations across the country,
  [including](https://www.ed.gov/about/news/press-release/us-department-of-education-opens-investigations-maryland-state-department-of-education-and-three-maryland-school-districts-alleged-title-ix)
  four in the past week alone.
- [Restoring](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/defending-women-from-gender-ideology-extremism-and-restoring-biological-truth-to-the-federal-government/)
  biological reality as the official policy of the federal government by
  recognizing that there are only two immutable sexes: male and female.
- [Restoring](https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/passports/passport-help/sex-marker.html)
  biological sex as the sole markers for official federal documents,
  including passports.
- [Banning](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/protecting-children-from-chemical-and-surgical-mutilation/)
  federal funding, sponsorship, and promotion of child gender
  mutilation, resulting in more than 40 hospitals and health systems
  across the country
  [stopping](https://www.statnews.com/2026/02/05/hospitals-stop-gender-care-minors-trump-administration-pressure/)
  or suspending child mutilation programs.
- [Ordering](https://www.hhs.gov/press-room/gender-dysphoria-report-release.html)
  the Department of Health and Human Services to perform a comprehensive
  review of best practices for children and adolescents, ultimately
  confirming the complete absence of scientific evidence supporting
  child gender mutilation.
- [Terminating](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/ending-radical-indoctrination-in-k-12-schooling/)
  federal support for “gender ideology” and “equity” curricula, forcing
  states to halt the indoctrination of children with woke ideology or
  risk losing federal funding.
- [Cutting ](https://www.hhs.gov/press-room/hhs-acf-states-remove-gender-ideology-sex-ed.html)billions
  of dollars in federal funding for the proliferation of radical gender
  ideology.
- [Prioritizing](https://www.war.gov/News/News-Stories/Article/article/4086111/dod-gender-dysphoria-incompatible-with-military-service-members-must-serve-in-a/)
  military readiness over radical gender ideology by requiring troops to
  serve according to their biological sex, and barring so-called
  transgender individuals from military service.
- [Ending](https://www.war.gov/News/News-Stories/Article/article/4086111/dod-gender-dysphoria-incompatible-with-military-service-members-must-serve-in-a/)
  all funding for sex change surgeries at the Department of War and
  [Department of Veterans
  Affairs](https://news.va.gov/press-room/va-to-phase-out-treatment-for-gender-dysphoria/).

<!-- -->

- [Eliminating](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/restoring-americas-fighting-force/) radical
  gender ideology from U.S. service academies and training programs.
- [Protecting](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/defending-women-from-gender-ideology-extremism-and-restoring-biological-truth-to-the-federal-government/)
  women’s spaces, including bathrooms, locker rooms, and shelters.

President Trump and his entire Administration will continue safeguarding
women’s sports, shielding children from radical gender ideology, and
restoring biological truth throughout the federal government, just as he
promised on the campaign trail.
