title: This Easter, President Trump Reaffirms America as a Beacon for Christian Liberty
---
 
**This Easter Sunday, as Christians around the world rejoice in the
glorious resurrection of Jesus Christ, President Donald J. Trump stands
as a fierce defender of the Christian faith.** From his first day back
in office, President Trump has made protecting people of faith a
cornerstone of his Administration. Under his leadership, the U.S. is
once again a beacon of religious liberty — honoring the biblical values
and heritage that built our nation.

### **President Trump is protecting Christians at home:**

-   President
    Trump [established](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/02/establishment-of-the-white-house-faith-office/) the historic White
    House Faith Office — the first-ever office in the West Wing
    dedicated exclusively to advancing faith.
-   President Trump
    [established](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/02/eradicating-anti-christian-bias/)
    a new Task Force to Eradicate Anti-Christian Bias to [investigate
    and
    dismantle](https://static.foxnews.com/foxnews.com/content/uploads/2025/09/anti-christian-bias-report.pdf)
    the systematic discrimination against Christians that occurred under
    the previous administration.
-   President
    Trump [pardoned](https://www.whitehouse.gov/fact-sheets/2025/02/fact-sheet-president-donald-j-trump-eradicates-anti-christian-bias/) Christians
    and pro-life activists who were unjustly
    [persecuted](https://www.foxnews.com/politics/biden-doj-weaponized-face-act-imprison-pro-life-activists-attorney-tells-house-systematic-campaign)
    for praying and peacefully living out their faith.
-   President
    Trump [declared](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/defending-women-from-gender-ideology-extremism-and-restoring-biological-truth-to-the-federal-government/) it
    the official policy of the U.S. Government that there are only two
    immutable sexes — male and female.
-   President
    Trump [banned](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/protecting-children-from-chemical-and-surgical-mutilation/) federal
    funding for the chemical and surgical mutilation of minors,
    protecting children from irreversible harm.
-   The Department of Justice
    [swiftly](https://x.com/agpambondi/status/2027449934337696099?s=46)
    [arrested](https://www.kare11.com/article/news/local/ice-in-minnesota/several-more-arrests-in-connection-with-st-paul-church-protest-against-ice/89-e824b7cb-11a5-4e39-b87f-727923fa7aa2)
    dozens of Radical Left rioters who attacked a worship service at a
    Minnesota church — sending a clear message that violence against
    places of worship will not be tolerated.
-   President Trump
    [signed](https://www.whitehouse.gov/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/WH-OBBB-Empowers-School-Choice-and-Ends-the-Biden-Student-Loan-Bailout.pdf)
    the first-ever national school choice program, empowering parents to
    choose faith-based schools for their children and ending the
    monopoly of government-run education.
-   The Trump Administration
    [ended](https://grants.nih.gov/grants/guide/notice-files/NOT-OD-26-028.html)
    the barbaric use of fetal tissue from aborted babies in federally
    funded research at the National Institutes of Health.
-   The Trump Administration
    [eliminated](https://www.hhs.gov/press-room/fact-sheet-announcements-on-conscience-and-life.html)
    Biden-era mandates forcing insurance companies to cover abortions.
-   The Trump Administration
    [strengthened](https://www.hhs.gov/press-room/conscience-dlc.html#:~:text=OCR%20facilitates%20and%20coordinates%20the,or%20provide%20referrals%20for%20abortion.)
    conscience protections for healthcare providers who object to
    abortion, sterilization, or assisted suicide, and
    [launched](https://www.hhs.gov/press-room/hhs-launches-whistleblower-form-to-protect-kids.html) [multiple](https://www.hhs.gov/press-room/hhs-protects-workers-conscience-rights.html)
    investigations into facilities that violated those rights.
-   President Trump
    [established](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/05/establishment-of-the-religious-liberty-commission/)
    the Religious Liberty Commission to
    [safeguard](https://www.whitehouse.gov/fact-sheets/2025/05/fact-sheet-president-donald-j-trump-establishes-the-religious-liberty-commission/)
    religious freedom for all faiths, with special attention to
    protecting Christianity from government overreach.
-   President
    Trump [ordered](https://www.whitehouse.gov/fact-sheets/2025/04/fact-sheet-president-donald-j-trump-directs-repeal-of-regulations-that-are-unlawful-under-10-recent-supreme-court-decisions/) the
    repeal of all regulations conflicting with Supreme Court precedents
    on religious liberty.
-   The Trump Administration directed federal agencies to
    [support](https://www.opm.gov/policy-data-oversight/latest-memos/reasonable-accommodations-for-religious-purposes/) and [protect](https://www.opm.gov/policy-data-oversight/latest-memos/protecting-religious-expression-in-the-federal-workplace/)
    religious expression in the workplace.
-   The Department of Veterans Affairs
    [rescinded](https://firstliberty.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/Trubey-VA-Letter-4.2.25.pdf)
    speech codes that censored military chaplains’ sermons.
-   The Small Business
    Administration [eliminated](https://www.sba.gov/article/2025/07/14/sba-launches-center-faith-eliminates-biden-ban-disaster-relief-faith-organizations) a
    Biden-era ban on disaster relief for faith-based organizations.
-   President
    Trump [placed](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/02/establishment-of-the-white-house-faith-office/) Faith
    Directors or Faith Liaisons in every federal department and agency.
-   President
    Trump [reformed](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/04/reforming-accreditation-to-strengthen-higher-education/) the
    accreditation process, which was repeatedly weaponized against
    faith-based institutions.
-   President
    Trump [issued](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/08/guaranteeing-fair-banking-for-all-americans/) an
    executive order combating the debanking of Americans based on their
    political and religious beliefs, which had been targeted at
    conservatives and people of faith.
-   President Trump’s Working Families Tax Cuts Act
    [increased](https://waysandmeans.house.gov/2026/03/17/chairman-smith-working-families-tax-cuts-lowers-the-high-cost-of-raising-a-family/#:~:text=The%20Working%20Families%20Tax%20Cuts%20permanently,better%20afford%20the%20cost%20of%20living.)
    the Child Tax Credit,
    [expanded](https://tax.thomsonreuters.com/news/understanding-obbb-enhancements-to-employer-leave-and-childcare-credits/#:~:text=The%20OBBB%20also%20boosted%20the%20employer%2Dprovided%20childcare%20credit%20under%C2%A0IRC%20%C2%A7%2045F.%20For%20tax%20years%20after%202025)
    employer childcare tax credits,
    [made](https://www.dol.gov/sites/dolgov/files/WB/WB_FactSheet_PaidFamilyLeave.pdf)
    the paid family leave tax credit permanent, and established Trump
    Accounts for the next generation of Americans.
-   The Department of
    Justice [restricted](https://www.justice.gov/media/1386461/dl?inline) the
    misuse of the FACE Act against pro-life advocates,
    [dismissing](https://www.justice.gov/media/1386461/dl?inline) several
    cases aimed at persecuting pro-life Americans.
-   President
    Trump [mandated](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/enforcing-the-hyde-amendment/) enforcement
    of the Hyde Amendment to end the use of federal taxpayer dollars to
    fund or promote abortion.
-   President
    Trump [reinforced](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/05/national-day-of-prayer-2025/) America’s
    “longstanding legacy of prayer, faith, and trust in God.” 

### **President Trump is protecting Christians abroad:**

-   President Trump [reinstated and
    expanded](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/memorandum-for-the-secretary-of-state-the-secretary-of-defense-the-secretary-of-health-and-human-services-the-administrator-of-the-united-states-for-international-development/) the
    Mexico City Policy, ensuring American taxpayer dollars no longer
    subsidize abortion, coercive sterilization, or radical gender
    ideology overseas.
-   President
    Trump [withdrew](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/withdrawing-the-united-states-from-the-worldhealth-organization/) the
    U.S. from the pro-abortion, anti-faith, anti-freedom World Health
    Organization.
-   The State
    Department [renewed](https://www.state.gov/united-states-renewed-membership-in-the-geneva-consensus-declaration-on-promoting-womens-health-and-strengthening-the-family/) its
    membership in the Geneva Consensus Declaration, which champions
    women’s health, strengthens the natural family, and rejects any
    international right to abortion.
-   The Trump Administration
    [launched](https://nypost.com/2026/04/01/us-news/trump-admins-probe-into-noelia-castillo-euthanasia-puts-spain-on-the-defense/)
    a probe into the Spanish government’s euthanasia of 25-year-old
    Noelia Castillo.
-   President Trump
    [ordered](https://x.com/DeptofWar/status/2004351717131903272)
    targeted strikes against jihadist Islamic State terrorists in
    Nigeria who have terrorized Christian communities for years.
-   The Trump Administration
    [delivered](https://www.state.gov/releases/office-of-the-spokesperson/2025/10/trump-administration-provides-life-saving-humanitarian-assistance-to-druze-christian-and-bedouin-communities-in-southern-syria/)
    critical humanitarian assistance to persecuted Christian communities
    in Southern Syria and beyond, standing firmly with believers under
    threat.
-   In response to anti-Christian violence around the globe, the
    Department of State
    [restricted](https://www.state.gov/releases/office-of-the-spokesperson/2025/12/combating-egregious-anti-christian-violence-in-nigeria-and-globally/)
    visas for individuals involved in violations of religious freedom.

**On this sacred Easter Sunday, we are reminded that faith is not a
private matter to be silenced by government — but a foundational
strength of our Republic.** Through bold leadership and unwavering
commitment, the Trump Administration is restoring religious liberty,
protecting the innocent, and ensuring that America will always remain
one nation under God.
