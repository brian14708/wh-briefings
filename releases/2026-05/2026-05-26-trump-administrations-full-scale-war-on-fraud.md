**President Donald J. Trump and Vice President JD Vance are unleashing
an unrelenting, full-scale assault on the fraudsters, scammers, and
corrupt operators who have looted billions from American taxpayers.**
The [White House Task Force to Eliminate Fraud](https://x.com/WHFraudTF)
is moving at unprecedented speed and ferocity to root out the waste,
abuse, and criminal exploitation of government programs that have
drained billions from hardworking taxpayers.

This is a direct offensive against every fraudulent scheme preying on
hardworking Americans — and the results are already staggering.

**Here are some of the Task Force’s key actions and victories to date:**

- **February 25, 2026:** The Trump Administration
  [halted](https://www.wsj.com/politics/policy/vance-announces-pause-of-nearly-260-million-in-minnesota-medicaid-funding-c9e25042?gaa_at=eafs&gaa_n=AWEtsqfraULQWGNCbsbDpG5peexvTMcytJUji92cKnMvwjroL11b58dSNO5U&gaa_ts=69a04a99&gaa_sig=OapzphsrBr9w8fmaDq7F6srOcMYrVrGthiATYZwGVR1IDqHJkeR0j2meNzX0glgXYlLDiCuYBhO4zymPUlVu1A%3D%3D)
  nearly $260 million in Medicaid payments to Minnesota over rampant
  fraud allegations, demanding full cooperation with federal
  investigators.
- **March 19, 2026:** Federal prosecutors
  [charged](https://www.justice.gov/usao-cdca/pr/2-foreign-nationals-among-11-arrested-charges-they-used-stolen-identities-fraudulently)
  11 individuals in a major real estate and loan fraud ring preying on
  elderly Americans in California.
- **March 25, 2026:** The Trump Administration
  [suspended](https://www.foxnews.com/politics/jd-vances-anti-fraud-task-force-ramps-up-identifying-fraud-across-us-after-suspending-70-providers-la)
  dozens of high-risk hospice and home health providers in the Los
  Angeles area.
- **March 30, 2026:** The Trump Administration
  [launched](https://home.treasury.gov/news/press-releases/sb0426) a new
  national fraud whistleblower program to empower Americans to expose
  waste and abuse.
- **April 2, 2026:** The Trump Administration
  [suspended](https://www.foxnews.com/politics/vance-anti-fraud-task-force-suspends-221-california-hospice-healthcare-providers-so-far)
  hundreds additional high-risk hospice and home health providers across
  California.
- **April 3, 2026:** Federal prosecutors
  [charged](https://x.com/USAttyEssayli/status/2039846991958872458) more
  than a dozen individuals in a $50 million hospice fraud scheme.
- **April 7, 2026:** The Department of Justice
  [secured](https://www.justice.gov/usao-cdca/pr/orange-county-man-pleads-guilty-orchestrating-fraud-scheme-submitted-nearly-270?bm-verify=AAQAAAAN_____9npR0xrBVRsPaPPLFXMCJBBTfQTTxZjmlJfnoWUQkk3qROnUN3WYF6N6KM6IkGRK0uAG-dkKehdLNyAo0adam7jw8WDx8gbbwcqfKePHmgeYQxoWKN-uAsGPCNZVHQDyvAkvixy-FDsYu_5Yry6DJMHULIMpVrRWgP2y0qOREHhuRlc_aUjs23MrCfdYxcskAk0_ZFI7wUgwItboUizKvJvxIZQSdyClzsymKQmNWCb2LlSPZ9oup9jCw8s4ZSwC2dWrIUnxDxHDelvYnov-C77a5CMXMRx_PscKV9g76eEBvZc7HlUw9GlJcNs8w0Pv5_z4v-GcDPNkKAPvCYvIJ5Z96kQuInwPlC75ywWMdzdxqVe4PREtzGGH3ph)
  a guilty plea from a California fraudster accused of submitting $270
  million in false reimbursement claims.
- **April 8, 2026:** The Department of Justice
  [confirmed](https://nypost.com/2026/04/08/us-news/acting-attorney-general-todd-blanche-announces-crackdown-on-fraudsters-with-1t-in-taxpayer-programs-on-the-line/)
  it has 8,000 active, ongoing fraud cases.
- **April 8, 2026:** The Task Force
  [uncovered](https://www.foxnews.com/politics/jd-vances-task-force-flags-nearly-63b-government-contracts-going-potentially-fraudulent-businesses)
  $6.3 billion in suspected fraudulent government contracts and
  immediately launched a sweeping investigation.
- **April 15, 2026:** The Trump Administration
  [suspended](https://www.foxnews.com/politics/vance-anti-fraud-task-force-suspends-447-hospices-los-angeles-600m-suspected-fraud)
  447 hospices and 23 home health agencies in Los Angeles, with
  estimated fraud exceeding $600 million.
- **April 16, 2026:** The Trump Administration
  [served](https://usdaoig.oversight.gov/articles/news/press-releases/operation-cold-snap-puts-minnesota-snap-traffickers-deep-freeze)
  criminal warrants and administrative charges on 20 Minnesota
  businesses suspected of SNAP fraud.
- **April 17, 2026:** The Department of Justice
  [announced](https://nypost.com/2026/04/17/us-news/dojs-new-fraud-division-thwarted-340-million-in-schemes-in-first-week/)
  its newly established National Fraud Enforcement Division took
  enforcement action in schemes totaling over $340 million in its first
  week alone.
- **April 24, 2026:** The Small Business Administration
  [referred](https://x.com/SBA_Kelly/status/2048867984777810075) 562,000
  fraudulent or delinquent pandemic-era loans — totaling $22 billion —
  for aggressive collection.
- **April 28, 2026:** The Department of Justice
  [conducted](https://www.foxnews.com/politics/fbi-raids-minneapolis-childcare-facilities-part-sweeping-fraud-investigation)
  targeted enforcement operations at nearly two dozen Minnesota
  childcare centers suspected of systemic fraud.
- **April 30, 2026:** The Department of Justice
  [launched](https://x.com/DOJFraudDiv/status/2049911398436585805) a
  West Coast Strike Force team targeting healthcare fraud across
  Arizona, Nevada, and northern California.
- **April 30, 2026:** The Trump Administration
  [deferred](https://x.com/DrOzCMS/status/2049904255025418385) an
  additional $91 million in federal Medicaid funds from non-cooperating
  Minnesota.
- **May 12, 2026:** The Trump Administration
  [identified](https://www.politico.com/news/2026/05/12/ice-optional-practical-training-fraud-00916797)
  over 10,000 suspected fraud cases in immigration student work
  programs.
- **May 13, 2026:** The Trump Administration
  [suspended](https://www.foxnews.com/politics/vance-led-task-force-cuts-home-health-hospice-providers-suspected-fraud)
  $1.4 billion in home health and hospice funding nationwide.
- **May 13, 2026:** The Trump Administration
  [deferred](https://nypost.com/2026/05/13/us-news/feds-defer-1-3b-in-california-medicaid-funding-amid-healthcare-fraud-crackdown/)
  $1.3 billion in federal Medicaid reimbursements for California.
- **May 13, 2026:** The Trump Administration
  [halted](https://www.reuters.com/legal/litigation/us-halting-medicare-enrollments-new-home-healthcare-hospice-providers-2026-05-13/)
  all new Medicare enrollments for hospice providers nationwide until
  the fraud crisis is brought under control.
- **May 13, 2026:** The Trump Administration
  [launched](https://www.wsj.com/politics/policy/trump-jd-vance-medicaid-fraud-40e9e78e?st=2sZnYa)
  audits of Medicaid Fraud Control Units in all 50 states.
- **May 13, 2026:** The Trump Administration
  [blocked](https://dailycaller.com/2026/05/13/donald-trump-education-department-student-loans-60-million-applications-fraud/)
  $60 million in fraudulent student loan applications in just the first
  month since deploying enhanced screening.
- **May 20, 2026:** The Department of Justice
  [charged](https://www.fox9.com/news/minneapolis-day-care-owner-featured-nick-shirley-video-charged)
  a Minneapolis daycare owner featured in Nick Shirley’s viral video.
- **May 21, 2026:** The Department of Justice
  [expanded](https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/minnesota-health-care-fraud-takedown-results-charges-against-15-defendants-over-90m-fraud)
  its Health Care Fraud Strike Force program, adding additional
  prosecutors to combat Medicaid fraud nationwide.
- **May 21, 2026:** The Department of Justice
  [charged](https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/minnesota-health-care-fraud-takedown-results-charges-against-15-defendants-over-90m-fraud)
  15 individuals in a wide-ranging Minnesota healthcare fraud scheme —
  including the highest loss amount ever charged in a Medicaid case in
  the state and the largest autism fraud scheme ever prosecuted.

**This is only the beginning.** The Trump Administration will continue
this relentless effort until every scheme is exposed, every dollar
possible is recovered, and the American people’s trust in their
government is restored.
