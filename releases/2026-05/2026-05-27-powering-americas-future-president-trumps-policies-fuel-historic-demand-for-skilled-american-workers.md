As President Donald J. Trump’s America First agenda fuels a blue-collar
resurgence, American workers are back in demand — and a new generation
is answering the call.

## **The Shift Is Already Underway**

**A seismic shift from years past towards the trades is a clear sign
that President Trump’s relentless push to restore the dignity of
American labor is taking hold.**

- 60% of Gen Z Americans
  [plan](https://www.manufacturingdive.com/news/60-of-gen-zers-will-pursue-skilled-trade-work-this-year-survey/811955/)
  to pursue skilled trade work this year, up from less than 40% just one
  year ago.
- Half of Gen Z college graduates are also pivoting to trades, signaling
  the end of the failed “college-for-all” experiment.
- Median pay in skilled trades now [matches or
  exceeds](https://thebirmgroup.com/why-skilled-trades-are-out-earning-college-graduates-in-2026/#:~:text=The%20Bureau%20of,real%2Dworld%20experience.)
  many four-year degrees, with trade workers
  [enjoying](https://www.washingtonpost.com/business/2026/01/31/labor-market-gap-trade-workers-white-collar/)
  greater employment stability than their white-collar peers for the
  first time in history.
- Meanwhile, 94% of U.S. contractors
  [report](https://thebirmgroup.com/why-skilled-trades-are-out-earning-college-graduates-in-2026/#:~:text=Ninety%2Dfour%20percent%20of%20U.S.%20contractors%20report%20difficulty%20filling%20roles%20across%20all%20skill%20tiers%2C%20which%20drives%20wages%20higher%20and%20increases%20competition%20for%20skilled%20professionals.)
  labor shortages, driving wages higher.

## **The Trump Administration Is Driving the Boom**

**President Trump’s policies have overhauled workforce training and
created clear pathways to high-paying careers without crushing debt.**

- President Trump
  [set](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/04/preparing-americans-for-high-paying-skilled-trade-jobs-of-the-future/)
  a national goal of over one million new registered apprentices and
  reformed broken federal programs.
- The Working Families Tax Cuts Act
  [expanded](https://www.savingforcollege.com/article/big-beautiful-bill-education-savings-529-plans#:~:text=Families%20can%20now%20use%20529%20plans%20for%20credentialing%20programs%20such%20as%20welding%2C%20aviation%20mechanics%2C%20and%20other%20trade%20certifications.%20Covered%20expenses%20include%20tuition%2C%20testing%20fees%2C%20and%20costs%20for%20books%2C%20equipment%2C%20and%20continuing%20education%20required%20to%20obtain%20or%20maintain%20a%20professional%20credential.)
  529 savings plans to trade certifications,
  [broadened](https://upcea.edu/workforce-pell-is-now-law-under-the-one-big-beautiful-bill-obbb/#:~:text=Section%202011%20of,degree%2Dseeking%20students.)
  Pell Grants for skilled trades,
  [delivered](https://www.kbkg.com/feature/obbb-tax-bill-makes-100-bonus-depreciation-permanent-what-you-need-to-know)
  100% bonus depreciation for equipment, and
  [eliminated](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2026/04/president-trump-champions-the-american-worker/#:~:text=No%20Tax%20on%20Overtime%3A%20Over%2025%20million%20Americans%20have%20claimed%20No%20Tax%20on%20Overtime%20so%20far%2C%20with%20an%20average%20deduction%20of%20over%20%243%2C000.)
  taxes on overtime — putting thousands of dollars back in workers’
  pockets.
- The Department of Education
  [launched](https://www.foxbusiness.com/politics/workforce-pell-grants-skilled-trades-jobs)
  the first-ever Workforce Pell Grant program to fast-track Americans
  into high-demand, high-paying careers.
- The Department of Labor has awarded over $229 million in grants to
  expand
  [apprenticeships](https://www.dol.gov/newsroom/releases/eta/eta20250630)
  in [critical
  sectors](https://www.dol.gov/newsroom/releases/eta/eta20260213-0) like
  shipbuilding, defense, and nuclear energy, and
  [launched](https://www.dol.gov/newsroom/releases/eta/eta20260401) a
  landmark initiative to integrate AI skills into registered
  apprenticeships to prepare the workforce for the jobs of the future.
- New guidance
  [bars](https://www.dol.gov/newsroom/releases/osec/osec20250710)
  illegal aliens from taxpayer-funded workforce programs, ensuring
  benefits go to American workers.
- These reforms build on President Trump’s first-term launch of
  [apprenticeship.gov](https://www.apprenticeship.gov/), a government
  site that connects job seekers, employers, and trainers nationwide.

## **A Workforce Ready to Rebuild America**

**With factories and supply chains returning under Trump’s tariff and
reshoring policies, demand for skilled labor is surging.**

- Hundreds of thousands of annual openings
  [projected](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/60-gen-zers-pursue-skilled-114359078.html?guccounter=1#:~:text=There%20will%20be%20openings%20for%20roughly%20150%2C000%20construction%20workers%20and%2080%2C000%20electricians%20each%20year%20for%20the%20next%20several%20years%2C%20according%20to%20U.S.%20Bureau%20of%20Labor%20Statistics%20data.)
  for construction workers and electricians alone — with
  [recent](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2026/05/jobs-report-trump-economy-roars-ahead-with-big-private-sector-job-gains/)
  [jobs](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2026/04/strong-march-jobs-report-signals-accelerating-momentum-under-president-trump/#:~:text=Construction%20is%20surging.%20Another%2026%2C000%20construction%20jobs%20were%20added%20in%20March%2C%20propelled%20by%20strong%20gains%20in%20specialty%20trades%20and%20residential%20building.)
  [data](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2026/02/this-is-the-trump-economy-job-growth-crushes-expectations-as-more-americans-work-for-higher-wages/#:~:text=As%20President%20Trump%E2%80%99s%20agenda%20takes%20effect%2C%20factory%20groundbreakings%20and%20data%20centers%20are%20leading%20to%20more%20construction%20jobs%20%E2%80%94%20with%20construction%20employment%20up%2033%2C000%20in%20January%2C%20including%2025%2C000%20new%20jobs%20in%20the%20nonresidential%20specialty%20trades%20(the%20highest%20monthly%20change%20in%20five%20years).)
  reinforcing the demand.
- Bureau of Labor Statistics
  [forecasts](https://www.wired.com/story/why-there-arent-enough-electricians-and-plumbers-to-build-ai-data-centers/#:~:text=The%20BLS%20projects%20the%20number%20of%20employed%20electricians%20to%20grow%209%20percent%20over%20the%20next%20decade%2C%20%E2%80%9Cmuch%20faster%20than%20the%20average%20for%20all%20occupations.%E2%80%9D)
  9% growth for electricians and more than
  [double](https://www.sacbee.com/news/equity-lab/article311748526.html)
  the national average for HVAC technicians over the next decade.
- As President Trump’s trade policies drive factories and critical
  supply chains back to America, the demand for skilled trades workers
  is [set to
  surge](https://www.forbes.com/sites/jackkelly/2025/04/22/the-booming-job-market-for-skilled-tradespersons/#:~:text=Skilled%20trades%2C%20encompassing,the%20right%20skills.).
- Since President Trump took office, private industry has responded,
  with major commitments from the nation’s most recognizable brands
  — including
  [Google](https://blog.google/company-news/outreach-and-initiatives/google-org/electrical-workers-ai-training/#:~:text=Building%20upon%20years,master%20AI%20skills.),
  [Amazon](https://www.aboutamazon.com/news/workplace/amazon-future-of-work-skills-jobs-training),
  [Apple](https://www.apple.com/newsroom/2025/02/apple-will-spend-more-than-500-billion-usd-in-the-us-over-the-next-four-years/#:~:text=Academy%20in%20Detroit-,To%20help%20companies%20transition%20to%20advanced%20manufacturing%2C%20Apple%20will%20open%20the,free%20programming%20that%20helps%20young%20people%20learn%20vital%20skills%20like%20coding.,-Apple%E2%80%99s%20investment%20in),
  [Meta](https://about.fb.com/news/2026/04/meta-cbre-invest-in-american-jobs-new-fiber-technician-training-program/),
  [Home
  Depot](https://corporate.homedepot.com/news/foundation-and-community/home-depot-foundation-invests-10-million-skilled-trades-training),
  [Lowe’s](https://corporate.lowes.com/newsroom/press-releases/lowes-foundation-plans-expanded-250-million-investment-help-train-and-develop-250000-tradespeople-over-next-decade-increasing-skilled-trades-workforce),
  [BlackRock](https://www.blackrock.com/corporate/newsroom/press-releases/article/corporate-one/press-releases/blackrock-launches-philanthropic-skilled-trades-initiative),
  and [Carrier
  Global](https://www.corporate.carrier.com/news/news-articles/202505_carrier-announces-additional-1-billion-investment-us-manufacturing-footprint-advanced-cuttingedge-rd-workforce-expansion.html#:~:text=The%20program%20aims%20to%20hire,high%2Dperformance%20climate%20solutions%20systems.)
  — to fund new apprenticeship, training, and education programs.

**America is rebuilding — by Americans, for Americans.** Under President
Trump’s leadership, a new generation of skilled workers is rising to
power a new Golden Age of American manufacturing and prosperity.
