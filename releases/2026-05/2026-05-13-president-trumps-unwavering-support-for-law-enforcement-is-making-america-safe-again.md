This [National Police
Week](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2026/05/peace-officers-memorial-day-and-police-week-2026/),
America honors the brave men and women of law enforcement who risk their
lives daily to protect our communities. Under President Donald J. Trump,
these heroes are respected, empowered, and supported — not defunded,
demonized, or betrayed by the Radical Left.

**After four years of Democrats’ war on police, President Trump has
delivered decisive leadership that puts officers back in control — and
the results are undeniable:**

In 2025, the U.S. murder rate
[posted](https://www.axios.com/2025/12/24/us-trump-murder-data-killing-crime-national-guard)
its largest single-year drop on record — plummeting to the [lowest
level](https://www.axios.com/2026/01/22/murder-rate-century-low) in 125
years — with [sharp
declines](https://www.axios.com/2026/02/11/violent-crime-homicides-falls-major-cities-trump) across
all [violent
crimes](https://majorcitieschiefs.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/MCCA-Violent-Crime-Report-2025-and-2024-Year-End.pdf).
Major cities saw
[massive](https://www.axios.com/2026/02/11/violent-crime-homicides-falls-major-cities-trump)
homicide reductions, while rapes,
[robberies](https://majorcitieschiefs.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/MCCA-Violent-Crime-Report-2025-and-2024-January-to-September.pdf),
assaults,
[shootings](https://www.thetrace.org/2025/12/data-shooting-stats-gun-violence-america/),
[officer
deaths](https://apnews.com/article/onduty-officer-deaths-2025-903002e6d28678f69d02123b9e3b05b4)
(an [80-year
low](https://nleomf.org/2025-year-end-officer-fatalities-report/)),
[traffic
fatalities](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2026/01/walz-is-wrong-president-trumps-immigration-policies-are-saving-lives/#:~:text=Traffic%20fatalities%20have-,dropped,-dramatically%2C%20with%20eased),
and
[overdoses](https://www.drugtopics.com/view/opioid-overdose-deaths-fall-by-34-in-2025-ashp-midyear-2025)
all plunged. These historic gains are
[accelerating](https://www.axios.com/2026/05/10/violent-crime-us-cities-trump)
into 2026 — especially in cities like Washington, D.C., and Memphis,
where President Trump deployed federal support to restore order and back
local police.

**These victories are the direct result of the Trump Administration’s
unbreakable commitment to law and order:**

- **Imposing the death penalty for cop killers:** President Trump
  [directed](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/restoring-the-death-penalty-and-protecting-public-safety/)
  the Department of Justice to aggressively seek capital punishment for
  those who murder police officers.
- **Scrapping Democrats’ soft-on-crime mandates:** The Trump
  Administration
  [rescinded](https://www.foxnews.com/us/trump-lifts-police-burden-executive-orders-first-100-days-veteran-officer#:~:text=That%20order%20promoted%20%22alternatives%20to%20arrest%20or%20incarceration%2C%22)
  radical Biden-era orders pushing “alternatives to arrest” for violent
  offenders and restrictions that tied officers’ hands when using lawful
  force to make arrests.
- **Empowering cities to clear the streets:** States and localities now
  have [stronger
  tools](https://www.whitehouse.gov/fact-sheets/2025/07/fact-sheet-president-donald-j-trump-takes-action-to-end-crime-and-disorder-on-americas-streets/)
  to remove disorderly vagrants and direct them toward treatment instead
  of enabling chaos.
- **Restoring critical funding and delivering relief:** The Working
  Families Tax Cuts Act [restored and
  expanded](https://www.republicanleader.senate.gov/newsroom/research/republicans-back-the-blue#:~:text=%E2%80%9CThe%20bill%20includes%20funding%20for%20the%20Edward%20Byrne%20Memorial%20Justice%20Assistance%20Grant%20Program%20(Byrne%20JAG)%20and%20Community%20Oriented%20Policing%20Services%20(COPS)%20to%20support%20boots%2Don%2Dthe%2Dground%20efforts%20to%20combat%20violent%20crime%20in%20local%20communities.%E2%80%9D)
  Byrne JAG and COPS grants to fight violent crime — while delivering No
  Tax on Overtime for America’s hardworking officers so they can keep
  more of what they earn protecting us.
- **Ending cashless bail:** The Trump Administration
  [halted](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/08/taking-steps-to-end-cashless-bail-to-protect-americans/)
  federal support for the Radical Left’s “cashless bail” scheme that
  turned streets into revolving doors for dangerous criminals — and
  imposed consequences on jurisdictions that refused to act.
- **Dismissing anti-police lawsuits and DEI mandates:** Biden-era DOJ
  actions targeting local police and fire departments and weakening
  hiring standards were
  [dropped](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2025/02/trump-administration-stops-lowering-standards-for-police-firefighters/)
  to protect public safety — not political correctness.
- **Reclaiming law enforcement’s authority:** President Trump
  [took](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/04/strengthening-and-unleashing-americas-law-enforcement-to-pursue-criminals-and-protect-innocent-citizens/)
  bold action to promote aggressive, proactive policing based on proven
  best practices — while shielding officers from unjust expenses and
  liabilities so they can enforce the law without fear.
- **Deploying backup to high-crime cities:** The Trump Administration
  [surged](https://www.whitehouse.gov/releases/2026/02/president-trump-returned-our-nation-to-law-and-order/#:~:text=The%20results%20are,nearly%2050%20years.)
  federal resources into high-crime Democrat-run cities — standing
  shoulder-to-shoulder with local police to crush chaos, restore order,
  and rescue communities long victimized by Democrat failures.
- **Giving police the equipment they need:** The Department of Justice
  [reversed](https://www.whitehouse.gov/fact-sheets/2025/04/fact-sheet-president-donald-j-trump-strengthens-americas-law-enforcement-to-pursue-criminals-and-protect-innocent-citizens/)
  [Biden-era
  restrictions](https://www.presidency.ucsb.edu/documents/fact-sheet-the-biden-harris-administrations-work-make-our-communities-safer-and-advance#:~:text=and%20restrict%20the%20transfer%20of%20military%20equipment%20to%20local%20law%20enforcement%20agencies)
  and restored access to critical surplus military assets so local law
  enforcement has the tools to win.
- **Targeting criminal illegal aliens:** Federal agents are now
  [empowered](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/01/protecting-the-american-people-against-invasion/)
  to support local police in removing violent criminal illegals,
  resulting in hundreds of thousands of arrests and removals that make
  communities safer.
- **Slashing restrictive consent decrees:** The Trump Administration
  [launched](https://www.whitehouse.gov/fact-sheets/2025/04/fact-sheet-president-donald-j-trump-strengthens-americas-law-enforcement-to-pursue-criminals-and-protect-innocent-citizens/)
  an overhaul of federal consent decrees that micromanaged and hindered
  effective policing in cities across America.

**Under President Trump, America’s police are stronger, our communities
are safer, and the results prove it.**
