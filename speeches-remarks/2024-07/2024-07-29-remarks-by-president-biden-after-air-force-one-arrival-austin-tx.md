3:08 P.M. CDT

Q    Mr. President — Mr. President, House Speaker Johnson says your
Supreme Court reform is “dead on arrival.”  What’s your reaction, sir?

THE PRESIDENT:  Who said that?

Q    Speaker Johnson said it’s “dead on arrival.”

THE PRESIDENT:  I think that’s what he is. 

Q    That he is?

THE PRESIDENT:  That he is — dead on arrival.

Q    How are you going to get this go — how are you going to get this
done?

THE PRESIDENT:  You’ve asked me that –- on everything I’ve ever passed
you’ve asked me that.  We’re going to figure a way.
