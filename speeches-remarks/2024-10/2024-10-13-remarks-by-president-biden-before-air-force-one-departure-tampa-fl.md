Q    Mr. President, where was Governor DeSantis?  Did you speak with him
while you were here?

THE PRESIDENT:  No, I didn’t. 

But I — by — by the way, I think we’re making real progress.  Everybody
seems pretty happy with the way it’s going.  We’re not leaving.  We’re
provi- — we’re going to — the next thing to do, we’re trying to make
sure we get the money in there for small businesses; talking to the
Congress to see if they can get the money quickly.  It’s important. 

So, you saw, I mean, Republicans and Democrats are happy with what we’re
doing.  And so, we’re making progress.  We’re making progress.
