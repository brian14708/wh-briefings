South Lawn

3:50 P.M. EDT

THE PRESIDENT: I’m heading —

(Cross-talk.)

THE PRESIDENT: I’m —

(Cross-talk.)

THE PRESIDENT: I’m heading to do something that should have been done a
long time ago: to make a formal apology to the Indian Nations for the
way we treated their children for so many years. That’s why I’m going —
that’s why I’m heading West.

Thank you.
