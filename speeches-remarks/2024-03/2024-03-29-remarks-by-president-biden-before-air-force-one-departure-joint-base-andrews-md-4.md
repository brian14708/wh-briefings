Joint Base Andrews  
Prince George’s County, Maryland

1:58 P.M. EDT

Q    Mr. President, when will you visit Baltimore?

THE PRESIDENT:  Beg your pardon?

Q    When will you visit Baltimore?

THE PRESIDENT:  I’m going to Baltimore next week with the governor. 

Q    Do you have a message for Evan Gershkovich and his family today,
sir?

THE PRESIDENT:  Yes, we admire the hell out of him.  We’ve tried
everything we can so far to put him on a plane.  And we’re not going to
give up.
