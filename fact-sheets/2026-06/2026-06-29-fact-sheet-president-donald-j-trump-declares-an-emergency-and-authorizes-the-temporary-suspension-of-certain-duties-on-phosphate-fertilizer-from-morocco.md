  the Temporary Suspension of Certain Duties on Phosphate Fertilizer from Morocco'

**PROTECTING AMERICA’S FOOD SUPPLY: **Today, President Donald J. Trump
took action to safeguard America’s food supply and support domestic
agricultural production by authorizing the temporary suspension of
certain duties on phosphate fertilizer imported from Morocco, helping
ensure an adequate supply of phosphate fertilizer for American Farmers.

- The proclamation declares an emergency regarding threats to the
  availability of sufficient supplies of fertilizers to meet America’s
  agricultural demand.
- The proclamation addresses the emergency by authorizing the temporary
  suspension of certain anti-dumping and countervailing duties in
  connection with importation of phosphate fertilizer from the Kingdom
  of Morrocco for eight months or until the emergency is terminated,
  whichever occurs first.  

**SECURING FERTILIZER ACCESS FOR U.S. FARMERS: **President Trump
believes that American farmers must maintain reliable access to key
fertilizers to mitigate significant risks to food production, safeguard
national security, and ensure a stable domestic food supply. 

- Phosphate fertilizers are a critical type of plant food, a crucial
  component of agriculture production, and vital to feeding the Nation
  and maintaining America’s food security.
- Global supply chains for phosphate fertilizer and fertilizer inputs
  have been recently disrupted due to events such as conflicts in
  fertilizer-producing regions and trade actions taken by major
  fertilizer-producing countries. 
- This proclamation will ensure that American famers have access to a
  sufficient and timely supply of phosphate fertilizers in the near
  term to mitigate any significant risks to the domestic food supply. 
- The Administration continues to partner with American producers to
  expand their domestic manufacturing capacity, which will help provide
  long term stability for American farming.

**PRIORITIZING AMERICA’S FARMERS AND FOOD SECURITY: **President Trump
continues to support American farmers and drive prices down for
hardworking families. 

- President Trump’s Working Families Tax Cuts Act delivered historic
  relief to American farmers:
  - Made the virtual elimination of the death tax permanent, benefiting
    more than 2 million family farms;

  <!-- -->

  - Increased Section 179 small business expensing limits allow farmers
    to immediately write off new equipment and land improvements,
    freeing up capital for growth;

  <!-- -->

  - Made the 20% small business tax deduction is now permanent, allowing
    farmers to keep more of their income and reinvest in their
    operations;

  <!-- -->

  - Allowed farmers to defer paying capital gains taxes when selling
    their farmland to another farmer who will keep the land in
    agricultural use.
- President Trump’s bold America First Trade Agenda has dramatically
  increased global market access, strengthened farmer incomes, supported
  rural jobs, and reduced the agricultural trade deficit.
- In February 2026, Trump signed an Executive Order mobilizing the
  Defense Production Act to protect domestic production of elemental
  phosphorus — a critical input essential to military readiness and
  America’s agricultural strength.
- In June 2026, President Trump signed an Executive Order to accelerate
  American agriculture innovation, empowering farmers and ranchers and
  bolstering the strength and security of our food supply.
