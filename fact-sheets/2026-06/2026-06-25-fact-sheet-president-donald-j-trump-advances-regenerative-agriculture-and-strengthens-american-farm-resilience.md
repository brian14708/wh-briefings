**ACCELERATING INNOVATION IN AMERICA’S FOOD SUPPLY: **Today, President
Donald J. Trump signed an Executive Order to accelerate American
agriculture innovation, empowering farmers and ranchers and bolstering
the strength and security of our food supply.

- The Order directs the Secretary of Agriculture to expand the reach of
  the Department of Agriculture’s Regenerative Agriculture Pilot
  Program, broadly share results with stakeholders, and create
  public-private partnerships in order to increase capacity for farmers
  interested in adopting regenerative farming practices.
- The Order directs the Administrator of the Environmental Protection
  Agency to prioritize registration of alternative crop protection tools
  and review data on pre-harvest desiccation uses to ensure alignment
  with safety and labeling standards.
- The Order directs the Secretary of Agriculture, the Secretary of
  Health and Human Services, and the Administrator of the Environmental
  Protection Agency to develop research methodologies and frameworks to
  advance our scientific understanding of the effect of cumulative
  exposure across chemical classes that are regulated by statute in the
  food supply.
- The Order directs the Secretary of Health and Human Services to
  identify creative solutions that evaluate exposure, diagnosis, and
  treatments of cumulative chemical exposures on individual health and
  prioritize research of new, innovative, and
  cost-effective alternatives to historic crop protection tools. 

**STRENGTHENING FOOD SECURITY AND PRODUCTION: **President Trump is
empowering America’s farmers and ranchers to ensure the Nation’s food
supply is the healthiest, most abundant, and most affordable in the
world.

- American farmers and ranchers are increasingly interested in adopting
  innovative conservation farming practices like regenerative
  agriculture that strengthen soil health, lower input costs, and
  improve chemical efficiency to reduce overall use, among other
  benefits that strengthen farm resilience. 
- The Order further advances ongoing Administration work to implement
  precision agriculture solutions in programs in ways that keep
  decision-making local and practical while avoiding burdensome
  mandates.
- President Trump and his Administration are ensuring that American
  farmers and ranchers have unhindered access to new cost-effective
  technologies that increase production capacity and bolster the
  Nation’s long-term food supply.
- President Trump is equipping America’s farmers and ranchers with new
  tools to more easily and efficiently feed the Nation and strengthen
  rural economies.

**MAKING AMERICA HEALTHY AGAIN: **President Trump is executing on his
promise to transform the status quo to ensure that all Americans live
longer, healthier lives, supported by systems that prioritize
prevention, well-being, and resilience.

- In February 2025, President Trump signed an Executive Order
  establishing the President’s Make America Healthy Again Commission.
- In May 2025, the Make America Healthy Again Commission released its
  Make Our Children Healthy Again Assessment and in September 2025,
  released its Make Our Children Healthy Again Strategy.
- Earlier this year, the Department of Health and Human Services, the
  Department of Agriculture, and the Environmental Protection Agency
  made a historic investment of over $1 billion to accelerate farm
  innovation and long-term food supply security.
