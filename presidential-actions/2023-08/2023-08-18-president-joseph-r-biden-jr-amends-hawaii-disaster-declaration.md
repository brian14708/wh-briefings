title: "President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Amends Hawaii Disaster\_Declaration"
---
 
Today, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. made additional disaster
assistance available to the State of Hawaii by authorizing an increase
in the level of Federal funding for emergency work undertaken in the
State of Hawaii as a result of wildfires beginning on August 8, 2023,
and continuing.  
   
Under the President’s order today, Federal funds for debris removal and
emergency protective measures, including direct Federal assistance has
been increased to 100 percent of the total eligible costs for a
continuous 30-day period of the State’s choosing within the first 120
days from the start of the incident period.
