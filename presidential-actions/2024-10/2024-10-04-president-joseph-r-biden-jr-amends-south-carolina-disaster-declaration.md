title: "President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Amends South Carolina Disaster\_Declaration"
---
 
Today, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. made additional disaster
assistance available to the State of South Carolina by authorizing an
increase in the level of Federal funding for emergency work undertaken
in the State of South Carolina as a result of Hurricane Helene beginning
on September 25, 2024, and continuing.  
  
Under the President’s order today, Federal funds for debris removal and
emergency protective measures, including direct Federal assistance has
been increased to 100 percent of the total eligible costs for a period
of 90 days of the State’s choosing within the first 120 days from the
start of the incident period.
