title: "President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Amends North Carolina Disaster\_Declaration"
---
 
Today, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. made additional disaster
assistance available to the State of North Carolina by authorizing an
increase in the level of federal funding for emergency work undertaken
in the State of North Carolina as a result of Tropical Storm Helene
beginning on September 25, 2024, and continuing.

Under the President’s order today, the Federal funds for debris removal
and emergency protective measures, including direct Federal assistance
has been increased to 100 percent of the total eligible costs for 180
days from the start of the incident period.
