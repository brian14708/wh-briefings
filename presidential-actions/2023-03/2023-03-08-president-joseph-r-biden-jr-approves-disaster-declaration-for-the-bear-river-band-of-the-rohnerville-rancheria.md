title: "President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Approves Disaster Declaration for the Bear\
  \ River Band of the Rohnerville\_Rancheria"
---
 
Today, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. declared that a major disaster
exists for the Bear River Band of the Rohnerville Rancheria and ordered
federal aid to supplement the Tribal Nation’s efforts in the areas
affected by an earthquake from December 20, 2022, to January 1, 2023.

Federal funding is available to the Bear River Band of the Rohnerville
Rancheria and certain private nonprofit organizations on a cost-sharing
basis for emergency work and the repair or replacement of facilities
damaged by the earthquake.

Federal funding is also available on a cost-sharing basis for hazard
mitigation measures for the Bear River Band of the Rohnerville
Rancheria.

Deanne Criswell, Administrator, Federal Emergency Management Agency
(FEMA), Department of Homeland Security, named Benigno Bern Ruiz as the
Federal Coordinating Officer for Federal recovery operations in the
affected areas. 

Additional designations may be made at a later date if requested by the
Tribal Nation and warranted by the results of further damage
assessments.
