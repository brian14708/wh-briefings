title: "President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Amends Virginia Major Disaster\_Declaration"
---
 
Today, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. made additional disaster
assistance available to the Commonwealth of Virginia by authorizing an
increase in the level of Federal funding for debris removal undertaken
in the Commonwealth as a result of Tropical Storm Helene.

Under the President’s order today, Federal funds for debris removal,
including direct Federal assistance has been increased to 100 percent of
the total eligible costs for a period of 120 days of the Commonwealth’s
choosing within the first 180 days from the start of the incident period
on September 25.
