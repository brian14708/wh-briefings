title: "President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Approves Disaster Declaration for the Hoopa\
  \ Valley\_Tribe"
---
 
Today, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. declared that a major disaster
exists for the Hoopa Valley Tribe and ordered Federal aid to supplement
the Tribal Nation’s efforts in the areas affected by a severe winter
storm from January 30 to January 31, 2024.

Federal funding is available to the Hoopa Valley Tribe certain private
nonprofit organizations on a cost-sharing basis for emergency work and
the repair or replacement of facilities damaged by a severe winter
storm.

Federal funding is also available on a cost-sharing basis for hazard
mitigation measures for the Hoopa Valley Tribe.

Mr. Andrew F. Grant of the Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA)
has been appointed to coordinate Federal recovery operations in the
affected areas. 

Additional designations may be made at a later date if requested by the
Tribal Nation and warranted by the results of further damage
assessments.
