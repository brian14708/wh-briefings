title: "President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Amends Puerto Rico Disaster\_Declaration"
---
 
Today, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. made additional disaster
assistance available to the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico by authorizing
an increase in the level of federal funding for emergency work
undertaken in the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico as a result of Hurricane
Fiona during the period of September 17 to September 21, 2022.

Under the President’s major disaster declaration issued for the
Commonwealth of Puerto Rico on September 21, 2022, federal funding was
made available for debris removal and emergency protective measures,
including direct federal assistance, under the Public Assistance program
at 100 percent of the total eligible costs for a period of 30 days from
the start of the incident period.         

Under the President’s order today, an extension of the period of 100
percent federal funding for debris removal and emergency protective
measures, including direct federal assistance, from 30 to 60 days from
the start of the incident period.
