title: "President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Amends California Disaster\_Declaration"
---
 
Today, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. made additional disaster
assistance available to the State of California by authorizing an
increase in the level of Federal funding for emergency work undertaken
in the State of California as a result of wildfires and straight-line
winds beginning on January 7, 2025, and continuing.

Under the President’s order today, Federal funds for debris removal and
emergency protective measures, including direct Federal assistance have
been increased to 100 percent of the total eligible costs for a period
of 180 days of the State’s choosing within the first 270 days from the
start of the incident period.
