NOMINATION SENT TO THE SENATE:

Sarah B. Rogers, of New York, to be Chief Executive Officer of the
United States Agency for Global Media.

WITHDRAWALS SENT TO THE SENATE:

William Hewes III, of Mississippi, to be a Commissioner of the Consumer
Product Safety Commission for a term of seven years from October 27,
2024, which was sent to the Senate on January 13, 2026.

Jeremy Carl, of Montana, to be an Assistant Secretary of State
(International Organizations), which was sent to the Senate on January
13, 2026.
