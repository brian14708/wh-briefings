title: "President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Amends Hoopa Valley Tribe Disaster\_Declaration"
---
 
Today, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. made additional disaster
assistance available to the Hoopa Valley Tribe by authorizing an
increase in the level of federal funding for Public Assistance projects
undertaken as a result of severe winter storms and mudslides during the
period of February 14 to March 5, 2023.  
   
Under the President’s major disaster declaration issued for the Hoopa
Valley Tribe on April 25, 2023, federal funding was made available for
Public Assistance and Hazard  Mitigation at 75 percent of the total
eligible costs.           
   
Under the President’s order today, the federal share for Public
Assistance has been increased to 90 percent of the total eligible
costs.
