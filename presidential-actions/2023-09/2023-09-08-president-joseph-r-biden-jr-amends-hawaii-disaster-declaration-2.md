title: "President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Amends Hawaii Disaster\_Declaration"
---
 
Today, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. made additional disaster
assistance available to the State of Hawaii by authorizing an increase
in the level of federal funding for Public Assistance projects
undertaken in the State of Hawaii as a result of wildfires beginning on
August 8, 2023, and continuing.

Under the President’s order today, the federal share for Public
Assistance has been increased to 90 percent of total eligible costs,
except assistance previously approved at 100 percent.
