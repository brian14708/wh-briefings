MEMORANDUM FOR THE SECRETARY OF STATE THE ADMINISTRATOR OF THE UNITED
STATES AGENCY FOR INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT  
  
SUBJECT: Delegation of Authority Under Section 7019(e) of the Department
of State, Foreign Operations, and Related Programs Appropriations Act,
2024  
  
By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and the
laws of the United States of America, including section 301 of title 3,
United States Code, I hereby delegate to the Secretary of State, in
consultation with the Administrator of the United States Agency for
International Development, the functions related to a comprehensive,
multi-year strategy for the promotion of democracy abroad under section
7019(e) of the Department of State, Foreign Operations, and Related
Programs Appropriations Act, 2024 (Title VII of Division F of Public
Law 118-47), and the accompanying Joint Explanatory Statement.  
   
The delegation in this memorandum shall apply to any provision of any
future public law that is the same or substantially the same as the
provision referenced in this memorandum.  
   
The Secretary of State is authorized and directed to publish this
memorandum in the *Federal Register*.  
 

                              JOSEPH R. BIDEN JR.
