title: "President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Amends Vermont Disaster\_Declaration"
---
 
Today, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. made additional disaster
assistance available to the State of Vermont by authorizing an increase
in the level of federal funding for debris removal undertaken in the
State of Vermont as a result of severe storms, flooding, landslides, and
mudslides from July 7 to July 21, 2023.

Under the President’s order today, Federal funds for debris removal has
been increased to 100 percent of the total eligible costs for a 30-day
period of the State’s choosing within the first 120 days from the start
of the incident period.
