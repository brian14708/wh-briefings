Today, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. made additional disaster
assistance available to the Soboba Band of Luiseño Indians by
authorizing an increase in the level of federal funding for Public
Assistance projects undertaken as a result of severe storms and flooding
during the period of February 14-15, 2019.  
  
Under the major disaster declaration issued for the Soboba Band of
Luiseño Indians on April 8, 2019, federal funding was made available for
Public Assistance and Hazard Mitigation at 75 percent of the total
eligible costs.             
  
Under the President’s order today, the federal share for Public
Assistance has been increased to 90 percent of the total eligible costs.
