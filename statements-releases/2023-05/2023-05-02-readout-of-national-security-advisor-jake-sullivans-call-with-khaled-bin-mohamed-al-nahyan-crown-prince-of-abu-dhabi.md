National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan spoke today with Khaled
bin Mohamed Al Nahyan, the Crown Prince of Abu Dhabi.  Mr. Sullivan
congratulated Sheikh Khaled on his recent appointment and welcomed the
opportunity to work with him and his team.  The two sides discussed the
ambitious U.S.-UAE agenda this year, including the Partnership for
Accelerating Clean Energy (PACE) and the COP 28 climate summit.  He
welcomed the opportunity to meet with Sheikh Khaled in person over the
coming months.
