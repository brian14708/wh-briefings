National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met today with National Security
Advisor Henrik Landerholm of Sweden in London, United Kingdom.  Mr.
Sullivan reiterated the United States’ strong support for Sweden joining
NATO as soon as possible.  Mr. Sullivan and Mr. Landerholm reviewed the
security situation in Europe, including their commitment to continue
supporting Ukraine in the face of Russia’s ongoing aggression.  They
also discussed ways to further cooperation on shared global challenges.
