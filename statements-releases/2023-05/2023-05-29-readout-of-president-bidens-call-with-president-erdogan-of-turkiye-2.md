President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. called President Recep Tayyip Erdogan
today to congratulate him on his re-election as President of Türkiye. 
They expressed their shared commitment to continue working together as
close partners to deepen cooperation between our countries and people.
They also discussed their readiness as NATO Allies to address regional
and global challenges, including strengthening transatlantic security at
the NATO Summit in Vilnius.
