Middle East and North Africa Brett McGurk spoke yesterday with Prime

relationship and the longstanding, close historical ties between the
American and Tunisian peoples.Â  Mr. McGurk and Prime Minister Bouden

diplomatic engagement, including by supporting the work of the diplomats
at the U.S. Embassy in Tunis and the Tunisian Embassy in Washington.
