Dear Madam Speaker: (Dear Madam President:)

In accordance with section 517 of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961, as
amended (22 U.S.C. 2321k), I am providing notice of my intent to rescind
the designation of Afghanistan as a Major Non‑NATO Ally.

                               Sincerely,

                               JOSEPH R. BIDEN JR.
