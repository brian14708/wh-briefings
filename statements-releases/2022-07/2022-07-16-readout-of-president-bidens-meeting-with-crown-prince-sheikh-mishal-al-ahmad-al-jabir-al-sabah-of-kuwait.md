---
 
Today in Jeddah, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. met with Crown Prince
Sheikh Mishal Al-Ahmad Al-Jabir Al-Sabah of Kuwait.  The President
expressed appreciation for the longstanding friendship between the
United States and Kuwait.  The two leaders reaffirmed the strong
bilateral relationship between the two countries and committed to work
closely together on a range of regional challenges.  President Biden
recognized Kuwait’s constructive diplomatic role in lowering regional
tensions, including Kuwait’s recent confidence-building efforts to
restore diplomatic ties between Gulf states and Lebanon.  President
Biden also commended Kuwait’s support for an initiative to connect
Iraq’s electricity grid with those of its Gulf neighbors.
