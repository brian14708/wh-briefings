President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. met today with King Hamad bin Isa Al
Khalifa of Bahrain in Jeddah.  The President underscored the United
States’ appreciation for the longstanding strategic partnership with
Bahrain, including its hosting of the U.S. Navy Forces Central
Command/5<sup>th</sup> Fleet.  The two leaders affirmed their commitment
to strengthening defense, security, economic, commercial, and
people-to-people ties.  
  
President Biden commended Bahrain’s expanded ties with Israel through
the Abraham Accords and the Negev process. The two leaders
discussed mutual efforts to foster regional integration
and deepen security cooperation.
