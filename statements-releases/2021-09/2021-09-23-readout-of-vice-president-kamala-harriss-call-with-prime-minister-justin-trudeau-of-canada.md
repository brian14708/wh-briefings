Vice President Kamala Harris spoke yesterday evening with Prime Minister
of Canada Justin Trudeau to congratulate the Prime Minister on his
party’s victory in the federal elections earlier this week.  The Vice
President conveyed our administration’s appreciation for the strong and
enduring partnership between the U.S. and Canada.  The Prime Minister
thanked the Vice President for the call, and the two leaders discussed
their shared commitment to ending the pandemic and strengthening our
economies.
