NOMINATIONS SENT TO THE SENATE:

     Douglas R. Bush, of Virginia, to be an Assistant Secretary of the
Army, vice Bruce D. Jette.

Secretary for Intelligence and Analysis, Department of the Treasury,
vice Isabel Marie Keenan Patelunas.

Department of the Army, vice James E. McPherson, resigned.

     Ashish S. Vazirani, of Maryland, to be a Deputy Under Secretary of
Defense, vice Laura Junor, resigned.
