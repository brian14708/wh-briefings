Vice President Harris spoke today with His Highness, Crown Prince Mishal
Al-Ahmad Al-Jabir Al-Sabah of Kuwait. The Vice President expressed her
appreciation for the longstanding friendship between the United States
and Kuwait. She also expressed her deep gratitude for the critical role
that Kuwait played in supporting the evacuation from Afghanistan of
Americans and vulnerable Afghans. The two leaders reaffirmed the strong
bilateral relationship between the two countries and committed to work
closely on regional challenges.
