title: Statement by NSC Spokesperson Emily Horne on Senior Administration Official
  Trip to Middle East, including Saudi Arabia and UAE
---
 
National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan will travel to the Middle East,
including Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates (UAE), joined by
National Security Council Middle East and North Africa Coordinator Brett
McGurk and U.S. Special Envoy for Yemen Tim Lenderking. Mr. Sullivan
will meet with senior leaders on a range of regional and global
challenges.
