  \ Nikos Christodoulides of the Republic of\_Cyprus"
---
 
On October 30, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. will host President Nikos
Christodoulides of the Republic of Cyprus for a bilateral meeting at the
White House. Building on the successful inaugural U.S.-Republic of
Cyprus Strategic Dialogue, launched on October 23, the leaders will
discuss a range of global issues, including energy security and
cooperation, events in the Middle East, and continued robust support to
Ukraine in its defense against Russian aggression. Acknowledging the
50th anniversary of the island’s division, President Biden will
reiterate U.S. support for a bizonal, bicommunal federation with
political equality for all Cypriots.
