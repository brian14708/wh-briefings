President Joseph R. Biden, Jr., spoke today with Chancellor Olaf Scholz
of Germany. The President underscored the enduring strength of the
U.S.-German relationship and noted his intention to continue our strong
collaboration on geopolitical priorities, including supporting Ukraine’s
defense against Russian aggression.
