National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan spoke by phone today with Indian
National Security Advisor Ajit Doval.  The two leaders discussed
regional security developments, underscoring the need for further
efforts to ensure stability in the Indo-Pacific region and globally. 
They welcomed progress in the bilateral partnership, including through
the upcoming Initiative on Critical and Emerging Technology (iCET)
Intersessional and Indian Ocean Dialogue.  They also discussed further
opportunities for closer collaboration in key domains, including clean
energy supply chains and defense cooperation.
