Given the projected trajectory and strength of Hurricane Milton,
President Biden is postponing his upcoming trip to Germany and Angola in
order to oversee preparations for and the response to Hurricane Milton,
in addition to the ongoing response to the impacts of Hurricane Helene
across the Southeast.
