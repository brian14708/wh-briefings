title: "Readout of Vice President Harris\u2019s Call with Prime Minister Mottley of\_\
  Barbados"
---
 
Vice President Kamala Harris today spoke to Prime Minister Mia Mottley
of Barbados, Chair of the Caribbean Community (CARICOM), and thanked her
for her partnership. The Vice President emphasized her belief that U.S.
economic and strategic interests are intertwined with those of our
neighbors in the Caribbean. They reviewed progress in the U.S.-Caribbean
partnership, and the Vice President’s leadership in strengthening the
relationships throughout the region, including four leader-level summits
that the Vice President hosted and the launch of the U.S.-Caribbean
Partnership to Address the Climate Crisis 2030. The Vice President
welcomed shared progress on climate adaptation and resilience, food
security, and renewable energy investments and reaffirmed her interest
in continuing to strengthen the bonds between the American people and
people throughout the Caribbean.
