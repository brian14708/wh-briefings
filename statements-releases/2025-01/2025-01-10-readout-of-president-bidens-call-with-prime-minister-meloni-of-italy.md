---
 
President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with Prime Minister Giorgia
Meloni of Italy to express thanks for Italy’s role as a leader in the
G7, NATO, and European Union. President Biden expressed appreciation to
Prime Minister Meloni for Italy’s support for the people of Ukraine. The
leaders affirmed the enduring strength of U.S.-Italy relations.
