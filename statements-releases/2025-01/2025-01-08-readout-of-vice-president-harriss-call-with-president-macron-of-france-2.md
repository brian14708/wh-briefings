title: "Readout of Vice President Harris\u2019s Call with President Macron of\_France"
---
 
Vice President Kamala Harris spoke today to President Emmanuel Macron of
France and thanked him for his partnership throughout the Biden-Harris
Administration. They reflected on their first meeting in November 2021
in Paris and further meetings in Washington and Munich. The Vice
President expressed continued support for a strong, united NATO and
thanked President Macron for his support of Ukraine. The Vice President
underscored her continued support for the longstanding U.S.-France
Alliance, based on shared democratic principles and values, and noted
how our partnership has advanced our shared global priorities, from
Africa to the Indo-Pacific and space and technology.
