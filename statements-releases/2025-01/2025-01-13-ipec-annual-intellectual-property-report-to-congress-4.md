The Office of the Intellectual Property Enforcement Coordinator (IPEC)
has issued its [Annual Intellectual Property Report to
Congress](https://bidenwhitehouse.archives.gov/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/IPEC-FY-24-Annual-Report_Final.pdf),
which brings together the combined and coordinated efforts of the White
House, the Departments of Agriculture, Commerce, Defense, Health and
Human Services, Homeland Security, Justice, State, and Treasury, the
Office of the U.S. Trade Representative, and the U.S. Copyright Office.
The report provides an overview of the intellectual property enforcement
strategy and related efforts undertaken by departments and agencies
during fiscal year 2024.

To view the Annual Intellectual Property Report to Congress,
visit: <https://bidenwhitehouse.archives.gov/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/IPEC-FY-24-Annual-Report_Final.pdf>
