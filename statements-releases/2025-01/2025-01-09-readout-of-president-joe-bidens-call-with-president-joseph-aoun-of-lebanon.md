President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with President Joseph Aoun of
Lebanon to congratulate him on his election victory. The Presidents
agreed that the time is now to fully implement the cessation of
hostilities announced on November 26, 2024, and for Lebanon and its
people to recover and rebuild. President Biden pledged to continue U.S.
support for Lebanon’s security forces, and for Lebanon’s recovery and
reconstruction as President Aoun works with the United States and
Lebanon’s true friends in the international community towards a Lebanon
that is once again secure, sovereign, and prosperous.  President Biden
assured President Aoun that he has full confidence President Aoun is the
right leader to guide Lebanon through this moment of challenges and
opportunities for his country.
