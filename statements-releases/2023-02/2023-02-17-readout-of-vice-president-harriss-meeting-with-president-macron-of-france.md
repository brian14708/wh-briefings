  France"
---
 
Vice President Kamala Harris met today with President Emmanuel Macron of
France in Munich, Germany.  The Vice President and President Macron
reaffirmed the importance of continued support for Ukraine and imposing
costs on Russia for its brutal invasion. The Vice President reaffirmed
that the United States would support Ukraine for as long as it takes,
and they discussed next steps in our united response. The Vice President
and President Macron discussed the strength of NATO and U.S.-European
relations. The Vice President and President Macron discussed  challenges
posed by the People’s Republic of China, including the importance of
upholding the rules-based order, and agreed to remain closely
aligned. The Vice President noted the importance of Africa and they
agreed to deepen cooperation toward economic growth on the
continent. They also discussed transatlantic cooperation in addressing
the climate crisis and accelerating the transition to clean energy.
