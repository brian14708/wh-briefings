title: "Readout of Vice President Harris\u2019s Meeting with Chancellor Scholz of\_\
  Germany"
---
 
Vice President Kamala Harris met today with Chancellor Olaf Scholz of
Germany in Munich, Germany.  The Vice President and Chancellor Scholz
discussed Russia’s brutal invasion of Ukraine, reviewed recent
developments, and reaffirmed their enduring commitment to the people of
Ukraine. The Vice President thanked Chancellor Scholz for his global
leadership. The Vice President welcomed Germany’s historic defense
investments and strong humanitarian, economic, and security assistance
for Ukraine, including Germany’s provision of Leopard tanks and
budgetary support for Ukraine.  They discussed challenges posed by the
People’s Republic of China, including the importance of upholding the
rules-based order, and agreed to remain closely aligned. The leaders
also discussed cooperation on efforts to address the climate crisis and
accelerate the transition to clean energy.
