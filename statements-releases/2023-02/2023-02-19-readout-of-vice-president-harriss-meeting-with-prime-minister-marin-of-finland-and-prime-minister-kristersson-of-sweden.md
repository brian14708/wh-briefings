title: "Readout of Vice President Harris\u2019s Meeting with Prime Minister Marin\
  \ of Finland and Prime Minister Kristersson of\_Sweden"
---
 
Vice President Kamala Harris met with Prime Minister Sanna Marin of
Finland and Prime Minister Ulf Kristersson of Sweden in Munich, Germany
on February 18.  Vice President Harris and Prime Ministers Marin and
Kristersson discussed the close bonds and cooperation between the United
States and Finland and Sweden.  The Vice President reiterated the U.S.
commitment to welcoming Sweden and Finland into the NATO Alliance and to
working with both countries to remain vigilant against any threats to
our shared security, and to deter and confront aggression or threat of
aggression.  The Vice President and the two Prime Ministers discussed
next steps in our response to Russia’s brutal invasion of Ukraine, the
importance of upholding the international rules-based order and other
regional and global issues.
