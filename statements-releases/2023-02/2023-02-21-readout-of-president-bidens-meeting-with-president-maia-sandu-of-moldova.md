President Joseph R. Biden Jr. met today with Moldovan President Maia
Sandu in Warsaw. President Biden reaffirmed strong U.S. support for
Moldova’s sovereignty and territorial integrity. He highlighted ongoing
US assistance to help Moldova strengthen its political and economic
resilience, including its democratic reform agenda and energy security,
and to address the effects of Russia’s war against Ukraine.
