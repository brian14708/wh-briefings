National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met with Senior Minister Tharman
Shanmugaratnam of Singapore today in Washington, DC. Mr. Sullivan and
Mr. Shanmugaratnam discussed the importance of the U.S.-Singapore
Strategic Partnership and reviewed opportunities to build on the
momentum generated by Vice President Harris’s August trip to
Singapore. They discussed pressing regional issues, including the crisis
in Burma, and global challenges such as climate change and energy
affordability. They underscored that bold action is needed to end this
pandemic and prepare for the next, agreeing on the importance of working
with partners to strengthen global health security financing and
establish a new financial intermediary fund.
