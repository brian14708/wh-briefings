National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met today in Washington with UAE
Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation Sheikh
Abdullah bin Zayed Al Nahyan to reaffirm areas of strategic cooperation
between the United States and the UAE. They discussed several issues of
mutual interest, including mechanisms for enhancing regional integration
and dialogue, and the development of a framework for closer bilateral
relations.
