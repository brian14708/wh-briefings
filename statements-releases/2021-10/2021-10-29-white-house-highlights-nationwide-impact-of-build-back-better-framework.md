Today, the White House released state-by-state fact sheets that
highlight how the Build Back Better Framework will impact working
families across the country. The Build Back Better Framework includes
transformative investments in climate, care, and kids that will lower
the costs of things families depend on, help people get back to work,
and grow the economy.

The framework will help the U.S. meet its climate goals, create millions
of good-paying jobs, enable more Americans to join and remain in the
labor force, and grow our economy from the bottom up and the middle out.
And, it will level the playing field and make corporations and the
wealthiest pay their fair share, so that middle class families can
finally get some breathing room.
