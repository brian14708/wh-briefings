NOMINATION SENT TO THE SENATE:

     Armando O. Bonilla, of the District of Columbia, to be a Judge of
the United States Court of Federal Claims for a term of fifteen years,
vice Edward J. Damich, term expired.

WITHDRAWAL SENT TO THE SENATE:

     Armando O. Bonilla, of Virginia, to be a Judge of the United States
Court of Federal Claims for a term of fifteen years, vice Edward J.
Damich, term expired, which was sent to the Senate on July 13, 2021.
