Dear Madam Speaker: (Dear Madam President:)

Pursuant to title 2, United States Code, section 2a(a), I transmit
herewith the statement showing the apportionment population for each
State as of April 1, 2020, and the number of Representatives to which
each State would be entitled.

Sincerely,

JOSEPH R. BIDEN JR.
