On May 7, Vice President Kamala Harris will meet virtually with Mexican
President Andrés Manuel López Obrador, who will be joined by Secretary
of Foreign Relations Marcelo Ebrard Casaubon and Secretary of Economy
Tatiana Clouthier Carrillo.  This meeting will deepen the partnership
between our countries to achieve the common goals of prosperity, good
governance, and addressing the root causes of migration.
