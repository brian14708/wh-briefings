  \ Travel to Colombia, Argentina, and\_Uruguay"

Special Assistant to the President and Senior Director for the Western
Hemisphere Juan Gonzalez and Department of State Acting Assistant
Secretary for Western Hemisphere Affairs Julie Chung will
travel April 11 to 15 to Colombia, Argentina, and Uruguay to engage with
government officials. In Bogota, they will discuss economic recovery,
security and rural development, the Venezuelan migrant crisis, and
Colombia’s regional climate leadership. In Argentina and Uruguay, they
will discuss regional priorities, including addressing the challenges
of the climate crisis and the COVID-19 pandemic and threats to
democracy, human rights, and security in our hemisphere and around the
world.
