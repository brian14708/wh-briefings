title: Readout of President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Call with Prime Minister Narendra
  Modi of India
---
 
President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with Prime Minister Narendra
Modi of India, committing that the United States and India will work
closely together in the fight against COVID-19. The President pledged
America’s steadfast support for the people of India who have been
impacted by the recent surge in COVID-19 cases. In response, the United
States is providing a range of emergency assistance, including
oxygen-related supplies, vaccine materials, and therapeutics. Prime
Minister Modi expressed appreciation for the strong cooperation between
both countries. The two leaders resolved that the United States and
India will continue to stand shoulder-to-shoulder in the effort to
protect our citizens and the health of our communities.
