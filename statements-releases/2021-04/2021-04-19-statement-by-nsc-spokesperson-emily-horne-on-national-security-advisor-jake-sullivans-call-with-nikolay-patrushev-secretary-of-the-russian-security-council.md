National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan spoke by phone today with
Nikolay Patrushev, Secretary of the Russian Security Council. The two
discussed a number of issues in the bilateral relationship, as well as
regional and global matters of concern. Mr. Sullivan and Secretary
Patrushev also discussed the prospect of a presidential summit between
the United States and Russia and agreed to continue to stay in touch.
