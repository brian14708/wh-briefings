  \ Sullivan\u2019s Call with Foreign Minister Retno Marsudi of\_Indonesia"
---
 
National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan spoke by phone yesterday with
Foreign Minister Retno Marsudi of Indonesia. Mr. Sullivan conveyed his
deep concern regarding the missing Indonesian submarine off the coast of
Bali, underscoring that the United States would do everything possible
to support Indonesia’s search and rescue effort. He highlighted the
Biden-Harris Administration’s commitment to deepening the U.S.-Indonesia
Strategic Partnership and promoting ASEAN centrality. They discussed the
deteriorating humanitarian situation in Burma and agreed that democracy
should be swiftly restored. They also discussed the importance of
upholding international law in the South China Sea.
