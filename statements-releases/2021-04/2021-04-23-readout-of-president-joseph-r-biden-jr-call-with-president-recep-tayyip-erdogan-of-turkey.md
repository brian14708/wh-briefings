  \ Erdogan of\_Turkey"

President Joseph R. Biden spoke today with Turkish President Recep
Tayyip Erdogan, conveying his interest in a constructive bilateral
relationship with expanded areas of cooperation and effective management
of disagreements. The leaders agreed to hold a bilateral meeting on the
margins of the NATO Summit in June to discuss the full range of
bilateral and regional issues.
