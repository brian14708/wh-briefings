President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with Chancellor Olaf Scholz
of Germany.  The leaders discussed their steadfast support to Ukraine
and condemned Russia’s aggression.  President Biden and Chancellor
Scholz discussed their ongoing assistance to Ukraine.
