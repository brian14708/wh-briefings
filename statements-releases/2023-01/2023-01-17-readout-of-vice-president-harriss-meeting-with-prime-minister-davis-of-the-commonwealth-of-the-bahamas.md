title: "Readout of Vice President Harris\u2019s Meeting with Prime Minister Davis\
  \ of The Commonwealth of The\_Bahamas"
---
 
Vice President Kamala Harris today met Prime Minister Philip Davis of
The Commonwealth of The Bahamas at the White House. The Vice President
congratulated The Bahamas on assuming the Chair of the Caribbean
Community, and reaffirmed the U.S. commitment to strengthen our
partnership with the nations and people of the Caribbean.  The Vice
President highlighted efforts through the U.S.-Caribbean Partnership to
Address the Climate Crisis 2030 to help address energy security, the
effects of climate change, and other challenges facing the region.  The
two also discussed the ongoing situation in Haiti, and the Vice
President thanked Prime Minister Davis for The Bahamas’ cooperation on
migration.
