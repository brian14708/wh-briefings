title: "President\_Biden Announces Presidential Delegation to the Dominican Republic\
  \ to Attend the Inauguration of His Excellency Luis\_Abinader"
---
 
President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. today announced the designation of a
Presidential Delegation to attend the Inauguration of His Excellency
Luis Abinader on August 16, 2024, in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic.

The Honorable Jennifer M. Granholm, Secretary of the U.S. Department of
Energy, will lead the delegation.

<span style="text-decoration: underline">Members of the Presidential
Delegation:</span>

Ms. Patricia Aguilera, Chargé d’Affaires, U.S. Embassy Santo Domingo

The Honorable Tom Perez, Assistant to the President and Senior Advisor
to the President and Director of the Office of Intergovernmental
Affairs, The White House

The Honorable Alejandra Y. Castillo, Assistant Secretary of Commerce for
Economic Development, U.S. Department of Commerce

The Honorable Chris Dodd, Special Presidential Advisor for the Americas,
U.S. Department of State

The Honorable Daniel P. Erikson, Special Assistant to the President and
Senior Director for the Western Hemisphere, National Security Council,
The White House
