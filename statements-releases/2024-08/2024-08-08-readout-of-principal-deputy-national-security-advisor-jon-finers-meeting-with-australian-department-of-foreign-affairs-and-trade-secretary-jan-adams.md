Principal Deputy National Security Advisor (PDNSA) Jon Finer met
yesterday with Australian Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade
Secretary Jan Adams. PDNSA Finer and Secretary Adams discussed our
strong bilateral cooperation across the Indo-Pacific region, including
with partners in Southeast Asia and the Pacific, as well as globally.
PDNSA Finer and Secretary Adams also welcomed the successful Australia –
U.S. Ministerial Consultations that took place August 6 in Annapolis,
Maryland, and reaffirmed support for further advancing the
U.S.-Australia Alliance.
