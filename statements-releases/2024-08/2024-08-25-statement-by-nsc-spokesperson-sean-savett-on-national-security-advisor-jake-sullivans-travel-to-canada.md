National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan will travel to Canada on August
25. While there, he will meet with Prime Minister of Canada Justin
Trudeau and address Canada’s annual cabinet retreat. These meetings
underscore the strength of the U.S.-Canada bilateral relationship on a
range of national security priorities, including the climate crisis,
Ukraine’s continued defense, and the advancement of democracy and human
rights around the world.
