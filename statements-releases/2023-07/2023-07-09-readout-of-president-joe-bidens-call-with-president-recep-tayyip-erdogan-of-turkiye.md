President Joe Biden spoke today with President Recep Tayyıp Erdoğan of
Türkiye in advance of the NATO Summit in Vilnius, Lithuania.  They
discussed the range of issues that NATO leaders will consider at the
Summit, expressed their shared commitment to continue supporting
Ukraine, and reviewed efforts to strengthen our bilateral ties. 
President Biden also conveyed his desire to welcome Sweden into NATO as
soon as possible.
