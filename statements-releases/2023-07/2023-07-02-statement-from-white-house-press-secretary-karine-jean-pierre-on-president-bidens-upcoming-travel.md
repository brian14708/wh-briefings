President Biden is scheduled to travel to the United Kingdom, Lithuania,
and Finland from July 9-13. President Biden will first travel to London,
United Kingdom for engagements with King Charles III and Prime
Minister Rishi Sunak to further strengthen the close relationship
between our nations. The President will then travel to Vilnius,
Lithuania from July 11- 12 to attend the 74th NATO Summit. On July 13,
President Biden will visit Helsinki, Finland for a U.S.-Nordic Leaders
Summit. More details about the trip will be announced soon.
