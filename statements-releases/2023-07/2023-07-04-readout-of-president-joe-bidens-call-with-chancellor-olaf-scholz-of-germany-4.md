President Joe Biden spoke today with Chancellor Olaf Scholz of
Germany to review preparations for the NATO Summit to be held in
Vilnius, Lithuania. They discussed a range of issues that leaders will
consider at the Summit, including ways to further strengthen the
Alliance, and looked forward to continuing these conversations with
Allies in Vilnius.  Chancellor Scholz wished the President and the
American people a happy Independence Day.
