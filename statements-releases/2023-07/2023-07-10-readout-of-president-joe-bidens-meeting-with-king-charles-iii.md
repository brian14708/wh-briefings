---
 
President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. met today with King Charles III at
Windsor Castle.  They underscored the strength of the relationship
between the United States and the United Kingdom and the friendship
between our people.  Following the meeting, they engaged with a group of
leading philanthropists and investors focused on mobilizing finance to
address the climate crisis.
