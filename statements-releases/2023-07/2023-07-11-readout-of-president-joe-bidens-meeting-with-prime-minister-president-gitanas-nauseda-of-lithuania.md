President Joe Biden met with President Gitanas Nausėda of Lithuania in
Vilnius on July 11, 2023. President Biden thanked President Nausėda for
Lithuania’s hospitality in hosting the 2023 NATO Summit. He emphasized
the ironclad U.S. commitment to NATO security, demonstrated through
persistent, consecutive rotations of U.S. military personnel in
Lithuania. The leaders discussed their ongoing commitment to Ukraine’s
security and imposing costs on Russia for its aggression for as long as
necessary.
