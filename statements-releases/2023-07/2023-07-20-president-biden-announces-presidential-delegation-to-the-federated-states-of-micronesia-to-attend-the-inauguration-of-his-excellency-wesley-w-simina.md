President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. today announced the designation of a
Presidential Delegation to attend the Inauguration of His Excellency
Wesley W. Simina on Wednesday, July 26<sup>th</sup>, 2023, in Palikir,
Federated States of Micronesia.

The Honorable Deb Haaland, Secretary of the United States Department of
the Interior, will lead the delegation.

**<u>Members of the Presidential Delegation:</u>**

Ms. Alissa Bibb, Chargé d’Affaires, a.i., U.S. Embassy Federated States
of Micronesia

The Honorable Erika Moritsugu, Deputy Assistant to the President and
Asian American, Native Hawaiian, and Pacific Islanders Senior Liaison,
The White House

The Honorable Carmen G. Cantor, Assistant Secretary for Insular and
International Affairs, Department of the Interior, and U.S. Ambassador
to the Federated States of Micronesia (2020-2022)

Rear Admiral Gregory C. Huffman, Commander, Joint Region Marianas
