President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. will meet with President Andrés Manuel
López Obrador of Mexico for a bilateral meeting in San Francisco on
November 17 on the sidelines of the APEC Economic Leaders’ Week.  During
the meeting, the two leaders will discuss ongoing efforts to strengthen
the U.S.-Mexico bilateral relationship and address issues of shared
concern. President Biden and President López Obrador will also discuss
how we can continue to work together as partners to manage migration at
our shared border and mobilize a hemispheric-wide response to this
challenge.
