President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke this afternoon with Prime Minister
Benjamin Netanyahu of Israel regarding the latest developments in Israel
and Gaza. The President and the Prime Minister discussed at length
ongoing efforts to secure the release of hostages held by Hamas,
including many children and a number of Americans.
