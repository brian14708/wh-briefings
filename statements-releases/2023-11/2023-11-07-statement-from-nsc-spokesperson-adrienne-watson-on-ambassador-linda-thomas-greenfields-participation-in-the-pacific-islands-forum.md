Ambassador Linda Thomas-Greenfield will represent President Biden at the
Pacific Islands Forum leaders session with Forum Dialogue Partners in
Rarotonga, Cook Islands, on November 10.  Ambassador Linda
Thomas-Greenfield’s participation at the Pacific Islands Forum, as
announced at the September 25<sup>th</sup> U.S.-Pacific Islands Forum
Summit in Washington, reaffirms President Biden’s commitment to renewing
and enhancing our relationships in the Pacific Islands, including
through the region’s preeminent organization.
