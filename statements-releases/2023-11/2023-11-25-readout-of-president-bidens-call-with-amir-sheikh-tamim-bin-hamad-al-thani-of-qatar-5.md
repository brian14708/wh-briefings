President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with Amir Sheikh Tamim Bin
Hamad Al-Thani and Prime Minister Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulrahman al
Thani of Qatar on implementation of the hostage deal and humanitarian
pause in Gaza.  The leaders discussed hurdles to implementation of the
deal and mechanisms for resolving those hurdles as soon as possible.
 They agreed to remain in close contact to ensure the deal is fully
implemented according to its terms.
