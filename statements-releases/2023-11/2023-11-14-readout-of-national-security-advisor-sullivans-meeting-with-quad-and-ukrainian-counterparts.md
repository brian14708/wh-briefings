National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met on November 13 with French
Diplomatic Counselor to the President Emmanuel Bonne, German Chancellery
Foreign and Security Policy Advisor Jens Ploetner, British National
Security Advisor Tim Barrow, and Head of the Office of the President of
Ukraine Andriy Yermak. Mr. Yermak provided an update on Ukraine’s
counteroffensive. The partners reaffirmed their continued commitment to
provide security assistance as Ukraine continues its fight against
Russian aggression, as well as to invest in our joint defense industrial
bases to support Ukraine’s long term security. They also discussed their
sustained support for Ukraine’s economic and energy resilience, as
well as its reconstruction. In addition, the group took stock of ongoing
efforts to build global consensus in support of a just and lasting
peace based on the principles of sovereignty and territorial integrity.
