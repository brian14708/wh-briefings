National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met today with President-elect
Javier Milei of Argentina to discuss the importance of continuing to
build on the strong relationship between the United States and Argentina
on economic issues, and on shared priorities such as investing in
technology and clean energy, advocating for human rights, and standing
up for democracies around the world. National Security Advisor Sullivan
congratulated President-elect Milei on his election victory and
committed to continued close cooperation between our two countries.
