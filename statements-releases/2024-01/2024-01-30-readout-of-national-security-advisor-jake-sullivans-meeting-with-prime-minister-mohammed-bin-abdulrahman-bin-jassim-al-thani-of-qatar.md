National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met with Prime Minister Sheikh
Mohammed bin Abdulrahman bin Jassim Al Thani of Qatar today to discuss
the ongoing crisis in Gaza and their efforts to secure the immediate
release of all remaining hostages held by Hamas.  Mr. Sullivan thanked
Sheikh Mohammed for his unwavering efforts in this regard.  He urged
that all possible efforts be brought to bear on Hamas to secure the
release of hostages without delay. They agreed to stay in regular
contact until all hostages are reunited with their families.
