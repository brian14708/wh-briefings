  \ Prime Minister Kishida Fumio and Mrs. Kishida Yuko of\_Japan"
---
 
President Joe Biden and First Lady Jill Biden will host Prime Minister
Kishida Fumio and Mrs. Kishida Yuko of Japan for an Official Visit to
the United States, which will include a state dinner, on April 10, 2024.
The visit will underscore the enduring strength of our Alliance
partnership, the unwavering U.S. commitment to Japan, and Japan’s
increasing global leadership role.  President Biden and Prime Minister
Kishida will discuss efforts to strengthen our political, security,
economic, and people-to-people ties so that our Alliance is postured to
address evolving challenges and advance our shared vision for a free,
open, secure, and prosperous Indo-Pacific region and world.
