  \ Jake Sullivan\u2019s Meeting with Director Wang\_Yi"

On January 26-27, National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan will meet with
Chinese Communist Party Politburo Member, Director of the Office of the
Foreign Affairs Commission, and Foreign Minister Wang Yi in Bangkok,
Thailand. This meeting continues the commitment by both sides at the
November 2023 Woodside Summit between President Biden and President Xi
to maintain strategic communication and responsibly manage the
relationship.
