National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met with Prime Minister Sheikh
Mohammed bin Abdulrahman bin Jassim Al Thani of Qatar today in Davos,
Switzerland. Both parties discussed the urgent effort to release all
remaining hostages held by Hamas, including American citizens, as well
as ongoing initiatives to facilitate increased and sustained life-saving
access to humanitarian assistance into Gaza.
