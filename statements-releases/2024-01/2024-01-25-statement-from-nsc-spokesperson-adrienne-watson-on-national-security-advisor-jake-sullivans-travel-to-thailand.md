National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan is traveling to Bangkok,
Thailand from January 25-27. There, he will meet with the Prime Minister
of the Kingdom of Thailand Srettha Thavisin and Deputy Prime
Minister/Foreign Minister Parnpree Bahiddha-nukara. In these meetings,
Mr. Sullivan will reaffirm the importance of the U.S.-Thailand treaty
alliance and partnership and discuss opportunities to strengthen
cooperation across a range of bilateral and global issues.Â
