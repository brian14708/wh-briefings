President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with Chancellor Olaf Scholz
of Germany. The leaders coordinated on their countries’ ongoing
steadfast support to Ukraine in the face of Russia’s war of aggression.
They also reaffirmed their support for Israel’s right to
self-defense and discussed the imperative of protecting civilians in
Gaza against harm, and ensuring the free flow of life-saving
humanitarian aid to people in need.
