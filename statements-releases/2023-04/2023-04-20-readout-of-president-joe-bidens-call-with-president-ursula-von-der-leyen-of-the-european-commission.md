President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with President Ursula von der
Leyen of the European Commission.  The two leaders discussed President
von der Leyen’s recent trip to Beijing and their shared commitment to
upholding the rules-based international order, human rights, and fair
trade practices.  They reaffirmed the importance of maintaining peace
and stability across the Taiwan Strait.  President Biden and President
von der Leyen also reiterated the commitment of the United States and
the European Union to ensure that Ukraine has the economic and security
assistance it needs and to continuing to work closely together to impose
costs on the Kremlin for Russia’s aggression.  The leaders also
discussed ongoing joint efforts to accelerate the transition to clean
energy economies.
