President Joseph R. Biden Jr. spoke today with President Emmanuel Macron
of France. Following up on their April 4 conversation, the two leaders
discussed President Macron’s recent travel to the People’s Republic of
China and their ongoing efforts to advance prosperity, security, shared
values, and the rules-based international order in the Indo-Pacific
region.  They reaffirmed the importance of maintaining peace and
stability across the Taiwan Strait.  The Presidents also reiterated
their steadfast support for Ukraine in the face of Russia’s brutal
aggression.
