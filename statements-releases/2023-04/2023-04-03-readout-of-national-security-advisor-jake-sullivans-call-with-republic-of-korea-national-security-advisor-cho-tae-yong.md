National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan spoke with newly appointed
Republic of Korea (ROK) National Security Advisor Cho Tae-yong in an
introductory call today. Mr. Sullivan expressed appreciation for Mr.
Cho’s service to the Alliance during his most recent posting as the
ROK’s Ambassador to the United States. Mr. Sullivan conveyed his hope
that he and Mr. Cho will work closely together in the coming weeks and
months to further strengthen the ironclad U.S.-ROK Alliance, including
in support of President Yoon’s upcoming State Visit to the United States
in April.
