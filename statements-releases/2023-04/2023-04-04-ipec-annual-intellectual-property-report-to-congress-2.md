The Office of the Intellectual Property Enforcement Coordinator (IPEC)
has issued its [Annual Intellectual Property Report to
Congress](https://bidenwhitehouse.archives.gov/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/FY22-IPEC-Annual-Report_Final.pdf),
which brings together the combined and coordinated efforts of the White
House, the Departments of Agriculture, Commerce, Defense, Health and
Human Services, Homeland Security, State, and Treasury, the Office of
the U.S. Trade Representative, and the U.S. Copyright Office. The report
provides an overview of the intellectual property enforcement strategy
and related efforts undertaken by departments and agencies during fiscal
year 2022.

To view the Annual Intellectual Property Report to Congress, visit:
<https://bidenwhitehouse.archives.gov/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/FY22-IPEC-Annual-Report_Final.pdf>
