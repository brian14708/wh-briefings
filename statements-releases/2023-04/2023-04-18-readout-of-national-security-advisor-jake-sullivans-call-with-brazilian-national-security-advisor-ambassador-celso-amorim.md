National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan spoke today with National
Security Advisor Ambassador Celso Amorim of Brazil. They discussed a
number of bilateral and global issues, including Russia’s war against
Ukraine, continued cooperation to combat climate change and protect the
environment, the G20, and our shared efforts to safeguard democratic
institutions.
