In advance of the first round of Guatemalan general elections on June
25<sup>th</sup>, National Security Advisor to the Vice President Dr.
Phil Gordon hosted a virtual meeting today with a diverse group of civil
society organizations in Guatemala who are engaged in the country’s
electoral observation effort. Dr. Gordon reiterated U.S. support for
free, fair, inclusive and peaceful elections and a transparent electoral
process. Vice President Harris continues to lead the implementation of
the U.S. Strategy to Address the Root Causes of Migration in Central
America, which includes a focus on democratic governance and support for
civil society, and the Vice President remains committed to strengthening
these efforts throughout northern Central America.
