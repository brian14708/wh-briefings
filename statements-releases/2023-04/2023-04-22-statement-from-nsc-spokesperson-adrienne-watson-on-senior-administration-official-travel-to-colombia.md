Principal Deputy National Security Advisor Jon Finer will travel to
Colombia on April 25 to lead the U.S. delegation for the international
conference on Venezuela. He will be accompanied by Special Presidential
Advisor for the Americas Chris Dodd and National Security Council Senior
Director for Western Hemisphere Affairs Juan Gonzalez.
