President Joseph R. Biden Jr. spoke today with President Emmanuel Macron
of France.  The two leaders discussed President Macron’s upcoming travel
to the People’s Republic of China (PRC). They also reiterated their
steadfast support for Ukraine in the face of Russia’s ongoing
aggression.
