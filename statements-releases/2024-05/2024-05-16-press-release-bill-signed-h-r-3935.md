On Thursday, May 16, 2024, the President signed into law:  
   
   
H.R. 3935, the “FAA Reauthorization Act of 2024,” which reauthorizes the
Federal Aviation Administration and related revenue authorities through
September 30, 2028; and  reauthorizes the National Transportation Safety
Board through September 30, 2028.  
  
Thank you to Representatives Graves and Larsen, Senators Cantwell and
Cruz, and many others for their leadership.
