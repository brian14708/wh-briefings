  \ Jake Sullivan\u2019s Travel to Saudi Arabia and\_Israel"
---
 
National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan will travel to Saudi Arabia on
Saturday, May 18 to meet with Prime Minister and Crown Prince Mohammed
bin Salman to discuss bilateral and regional matters, including the war
in Gaza and ongoing efforts to achieve a lasting peace and security in
the region.

On Sunday, May 19, National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan will travel
to Israel to meet with senior Israeli officials, including Prime
Minister Netanyahu, to discuss the war in Gaza, ongoing negotiations to
secure the release of all the hostages, the humanitarian crisis, and our
shared objective for the enduring defeat of Hamas through both military
pressure and a political plan.
