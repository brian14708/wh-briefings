Assistant to the President and Principal Deputy National Security
Advisor Jon Finer met with the United Nations High Commissioner for
Refugees, Filippo Grandi, on Friday. Mr. Finer reaffirmed the strong
U.S. commitment to refugees and displaced people around the world, and
thanked the High Commissioner for his continued partnership. Mr. Finer
and the High Commissioner discussed our shared efforts to address
humanitarian and displacement crises, particularly those related to
Sudan, Afghanistan, Syria, and Haiti. They also welcomed recent progress
towards strengthening refugee self-reliance, such as the Shirika Plan in
Kenya. Noting the success of the Los Angeles Declaration Ministerial
held in Guatemala, Mr. Finer and the High Commissioner discussed our
strengthened collaboration in promoting regional cooperation to protect
refugees and integrate migrants in the Western Hemisphere and advancing
the Safe Mobility Office Initiative.
