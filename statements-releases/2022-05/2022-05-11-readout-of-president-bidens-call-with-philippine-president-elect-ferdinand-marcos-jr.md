President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with President-elect
Ferdinand Marcos Jr. of the Philippines to congratulate him on his
election. President Biden underscored that he looks forward to working
with the President-elect to continue strengthening the U.S.-Philippine
Alliance, while expanding bilateral cooperation on a wide range of
issues, including the fight against COVID-19, addressing the climate
crisis, promoting broad-based economic growth, and respect for human
rights.
