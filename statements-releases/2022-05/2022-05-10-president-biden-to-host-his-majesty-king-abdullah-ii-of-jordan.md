title: "President\_Biden to Host His Majesty King Abdullah II of\_Jordan"
---
 
The President is pleased to welcome His Majesty King Abdullah II of
Jordan and his Royal Highness Crown Prince Hussein to the White House on
May 13, 2022. King Abdullah’s visit to Washington, his second of the
Biden Administration, will reinforce the close friendship and enduring
partnership between the United States and Jordan. Jordan is a critical
force for stability in the Middle East and strategic partner and ally of
the United States.
