National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met today with Oscar Stenström,
State Secretary for Foreign Affairs to the Prime Minister of Sweden. Mr.
Sullivan and Mr. Stenström discussed the security situation in Europe in
light of Russia’s aggression in Ukraine, including their ongoing efforts
to support Ukraine and impose costs on Russia. They committed to
continue their close coordination on the full range of security issues.
