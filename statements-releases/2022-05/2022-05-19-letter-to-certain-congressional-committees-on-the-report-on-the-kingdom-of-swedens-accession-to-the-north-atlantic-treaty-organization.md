Dear Mr. Chairman:  (Dear Madam Chair:)

I am providing the report on the Kingdom of Sweden’s Accession to the
North Atlantic Treaty Organization, prepared consistent with section
3(2)(E) of the Resolution of Advice and Consent to Ratification of the
Protocols to the North Atlantic Treaty of 1949 on the Accession of
Poland, Hungary, and the Czech Republic of April 30, 1998.

                               Sincerely,

                               JOSEPH R. BIDEN JR.
