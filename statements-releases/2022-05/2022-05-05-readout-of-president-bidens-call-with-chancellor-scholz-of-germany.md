---
 
President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with Chancellor Olaf Scholz
of Germany.  The leaders underscored their commitment to continue
holding Russia accountable for its brutal actions in Ukraine, and
reviewed their ongoing efforts to provide security assistance to the
government of Ukraine and humanitarian aid to the millions of Ukrainians
affected by the violence. The President welcomed Germany’s provision of
security assistance and recent pledge of additional humanitarian aid, as
well as their close partnership throughout this crisis.
