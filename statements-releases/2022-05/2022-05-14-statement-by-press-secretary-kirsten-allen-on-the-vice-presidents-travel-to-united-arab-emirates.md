Vice President Kamala Harris will travel to Abu Dhabi, United Arab
Emirates on May 16 to lead a Presidential Delegation and offer
condolences on behalf of the Biden-Harris Administration and the
American people on the passing of His Highness Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed
Al Nahyan. She will meet with His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed Al
Nahyan, President of the United Arab Emirates. In addition to honoring
the memory and the legacy of Sheikh Khalifa, the Vice President will
underscore the strength of the partnership between our countries and our
desire to further deepen our ties in the coming months and years. Â
