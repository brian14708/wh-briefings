National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan spoke by phone today with
Republic of Korea (ROK) National Security Advisor Kim Sung-han to
discuss the  Democratic People’s Republic of Korea (DPRK) recent launch
of three ballistic missiles. They both condemned the DPRK’s
destabilizing ballistic missile tests and committed to continue building
on their close coordination. Mr. Sullivan also reaffirmed the United
States’ steadfast commitment to the defense of the ROK.
