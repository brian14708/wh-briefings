title: "Readout of Vice President Harris\u2019s Call with President Castro of\_Honduras"
---
 
Vice President Kamala Harris today spoke with President Xiomara Castro
of Honduras.  President Castro updated the Vice President on actions she
has taken to advance Honduras’ democracy and economy since the Vice
President attended her historic inauguration in January 2022.  The Vice
President and President Castro discussed deepening cooperation between
the United States and Honduras across a broad range of issues, including
expanding economic opportunity and addressing the root causes of
migration.  The Vice President underscored the United States’ commitment
to advancing our shared interests together and reiterated her strong
support for the partnership between the United States and Honduras.
