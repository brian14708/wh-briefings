On Thursday, President Biden will welcome Prime Minister Magdalena
Andersson of Sweden and President Sauli Niinistö of Finland to the White
House.  The leaders will discuss Finland’s and Sweden’s NATO
applications and European security, as well as strengthening our close
partnerships across a range of global issues and support for Ukraine.
