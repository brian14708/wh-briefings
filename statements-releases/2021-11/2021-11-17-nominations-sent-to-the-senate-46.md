NOMINATIONS SENT TO THE SENATE:

     Laura Farnsworth Dogu, of Texas, a Career Member of the Senior
Foreign Service, Class of Career Minister, to be Ambassador
Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the United States of America to the
Republic of Honduras.  
  
     Michael F. Gerber, of Pennsylvania, to be a Member of the Federal
Retirement Thrift Investment Board for a term expiring September 25,
2022, vice Michael D. Kennedy, term expired.  
  
     Michael F. Gerber, of Pennsylvania, to be a Member of the Federal
Retirement Thrift Investment Board for a term expiring September 25,
2026.  (Reappointment)  
  
     N. Nickolas Perry, of New York, to be Ambassador Extraordinary and
Plenipotentiary of the United States of America to Jamaica.  
  
     Kenneth L. Wainstein, of Virginia, to be Under Secretary for
Intelligence and Analysis, Department of Homeland Security, vice David
James Glawe.
