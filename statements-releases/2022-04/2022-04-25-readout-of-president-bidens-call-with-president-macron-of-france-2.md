President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with President Emmanuel
Macron of France to congratulate him on his re-election.  He underscored
the close and enduring relations between the United States and France,
our oldest ally, based on shared democratic values, economic ties, and
security cooperation. President Biden conveyed his readiness to continue
working closely with President Macron on our shared global priorities.
