  \ Jake Sullivan\u2019s meeting with Prime Minister Shmyhal of\_Ukraine"
---
 
National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met with Prime Minister Denys
Shmyhal of Ukraine this afternoon. Mr. Sullivan conveyed the United
States’ unwavering commitment to supporting the government and people of
Ukraine. The two discussed the security, economic, and humanitarian
assistance the United States is providing, including the new support
announced by President Biden yesterday, and ongoing efforts with
international partners to impose further costs on Russia for its
unprovoked aggression.
