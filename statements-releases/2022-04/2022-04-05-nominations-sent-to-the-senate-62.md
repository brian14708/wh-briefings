NOMINATIONS SENT TO THE SENATE:

     Robert F. Godec, of Virginia, a Career Member of the Senior Foreign
Service, Class of Career Minister, to be Ambassador Extraordinary and
Plenipotentiary of the United States of America to the Kingdom of
Thailand.

     Kalpana Kotagal, of Ohio, to be a Member of the Equal Employment
Opportunity Commission for a term expiring July 1, 2027, vice Janet
Dhillon, term expiring.

     Vinay Vijay Singh, of Pennsylvania, to be Chief Financial Officer,
Department of Housing and Urban Development, vice Irving Dennis.
