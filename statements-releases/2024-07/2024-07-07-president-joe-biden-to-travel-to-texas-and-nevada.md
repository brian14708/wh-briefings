---
 
From Monday, July 15<sup>th</sup>, to Wednesday, July 17<sup>th</sup>,
the President will travel to Texas and Nevada.

On Monday, July 15<sup>th</sup>, the President will travel to Austin,
Texas. The President will commemorate the 60<sup>th</sup> Anniversary of
the Civil Rights Act during a visit to the LBJ Presidential Library.  
  
On Monday, July 15<sup>th</sup>, the President will also travel to Las
Vegas, Nevada.

On Tuesday, July 16<sup>th</sup>, the President will deliver remarks
during the 115<sup>th</sup> NAACP National Convention and discuss the
Biden-Harris Administration’s commitment to advancing racial justice and
equity for all Americans, including Black Americans.

On Wednesday, July 17<sup>th</sup>, the President will deliver remarks
at the UnidosUS Annual Conference on the Biden-Harris Administration’s
historic accomplishments including lowering prescription drug costs for
America’s seniors, lowering the Latino uninsured rate, and creating a
Latino small business boom.

  
Additional details to follow.
