President Biden was briefed today by senior White House staff on
artificial intelligence (AI). The President and his team discussed
national security issues related to AI and AI research and development
to achieve a better future for all. The President also evaluated the
progress in implementing his Executive Order on Safe, Secure, and
Trustworthy AI. The President, Vice President, and the entire
Biden-Harris Administration continue taking action to ensure that
America leads the way in seizing the promise and managing the risks of
AI.
