Principal Deputy National Security Advisor Jon Finer met today with
Haitian Prime Minister Garry Conille. Mr. Finer reaffirmed the United
States’ unwavering support for the people of Haiti, who have suffered at
the hands of gangs for too long, with nearly five million people now in
need of emergency food aid. Mr. Finer and Mr. Conille discussed the
initial deployment of the United Nations-authorized Multinational
Security Support (MSS) mission to Haiti.  Mr. Finer reiterated the
United States’ strong support for the integration of robust
accountability and oversight mechanisms into the mission. The two
committed to continued close cooperation in support of improved
security, economic growth, and democratic governance in Haiti.
