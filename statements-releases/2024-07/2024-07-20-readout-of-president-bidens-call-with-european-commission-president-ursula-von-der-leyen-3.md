President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with European Commission
President Ursula von der Leyen to congratulate her on her
re-election. The leaders reaffirmed their commitment to a strong U.S.-EU
relationship and to tackling the world’s biggest challenges together.
 They also discussed support for Ukraine and efforts to hold Russia to
account for its aggression.
