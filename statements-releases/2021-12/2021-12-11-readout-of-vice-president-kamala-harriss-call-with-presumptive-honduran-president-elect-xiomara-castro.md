  \ President-Elect Xiomara\_Castro"

Yesterday, Vice President Kamala Harris called and congratulated Xiomara
Castro on her historic victory as Honduras’ first female president. 
They discussed their shared interest in addressing the root causes of
migration, including by increasing economic opportunity, combatting
corruption, addressing security threats, and improving access to health
and education.  They committed to working together and deepening the
partnership between the United States and Honduras.
