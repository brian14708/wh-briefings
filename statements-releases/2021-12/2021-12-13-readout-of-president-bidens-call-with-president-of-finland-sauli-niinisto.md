President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with President of Finland
Sauli Niinistö. They discussed their shared concerns about Russia’s
destabilizing military buildup along Ukraine’s border and the importance
of transatlantic efforts to de-escalate the situation. President Biden
welcomed Finland’s selection of the F-35 for its next fighter jet,
noting this procurement would provide a strong foundation for even
closer bilateral defense ties for years to come.
