President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. today announced the designation of a
Presidential Delegation to attend the Inauguration of His Excellency
Recep Tayyip Erdoğan on Saturday, June 3, 2023.  
   
T.H. Jeffry L. Flake, United States Ambassador to the Republic of
Türkiye, will attend the Inauguration in Ankara, Türkiye.
