National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met with the Chairman of the
Communist Party of Vietnam’s Central External Relations Commission, Le
Hoai Trung at the White House on June 29, 2023. They reaffirmed the
strength of bilateral relations between the two countries on this
10-year anniversary of the U.S. – Vietnam Comprehensive Partnership, and
discussed a wide range of areas for future cooperation, including on
energy, climate change, investment, education, human rights, and efforts
to uphold peace and stability in the Indo-Pacific. Mr. Sullivan and
Chairman Trung shared the view that the U.S.-Vietnam relationship is a
powerful example of reconciliation from the past with the continued
potential to drive greater prosperity, stability, and peace in the
future. They look forward to further deepening U.S.-Vietnam ties going
forward.
