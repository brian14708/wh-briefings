Principal Deputy National Security Advisor Jon Finer met today
with Maldives Minister of Foreign Affairs Abdulla Shahid to discuss the
growth in U.S.-Maldives relations and explore areas where our countries
can increase cooperation.  Mr. Finer congratulated Maldives on
officially opening their embassy in Washington, D.C. on Friday and
expressed the United States’ desire to open its embassy in Malé soon. 
The two discussed Maldives’ commitment to ensuring its democracy is
delivering results for the Maldivian people and our shared resolve in
tackling the climate crisis. They committed to continue cooperation
between the United States and Maldives on a wide range of issues to
ensure a free, open, peaceful, and stable Indian Ocean region including
security cooperation, climate change, and sustainable development.
