President Biden spoke today with President Emmanuel Macron of France,
Chancellor Olaf Scholz of Germany, and Prime Minister Rishi Sunak of the
United Kingdom. The leaders discussed the situation in Russia.  They
also affirmed their unwavering support for Ukraine**.**
