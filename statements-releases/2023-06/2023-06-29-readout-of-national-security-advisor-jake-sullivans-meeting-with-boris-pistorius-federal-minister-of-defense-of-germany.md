National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met yesterday with German
Federal Minister of Defense Boris Pistorius to discuss a range of global
security issues. They reaffirmed their countries’ commitment to
supporting Ukraine for as long as it takes, including through the
continued provision of security assistance.  They reviewed preparations
for the upcoming NATO Summit in Vilnius.  They also discussed efforts to
expand partnerships in the Indo-Pacific, noting the importance of
bolstering global resilience and countering the challenges posed by the
People’s Republic of China.
