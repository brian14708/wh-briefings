President Joe Biden spoke today with Prime Minister Giorgia Meloni of
Italy as part of his close coordination with key Allies and
partners following recent events in Russia. The leaders affirmed their
unwavering support for Ukraine. They also coordinated on preparations
ahead of the upcoming NATO Summit and discussed recent developments in
North Africa. President Biden invited Prime Minister Meloni to visit the
White House in July.
