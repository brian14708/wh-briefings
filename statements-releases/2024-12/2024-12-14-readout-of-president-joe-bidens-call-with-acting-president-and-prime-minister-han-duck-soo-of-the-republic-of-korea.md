President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with Acting President and
Prime Minister Han Duck-soo of the Republic of Korea (ROK). President
Biden expressed his appreciation for the resiliency of democracy and the
rule of law in the ROK and reaffirmed the ironclad commitment of the
United States to the people of the ROK. The two leaders discussed the
tremendous progress we have made toward further strengthening the
U.S.-ROK Alliance in recent years, and President Biden expressed his
confidence that the Alliance will remain the linchpin for peace and
prosperity in the Indo-Pacific region during Acting President Han’s
tenure.
