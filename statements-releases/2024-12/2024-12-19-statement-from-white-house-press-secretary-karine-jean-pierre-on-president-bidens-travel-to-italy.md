President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. will travel to Rome, Italy from January 9
to 12 to meet separately with His Holiness Pope Francis, President of
Italy Sergio Mattarella, and Prime Minister of Italy Giorgia Meloni.
On January 10, President Biden will have an audience with the Pope and
discuss efforts to advance peace around the world. He will also meet
with Italy’s leaders to highlight the strength of the U.S.-Italy
relationship, thank Prime Minister Meloni for her strong leadership of
the G7 over the past year, and discuss important challenges facing the
world.
