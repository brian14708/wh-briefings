title: "Readout of President Biden\u2019s Call with Prime Minister Johnson of the\
  \ United Kingdom"
---
 
President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with Prime Minister Boris
Johnson of the United Kingdom. The leaders discussed their recent
diplomatic engagements with Ukraine and Russia. They also reviewed
ongoing diplomatic and deterrence efforts in response to Russia’s
continued military build-up on Ukraine’s borders and reaffirmed their
support for Ukraine’s sovereignty and territorial integrity. They
discussed efforts to reinforce the defensive posture on NATO’s eastern
flank and underlined the continued close coordination among Allies and
partners, including on readiness to impose severe consequences on Russia
should it choose further military escalation.
