title: "President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Amends Puerto Rico Disaster\_Declaration"
---
 
Yesterday, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. made additional disaster
assistance available to the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico by authorizing
an increase in the level of Federal funding for Public Assistance
projects undertaken in the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico as a result of
earthquakes during the period of December 28, 2019, to July 3, 2020.  
  
Under the President’s major disaster declaration issued for the
Commonwealth of Puerto on January 16, 2020, Federal funding was made
available for Public Assistance, Hazard Mitigation, and Other Needs
Assistance at 75 percent of the total eligible costs.           
  
Under the President’s order today, the Federal share for Public
Assistance has been increased to 90 percent of the total eligible
costs.
