President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke with President Emmanuel Macron of
France and Chancellor Olaf Scholz of Germany today about Russia’s
ongoing escalation along the borders of Ukraine. The leaders strongly
condemned President Putin’s decision to recognize the so-called DNR and
LNR regions of Ukraine as “independent.” They discussed how they will
continue to coordinate their response on next steps.
