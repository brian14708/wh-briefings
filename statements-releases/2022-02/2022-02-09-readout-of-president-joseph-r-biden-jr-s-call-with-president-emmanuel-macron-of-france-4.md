President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with President Emmanuel
Macron of France.  They discussed President Macron’s recent meetings in
Russia and Ukraine. They also talked about ongoing diplomatic and
deterrence efforts, undertaken in close coordination with our Allies and
partners, in response to Russia’s continued military build-up on
Ukraine’s borders.
