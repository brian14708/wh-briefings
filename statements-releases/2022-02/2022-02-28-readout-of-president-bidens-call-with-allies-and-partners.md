President Joseph R. Biden hosted a secure conference call today with
Allies and partners about our unified response to Russia’s unjustified
and unprovoked war on Ukraine. The leaders recognized the bravery of the
Ukrainian people in the face of Russian aggression and
discussed their continued support to Ukraine, including security,
economic, and humanitarian assistance. They also discussed their
coordinated efforts to impose severe costs and consequences to hold
Russia accountable while working to maintain global economic stability,
including with regard to energy prices. 

Joining President Biden on the call were Prime Minister Trudeau of
Canada, European Commission President von der Leyen, European Council
President Michel, President Macron of France, Chancellor Scholz of
Germany, Prime Minister Draghi of Italy, Prime Minister Kishida of
Japan, NATO Secretary General Stoltenberg, President Duda of Poland,
President Iohannis of Romania, and Prime Minister Johnson of the United
Kingdom.
