title: "Readout of President Biden\u2019s Call with Chancellor Olaf Scholz of Germany"
---
 
President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with Chancellor Olaf Scholz
of Germany. The leaders discussed their respective conversations with
President Putin and President Zelenskyy regarding Russia’s continued
military build-up on Ukraine’s borders. They reaffirmed their commitment
to Ukraine’s sovereignty and territorial integrity and underscored the
importance of continued transatlantic coordination on diplomacy and
deterrence measures and the reinforcement of NATO’s eastern flank if
Russia further invades Ukraine.
