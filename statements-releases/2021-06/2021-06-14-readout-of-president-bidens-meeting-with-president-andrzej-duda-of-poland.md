President Biden spoke today on the margins of the NATO Summit with
President Andrzej Duda of Poland. The President reiterated his support
for NATO’s strengthened defense and deterrence agenda and his resolute
commitment to the defense of Allies on NATO’s eastern flank, including
Poland. He discussed his plans for the upcoming summit with President
Putin.
