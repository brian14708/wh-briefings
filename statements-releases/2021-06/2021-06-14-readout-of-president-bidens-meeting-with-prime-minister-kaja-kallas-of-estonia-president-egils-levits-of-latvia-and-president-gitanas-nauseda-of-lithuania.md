President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. met today with Prime Minister Kaja Kallas
of Estonia, President Egils Levits of Latvia, and President Gitanas
Nauseda of Lithuania on the margins of the NATO Summit in Brussels. The
President underscored strong U.S. support for the security of Estonia,
Latvia, and Lithuania, as well as the entire Baltic region. The four
leaders committed to further strengthening our political, military, and
economic partnerships, including working together through NATO to
address challenges posed by Russia and China.
