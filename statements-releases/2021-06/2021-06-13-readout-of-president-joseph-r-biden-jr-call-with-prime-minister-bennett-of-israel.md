title: "Readout of President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Call with Prime Minister Bennett\
  \ of\_Israel"
---
 
President Biden spoke today with Prime Minister Bennett to offer his
warm congratulations to Prime Minister Bennett on becoming Prime
Minister of the State of Israel.  President Biden highlighted his
decades of steadfast support for the U.S.-Israel relationship and his
unwavering commitment to Israel’s security.  He expressed his firm
intent to deepen cooperation between the United States and Israel on the
many challenges and opportunities facing the region.  The leaders agreed
that they and their teams would consult closely on all matters related
to regional security, including Iran.  The President also conveyed that
his administration intends to work closely with the Israeli government
on efforts to advance peace, security, and prosperity for Israelis and
Palestinians.
