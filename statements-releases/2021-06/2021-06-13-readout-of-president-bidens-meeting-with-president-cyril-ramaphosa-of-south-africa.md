title: "Readout of President Biden\u2019s Meeting with President Cyril Ramaphosa of\
  \ South Africa"
---
 
President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. yesterday met with President Cyril
Ramaphosa of South Africa on the margins of the G7 Summit. They
discussed the U.S.-South Africa bilateral relationship and underscored
the need to expand global COVID-19 vaccine supply and to cooperate on
climate goals in Africa and on other bilateral and regional issues.
