title: Readout of President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Call with President Emmanuel Macron
  of France
---
 
President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with President Emmanuel
Macron of France to express his desire to strengthen bilateral ties with
our oldest ally. President Biden also stressed his commitment to
bolstering the transatlantic relationship, including through NATO and
the United States’ partnership with the European Union. The leaders
agreed on the need for close coordination, including through
multilateral organizations, in tackling common challenges such as
climate change, COVID-19, and the global economic recovery.  They also
agreed to work together on shared foreign policy priorities, including
China, the Middle East, Russia, and the Sahel.
