National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan spoke by phone today with
Republic of Korea National Security Advisor Suh Hoon. Mr. Sullivan
stressed the Biden administration’s commitment to further strengthening
the U.S.-ROK alliance. They discussed the importance of close
coordination on the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea, as well as on
other regional and global challenges including COVID-19 and combating
climate change.
