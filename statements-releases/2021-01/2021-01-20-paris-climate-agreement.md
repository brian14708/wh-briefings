ACCEPTANCE ON BEHALF OF THE UNITED STATES OF AMERICA

I, Joseph R. Biden Jr., President of the United States of America,
having seen and considered the Paris Agreement, done at Paris on
December 12, 2015, do hereby accept the said Agreement and every article
and clause thereof on behalf of the United States of America.

Done at Washington this 20th day of January, 2021.

JOSEPH R. BIDEN JR.
