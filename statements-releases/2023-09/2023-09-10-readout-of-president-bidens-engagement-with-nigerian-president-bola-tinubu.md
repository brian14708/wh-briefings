President Joe Biden met with Nigerian President Bola Tinubu today on the
sidelines of the G20 in New Delhi, India to reinforce our enduring
commitment to the U.S.-Nigeria relationship and to the longstanding
friendship between our two countries and peoples. President Biden
welcomed the Tinubu Administration’s steps to reform Nigeria’s
economy and thanked President Tinubu for his strong leadership as the
chair of the Economic Community of West African States to defend and
preserve democracy and the rule of law in Niger and the broader region.
Nigeria’s invitation to the G20 Summit is a recognition of Nigeria’s
important global role as Africa’s largest democracy and economy
