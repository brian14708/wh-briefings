National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met today with Prime Minister
and Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. They
discussed a number of bilateral and regional matters, including ongoing
efforts to create new conditions for an enduring and sustainable peace
between Israelis and Palestinians. They also discussed the humanitarian
response in Gaza, including efforts to increase the flow of critical
aid. Finally, they discussed areas of deepening bilateral cooperation in
the fields of security, commerce, space exploration, and advanced
