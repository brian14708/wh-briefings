  \ Pendergrass and FEMA Administrator Deanne\_Criswell"
---
 
This morning, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke with Commissioner of
Lee County, Florida Cecil Pendergrass. FEMA Administrator Deanne
Criswell joined them on the call. They discussed ways the Biden-Harris
Administration can support Lee County in the immediate aftermath of
Hurricane Ian, including meeting immediate needs for search and rescue
efforts and power restoration as well as helping to address challenges
faced by local hospitals and problems presented by damage to roads and
bridges. The President reassured the Commissioner that the
Administration would provide all the resources at its disposal. The
Commissioner thanked the President for the support that has been
provided thus far.
