National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met today at the White House
with Indian External Affairs Minister Dr. S. Jaishankar to review
progress in the U.S.-India strategic partnership and exchange views on
global and regional priorities, including the implications of Russia’s
aggression against Ukraine and its impact on food and energy insecurity
around the world. Looking forward to India’s G20 presidency, they also
discussed approaches to promoting debt sustainability and advancing a
free, open, secure, and prosperous Indo-Pacific.
