The President spoke this morning with Governor Ron DeSantis of Florida
to discuss the steps the Biden-Harris Administration is taking to
support Florida in response to Hurricane Ian, including the issuance of
a Disaster Declaration this morning. The President told the Governor he
is sending his FEMA Administrator to Florida tomorrow to check in on
response efforts and see where additional support is needed. The
President and Governor committed to continued close coordination.
