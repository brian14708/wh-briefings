President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. will speak with President Xi Jinping of
the People’s Republic of China (PRC) this Friday. This is part of our
ongoing efforts to maintain open lines of communication between the
United States and the PRC. The two Leaders will discuss managing the
competition between our two countries as well as Russia’s war against
Ukraine and other issues of mutual concern.
