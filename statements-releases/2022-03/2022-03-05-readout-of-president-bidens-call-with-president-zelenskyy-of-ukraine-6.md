President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke this evening with President
Volodymyr Zelenskyy of Ukraine.  President Biden highlighted the ongoing
actions undertaken by the United States, its Allies and partners, and
private industry to raise the costs on Russia for its aggression in
Ukraine.  In particular, he welcomed the decision this evening by Visa
and Mastercard to suspend service in Russia. President Biden noted his
administration is surging security, humanitarian, and economic
assistance to Ukraine and is working closely with Congress to secure
additional funding.  President Biden reiterated his concern about the
recent Russian attack on a Ukrainian nuclear power plant, and he
commended the skill and bravery of the Ukrainian operators who have kept
the reactors in safe condition.  The leaders also discussed the recent
talks between Russia and Ukraine.
