title: "President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Amends Alaska Disaster\_Declaration"
---
 
Yesterday, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. made additional disaster
assistance available to the State of Alaska by authorizing an increase
in the level of Federal funding for Public Assistance projects
undertaken in the State of Alaska as a result of an earthquake on
November 30, 2018. 

Under the major disaster declaration issued for the State of Alaska on
January 31, 2019, Federal funding was made available for Public
Assistance, Hazard Mitigation, and Other Needs Assistance at 75 percent
of the total eligible costs.         

Under the President’s order yesterday, the Federal share for Public
Assistance has been increased to 90 percent of the total eligible costs.
