During the week of March 14, Assistant to the President for Homeland
Security Dr. Liz Sherwood-Randall led an interagency delegation to
Paris, France, with representatives from the Department of Defense, the
Department of State, and the National Security Council.  They met with
the Chief of the Military Staff of the President of the Republic Admiral
Jean-Philippe Rolland and separately with National Intelligence and
Counter-Terrorism Coordinator Laurent Nuñez to discuss global terrorism
trends, counterterrorism cooperation between the United States and
France, and other vital national security interests.
