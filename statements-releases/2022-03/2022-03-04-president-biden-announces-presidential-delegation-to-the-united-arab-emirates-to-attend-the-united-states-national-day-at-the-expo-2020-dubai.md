FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  
March 4, 2022

**President Biden Announces Presidential Delegation to the United Arab
Emirates to Attend the United States National Day at the Expo 2020
Dubai **

President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. today announced the designation of a
Presidential Delegation to the United Arab Emirates to attend the United
States National Day at Expo 2020 Dubai on March 6, 2022.    
  
The Honorable Gina M. Raimondo, Secretary of Commerce, will lead the
delegation.  
  
**<u>Members of the Presidential Delegation:</u>**  
  
Mr. Sean Murphy, Chargé d’Affaires ad interim, U.S. Embassy Abu Dhabi  
  
The Honorable Roy Blunt, United States Senator (R), Missouri  
  
Ms. Jennifer Hall Godfrey, Senior Official for Public Diplomacy and
Public of Affairs, U.S. Department of State  
  
The Honorable Maria Rosario Jackson, Ph.D., Chair of the National
Endowment for the Arts  
  
The Honorable Robert Clark, U.S. Commissioner General at Expo 2020
Dubai  
  
Mr. Dilawar Syed, Special Representative for Commercial and Business
Affairs,   
U.S. Department of State  
  
The Honorable Barbara Leaf, Special Assistant to the President and
Senior Director for the Middle East and North Africa, National Security
Council  
  
Ms. Meghan Gregonis, Consul General, U.S. Consulate Dubai
