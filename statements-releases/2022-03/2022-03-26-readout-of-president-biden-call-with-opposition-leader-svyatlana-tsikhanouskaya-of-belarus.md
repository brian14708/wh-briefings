  \ of\_Belarus"

President Joseph R. Biden, Jr., spoke with democratic opposition leader
Svyatlana Tsikhanouskaya of Belarus from Air Force One. He thanked her
for attending his speech in Warsaw tonight. The President underscored
the continued support of the United States for the Belarusian people in
defending and advancing human rights, including freedom of expression,
and free and fair elections.
