President Joe Biden and President Pedro Sánchez of Spain spoke today
about the latest developments in Afghanistan. They praised the
dedication of their personnel in Kabul who are working together to
safely evacuate their citizens, Afghans who courageously supported the
work of the EU mission, and other vulnerable Afghan nationals. President
Biden praised Spain’s leadership in rallying international support for
Afghan women and girls. He also thanked President Sánchez for Spain’s
assistance in temporarily housing Afghans at risk who are bound for the
United States at military bases in Rota and Moron.
