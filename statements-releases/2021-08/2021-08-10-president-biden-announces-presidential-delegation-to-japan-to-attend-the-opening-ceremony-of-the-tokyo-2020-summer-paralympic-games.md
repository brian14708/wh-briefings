President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. today announced the designation of a
Presidential Delegation to Japan to attend events of the Tokyo 2020
Summer Paralympic Games and the Opening Ceremony on Tuesday, August 24,
2021.

Mr. Douglas Emhoff, Second Gentleman of the United States, will lead the
delegation.

<span style="text-decoration: underline">Member of the Presidential
Delegation:</span>

Mr. Raymond Greene, Chargé d’Affaires ad interim, U.S. Embassy Tokyo
