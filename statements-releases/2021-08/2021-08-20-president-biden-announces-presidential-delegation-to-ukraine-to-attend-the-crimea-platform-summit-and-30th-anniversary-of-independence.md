  \ Crimea Platform Summit and 30th Anniversary of\_Independence"

President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. today announced the designation of a
Presidential Delegation to Kyiv, Ukraine to attend the Crimea Platform
Summit and events of the 30<sup>th</sup> Anniversary of Independence on
August 23-24, 2021.  
  
The Honorable Jennifer M. Granholm, Secretary of Energy, will lead the
delegation.  
  
<span style="text-decoration: underline">Members of the Presidential
Delegation:</span>  
  
Ms. Kristina Kvien, Chargé d’Affaires ad interim, U.S. Embassy Kyiv

Dr. Andrew Light, Assistant Secretary for International Affairs, U.S.
Department of Energy
