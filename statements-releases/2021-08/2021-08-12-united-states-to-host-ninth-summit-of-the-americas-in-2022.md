The United States is preparing to host the Ninth Summit of the Americas
in early Summer 2022.  The Summit has taken place approximately once
every three years since 1994 and is the only meeting of all leaders from
the countries of North, South, and Central America and the Caribbean. 
This will be the first time the United States has hosted the Summit
since the inaugural meeting in Miami, in 1994. Additional details to
follow.
