National Security Adviser Jake Sullivan and Chief of Staff Ron Klain
have briefed the President on the rocket attack at Hamid Karzai
International Airport. The President was informed that operations
continue uninterrupted at HKIA, and has reconfirmed his order that
commanders redouble their efforts to prioritize doing whatever is
necessary to protect our forces on the ground.
