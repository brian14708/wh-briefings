---
 
President Biden spoke today with President Macron of France on a number
of global and bilateral matters. They reviewed developments in Ukraine
as well as in the Middle East, to include efforts to secure a ceasefire
deal in Lebanon that will allow residents on both sides of the Blue Line
to return safely to their homes. They committed to remain in close
consultation directly and through their national security teams.
