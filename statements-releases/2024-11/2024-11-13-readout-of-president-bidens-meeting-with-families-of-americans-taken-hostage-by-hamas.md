President Joe Biden met this afternoon with families of Americans taken
hostage by Hamas. The President provided an update on U.S. efforts to
secure the release of all hostages, and reaffirmed the Biden-Harris
Administration’s commitment to getting the hostages home to their loved
ones and families. The Administration has worked tirelessly to get a
deal done as soon as possible to bring the hostages home, and the
President also assured the families those efforts will continue.
