President Joseph R. Biden Jr. will meet with President Xi Jinping of the
People’s Republic of China (PRC) in Lima, Peru on November 16.  This
will be the third meeting between the two leaders, building on the
November 2023 Woodside Summit in California and their November 2022
meeting in Bali, Indonesia. President Biden and President Xi will
discuss bilateral, regional, and global issues.
