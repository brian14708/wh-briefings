National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan spoke by phone today with
Stephen Lovegrove, incoming National Security Advisor to the Prime
Minister of the United Kingdom. Mr. Sullivan expressed support for the
United Kingdom’s G7 presidency and welcomed the virtual leaders’ summit
later this week. They agreed on the need for continued coordination on
shared foreign policy priorities, including Afghanistan, Burma, China,
and Iran.
