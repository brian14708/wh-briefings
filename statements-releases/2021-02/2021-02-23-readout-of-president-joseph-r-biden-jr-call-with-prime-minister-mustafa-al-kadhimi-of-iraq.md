title: Readout of President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Call With Prime Minister Mustafa
  Al-Kadhimi of Iraq
---
 
President Joseph R. Biden spoke today with Prime Minister Mustafa
Al-Kadhimi of Iraq. The President affirmed U.S. support for Iraq’s
sovereignty and independence and commended the Prime Minister’s
leadership. They discussed the recent rocket attacks against Iraqi and
Coalition personnel and agreed that those responsible for such attacks
must be held fully to account. Together, they discussed the importance
of advancing the Strategic Dialogue between our countries and expanding
bilateral cooperation on other key issues. The President agreed to stay
in close touch with the Prime Minister over the coming days and weeks.
