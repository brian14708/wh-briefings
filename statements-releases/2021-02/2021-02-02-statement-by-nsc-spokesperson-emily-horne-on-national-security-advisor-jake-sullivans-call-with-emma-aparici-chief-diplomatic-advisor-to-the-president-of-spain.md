National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan spoke by phone today with Emma
Aparici, Chief Diplomatic Advisor to the President of Spain. Mr.
Sullivan underscored the Biden administration’s commitment to
strengthening the U.S.-Spain relationship. He expressed appreciation for
Spain’s continued hosting of U.S. military forces. They agreed to work
together on shared foreign policy priorities, including China, Latin
America, and the Sahel. They also discussed the importance of
partnership on common global challenges, including the COVID-19 pandemic
and climate change.
