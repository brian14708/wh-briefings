National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan spoke by phone today with
Geoffrey van Leeuwen, Foreign Affairs and Defense Advisor to the Prime
Minister of the Netherlands.  Mr. Sullivan underscored the Biden
Administration’s commitment to further strengthening the
U.S.-Netherlands relationship and reinvigorating transatlantic ties.  He
expressed appreciation for long-standing contributions and sacrifices
made by Dutch troops in Iraq against ISIS and in NATO’s mission in
Afghanistan.  Mr. Sullivan and Mr. van Leeuwen discussed our close
cooperation on advanced technologies and cyber security.  They agreed to
work together on shared foreign policy priorities, including in regard
to China and Russia.  They also discussed the importance of partnership
on common global challenges, including climate change.
