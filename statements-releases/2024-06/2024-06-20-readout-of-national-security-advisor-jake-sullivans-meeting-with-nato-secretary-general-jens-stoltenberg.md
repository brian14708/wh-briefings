National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met today with NATO Secretary
General Jens Stoltenberg.  They discussed ongoing work ahead of the 75th
Anniversary Summit in Washington, DC next month, including strengthening
Allied resilience and further galvanizing transatlantic
defense industry.  They also underscored NATO’s unwavering
support for Ukraine.
