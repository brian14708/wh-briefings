title: "The White\_House Opens Volunteer Applications for the 2023 Holiday\_Season"
---
 
President Joe Biden and First Lady Jill Biden are excited to continue
the tradition of welcoming volunteer holiday decorators, Holiday Open
House volunteers, and volunteer holiday performers to help celebrate the
holiday season at the White House. Starting today, applications are now
open for those interested in volunteering for the 2023 holiday
season.   
  
The White House welcomes volunteer applicants from all U.S. states
and territories, and encourages military-connected family members,
educators, frontline workers, and students to apply. The deadline to
submit a volunteer application to be a holiday decorator, Holiday Open
House volunteer, or volunteer holiday performer will be on **Thursday,
September 7, 2023 at 5:00 P.M. ET.** Selected applicants will be
notified on or after **Thursday, September 28, 2023at 5:00 P.M. ET.**  
  
All selected volunteers are responsible for their individual travel and
personal expenses.   
  
**VOLUNTEER HOLIDAY DECORATOR APPLICATION**  
Those interested in volunteering to decorate the White House ahead of
the holiday season may
apply [HERE](https://events.whitehouse.gov/EventInterestHolidayVol?rid=CFKMK9DJ8B).
Please note each person must apply individually, and group applications
will not be accepted. Any individual applying to decorate at the White
House **must be 18 years of age or older to participate.** Volunteers
must be available to be in Washington, D.C. the week of **Sunday,
November 19, 2023.**  
  
**HOLIDAY OPEN HOUSE VOLUNTEER APPLICATION **  
Those interested in volunteering to assist with White House Holiday Open
Houses may
apply [HERE](https://46eop.my.site.com/Surveys/survey/runtimeApp.app?invitationId=0Ki3d0000006pkq&surveyName=X2023_white_house_holiday_open_house_volunteer_form&UUID=b1265a26-5991-4343-b4de-6fa46968ca80).
Any individual applying to volunteer for an open house at the White
House **must be 18 years of age or older to participate.** White House
Holiday Open Houses range from **Tuesday, December 5,
2023** to **Sunday,December 24, 2023**.   
  
**VOLUNTEER HOLIDAY PERFORMER APPLICATION**  
Performers and musical groups, including school bands, choirs, and
holiday-themed entertainers, may
apply [HERE](https://events.whitehouse.gov/EventInterestHolidayVol?rid=YQX462XWFW) for
the opportunity to perform during a White House Holiday Open House.
White House Holiday Open Houses range from **Tuesday, December 5,
2023** to **Sunday,December 24, 2023**.   
  
For any questions regarding application or process, please reach out
to <Volunteers@who.eop.gov>.
