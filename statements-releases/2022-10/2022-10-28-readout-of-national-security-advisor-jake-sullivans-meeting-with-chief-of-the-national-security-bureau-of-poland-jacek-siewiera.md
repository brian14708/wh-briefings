National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met today with Jacek Siewiera,
Chief of the National Security Bureau of Poland, to exchange views on a
range of shared global policy issues. They affirmed the commitment and
resolve of the United States and Poland to support Ukraine in its
defense against Russian aggression, and discussed the need to continue
to impose costs on Russia for its unjust and unlawful actions. They also
discussed the importance of continued NATO unity as the Alliance
implements deliverables agreed to at the Madrid Summit. In addition,
they affirmed the strong bilateral relationship between the United
States and Poland and discussed opportunities for expanded cooperation
in the defense and energy sectors.
