National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met today with Norwegian Foreign
Minister Anniken Huitfeldt.  They expressed their concern over Russia’s
recent missile strikes in Ukraine and the Kremlin’s illegitimate
attempts to annex Ukrainian territory. They discussed their continued
commitment to hold Russia accountable for its actions and provide
Ukraine with security assistance to defend itself, as well as the
importance of protecting critical energy infrastructure and ensuring
energy security in Europe. In addition, they compared views on regional
security issues in the Middle East and South America.
