President Joseph R. Biden, Jr., spoke today with President Volodymyr
Zelenskyy of Ukraine. He expressed his condemnation of Russia’s missile
strikes across Ukraine, including in Kyiv, and conveyed his condolences
to the loved ones of those killed and injured in these senseless
attacks.  President Biden pledged to continue providing Ukraine with the
support needed to defend itself, including advanced air defense
systems.  He also underscored his ongoing engagement with allies and
partners to continue imposing costs on Russia, holding Russia
accountable for its war crimes and atrocities, and providing Ukraine
with security, economic, and humanitarian assistance.
