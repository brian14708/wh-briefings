National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met today with Tomas Pojar,
National Security Advisor to the Prime Minister of the Czech Republic.
They expressed their strong commitment to continuing to support Ukraine
politically, militarily, and economically as it defends itself against
Russian aggression. They also underscored their continued readiness to
impose costs on Russia for its actions.  They welcomed the deepening
defense cooperation between the United States and the Czech
Republic, including through the development of a U.S.-Czech Defense
Cooperation Agreement.  In addition, they discussed energy security, the
Indo-Pacific, and the Czech Republic’s presidency of the Council of the
European Union.
