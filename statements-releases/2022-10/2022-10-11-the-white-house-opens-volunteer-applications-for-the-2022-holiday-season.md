title: "The White\_House Opens Volunteer Applications for the 2022 Holiday\_Season"
---
 
President Joe Biden and First Lady Jill Biden are excited to continue
the tradition of welcoming volunteer decorators, performers,
and greeters to help celebrate the holiday season at the White
House. Starting today, applications are now open for those interested in
volunteering. 

The White House welcomes submissions from all U.S. states
and territories, and encourages military family members, educators,
frontline workers, and students to apply. All volunteer applications
will close on **Wednesday, October 19<sup>th</sup>, 2022 at 5:00 P.M.
ET** and applicants will be notified by **on or after Monday, October
31, 2022 at 5:00 P.M. ET.**

**VOLUNTEER DECORATOR APPLICATION**

Those interested in volunteering to decorate the White House ahead of
the holiday season, you may
apply [HERE](https://events.whitehouse.gov/EventInterestHolidayVol?rid=CXGXKMRTCR).
Please note each person must apply individually as no group applications
will be accepted. Volunteers must be available to be in Washington,
D.C. the week of November 21st.

**VOLUNTEER ENTERTAINMENT APPLICATION**

Performers and musical groups, including school bands, choirs, and
Holiday-themed entertainers, you may
apply [HERE](https://events.whitehouse.gov/EventInterestHolidayVol?rid=KJVVRWPDJC) for
the opportunity to perform during a Holiday Open House Tour. Performers
must be available to be in Washington, D.C. during the window
of November 29th to December 23rd.

**HOLIDAY OPEN HOUSE VOLUNTEER APPLICATION **

Those interested in volunteering to welcome tour guests at a White House
Holiday Open House, you may
apply [HERE](https://46eop.force.com/Surveys/survey/runtimeApp.app?invitationId=0Kit0000001FIRZ&surveyName=X2022_white_house_holiday_open_house_volunteer_form&UUID=fc131a9f-c1be-4edc-ba71-ea01964582dd).
Volunteers must be available to be in Washington, D.C. during the window
of November 29th to December 23rd.

***COVID-19 GUIDANCE***

*The White House will continue to closely monitor the COVID-19 situation
based on recommendations from the CDC and other public health officials
and medical experts in order to maintain a safe, healthy environment for
all volunteers and entertainers for this year’s holiday
season. Furthermore, the White House reserves the right to adjust its
availability as necessary to adhere to the latest health guidance.*

*For any questions regarding application or process, please reach out
to <Volunteers@who.eop.gov>.*
