National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met today with Asta Skaisgiryte,
Chief Foreign Policy Advisor, and Kestutis Budrys, Chief National
Security Advisor, to President Gitanas Nauseda of Lithuania. They
discussed their shared commitment to continuing to provide Ukraine with
security assistance to defend its sovereignty and territorial integrity
and to maintain sanctions pressure to hold Russia accountable.  Mr.
Sullivan reiterated the ironclad commitment of the United States to
NATO’s collective defense. In addition, they discussed plans for the
2023 NATO Summit in Vilnius.
