National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met today in Washington with
Frédéric Bernard, Head of Cabinet to the President of the European
Council. They reaffirmed their commitment to continue supporting Ukraine
as it defends its democracy and imposing costs on Russia for its
aggression.  They also discussed ongoing U.S.-EU cooperation on Europe’s
energy security as well as the promotion of peace and stability in the
Western Balkans and the South Caucasus.
