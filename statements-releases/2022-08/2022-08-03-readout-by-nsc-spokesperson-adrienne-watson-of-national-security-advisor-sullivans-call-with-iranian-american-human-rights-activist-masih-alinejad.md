Today National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan spoke with Masih Alinejad
and her husband Kambiz Foroohar following the arrest of an armed
individual outside their home in New York.  Alinejad is a leading
American activist, journalist, and champion for human rights and women’s
equality in Iran.  On behalf of President Biden, Mr. Sullivan expressed
his profound concern over Alinejad’s physical safety, and commended
the work of law enforcement agencies for their actions to ensure the
protection of Alinejad and her family.  

Mr. Sullivan also affirmed that the U.S. Government will use all tools
at its disposal to disrupt and deter threats from Iran, including those
which target U.S. citizens and dissidents living in the United States.
 He further noted that President Biden will continue to receive updates
on her situation, and indicated that the United States stands with
Alinejad and all those in Iran who demand equal rights and dignity.
