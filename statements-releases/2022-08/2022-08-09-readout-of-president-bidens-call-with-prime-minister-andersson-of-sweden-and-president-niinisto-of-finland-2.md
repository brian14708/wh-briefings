President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with Prime Minister Magdalena
Andersson of Sweden and President Sauli Niinistö of Finland before
today’s signing of the Instruments of Ratification for the Accession
Protocols to the North Atlantic Treaty for Finland and Sweden. President
Biden congratulated them on the U.S. Senate’s swift, bipartisan
ratification of their NATO accession protocols, and welcomed Finland and
Sweden moving one step closer to becoming NATO Allies.  The United
States looks forward to depositing its instrument of ratification at the
earliest possible moment. President Biden affirmed that the United
States will work with Finland and Sweden to remain vigilant against any
threats to our shared security, and to deter and confront aggression or
threat of aggression.
