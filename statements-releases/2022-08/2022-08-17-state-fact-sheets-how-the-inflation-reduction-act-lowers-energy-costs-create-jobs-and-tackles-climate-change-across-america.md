Today, the White House released state fact sheets highlighting how the
Inflation Reduction Act tackles the climate crisis in states across the
country and how families and communities can benefit from a clean energy
future. The fact sheet outlines how families can save on their utility
bills, get tax credits for electric vehicles and energy-saving
appliances, and access the economic opportunities of the clean energy
future.

President Biden and Congressional Democrats beat back special interests
to pass this historic legislation, delivering the most significant
action in U.S. history to tackle the climate crisis and strengthen U.S.
energy security. By signing the Inflation Reduction Act, President Biden
is delivering on his promise to lower energy costs, create good-paying
jobs, and deliver a clean, secure, and healthy future for families
across America.
