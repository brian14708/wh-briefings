Assistant to the President for National Security Affairs Jake Sullivan
spoke with His Highness Ahmad al-Nawaf al-Sabah of Kuwait on August 11
to address the U.S.-Kuwait partnership and regional security.  Mr.
Sullivan congratulated Prime Minister al-Sabah on his appointment on
behalf of President Biden and re-affirmed the enduring U.S. commitment
to Kuwait.  Together they discussed strengthening the U.S.-Kuwait
relationship in light of the historic Jeddah Summit and the need to turn
the UN-mediated truce in Yemen into a lasting settlement.  Mr. Sullivan
also thanked Kuwait for its generous humanitarian aid to the Ukrainian
people.
