  \ Jake Sullivan\u2019s Trilateral Dialogue with Japan and the Republic of\_Korea"

National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan, National Security Secretariat
Secretary General Akiba Takeo of Japan, and Director of the National
Security Office of the Republic of Korea (ROK) Kim Sung-han will meet at
the Headquarters of the United States Indo-Pacific Command in Honolulu,
Hawaii on September 1, 2022.  The officials will meet bilaterally on
August 31.  After the trilateral meeting, Mr. Sullivan will visit the
United States Indo-Pacific Command to discuss our alliances in defense
of the free and open Indo-Pacific.
