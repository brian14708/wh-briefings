President Joseph R. Biden Jr. spoke today with Prime Minister Yair Lapid
of Israel to consult on global and regional security challenges,
including threats posed by Iran. The President underscored U.S.
commitment to never allow Iran to acquire a nuclear weapon. The
President expressed appreciation for the warm reception during his July
trip to Israel, a visit that illustrated the unbreakable bonds and
friendship between our two countries. The President also emphasized the
importance of concluding the maritime boundary negotiations between
Israel and Lebanon in the coming weeks.
