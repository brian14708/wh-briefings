  Rica"

Vice President Kamala Harris met today with President Rodrigo Chaves of
Costa Rica on the margins of the Summit of the Americas in Los Angeles,
California. The Vice President underscored the importance we place on
the U.S.-Costa Rica partnership and shared efforts to build a more
prosperous and inclusive future for the Western Hemisphere, including
work underway at the Summit. In particular, they discussed how to
advance a coordinated and shared response to migration across the
hemisphere.
