On Tuesday, June 21, officials from the White House Gender Policy
Council and National Security Council convened a meeting with leaders
and experts on global reproductive rights. Participants discussed how a
ruling in *Dobbs v. Jackson Women’s Health Organization* might impact
access to sexual and reproductive health and rights around the world,
and offered recommendations to the Administration to protect and advance
women’s health globally.   

White House officials thanked the leaders for their efforts and
underscored the Administration’s commitment to support sexual and
reproductive health and rights across the globe.

Participants included:

-   Amnesty International
-   Center for Reproductive Rights
-   EngenderHealth
-   Ibis Reproductive Health
-   Ipas
-   PAI
-   Women Deliver
