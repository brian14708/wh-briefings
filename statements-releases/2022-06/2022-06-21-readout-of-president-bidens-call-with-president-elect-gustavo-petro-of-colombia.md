  \ of\_Colombia"

President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke with President-elect Gustavo Petro
today to congratulate him and Vice President-elect Francia Marquez on
their election. President Biden commended the Colombian people for
holding free and fair elections and reaffirmed U.S. support for
Colombia. He underscored that he looks forward to working with the
President-elect to continue strengthening bilateral cooperation,
including on climate change, health security, and implementation of the
2016 Peace Accord. President Biden also welcomed the opportunity to
discuss bilateral security and counternarcotics cooperation. They
discussed the importance of mutual respect and partnership as the
foundation of the U.S.-Colombia relationship, and agreed to have their
teams follow up and engage directly on shared interests.
