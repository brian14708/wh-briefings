President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke by phone today with President Recep
Tayyip Erdogan of Turkey. President Biden noted he looks forward to
seeing President Erdogan at the NATO Summit in Madrid where leaders will
discuss the consequences of Russia’s invasion of Ukraine for
Transatlantic security and other threats to the Alliance such as
terrorism, as well as take historic decisions to strengthen the
Alliance’s collective defense and security.
