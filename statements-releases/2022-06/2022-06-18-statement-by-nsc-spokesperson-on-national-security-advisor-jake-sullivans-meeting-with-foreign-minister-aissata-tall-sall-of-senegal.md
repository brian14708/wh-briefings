National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met yesterday with Foreign
Minister Aïssata Tall Sall of Senegal at the White House.  Mr. Sullivan
affirmed the Administration’s commitment to partnering with Senegal and
the African Union to tackle shared global challenges.  The National
Security Advisor and Foreign Minister discussed the devastating impact
of Russia’s war on Ukraine on food security in Africa, and the need to
keep global food and fertilizer markets open and functioning.  The
National Security Advisor and Foreign Minister agreed to deepen
cooperation, including on maritime issues, and Mr. Sullivan commended
Senegal’s leadership to address insecurity in West Africa.
