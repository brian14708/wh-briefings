Principal Deputy National Security Advisor Jon Finer met today with
Ukrainian Prime Minister Denys Shmyhal to underscore steadfast U.S.
support for Ukraine’s self defense and recovery from Russian
aggression.  Prime Minister Shmyhal thanked the people of the United
States for their support, and stressed the need for additional U.S.
security assistance given Ukraine’s urgent battlefield needs.  The pair
also discussed Ukraine’s continued reforms, and
the importance of continued progress on these for its Euro-Atlantic
integration.
