National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan spoke by telephone on Wednesday
with Sri Lankan National Security Advisor Sagala Ratnayake. Their
discussion covered areas of bilateral engagement including U.S. support
to Sri Lanka’s security and sovereignty, Sri Lanka’s ongoing efforts to
complete the fiscal, monetary, and governance elements of its IMF
program, and future opportunities for cooperation. National Security
Advisor Sullivan expressed his interest in maintaining an ongoing
engagement with Sri Lanka in order to collaboratively pursue peace and
security in the region.
