On April 16, Assistant to the President and Homeland Security Advisor
Liz Sherwood-Randall and Deputy Assistant to the President and Senior
Advisor for Energy and Investment Amos Hochstein travelled to Mexico
City to meet with President Andrés Manuel López Obrador and with Foreign
Secretary Alicia Bárcena to advance the extensive economic and security
cooperation between our two countries.
