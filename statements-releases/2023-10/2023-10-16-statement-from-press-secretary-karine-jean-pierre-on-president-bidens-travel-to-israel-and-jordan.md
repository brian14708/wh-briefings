President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. will travel to Israel on Wednesday,
October 18, to demonstrate his steadfast support for Israel in the face
of Hamas’s brutal terrorist attack and to consult on next steps.   
    
President Biden will then travel to Amman, Jordan, where he will meet
with his Majesty King Abdullah, Egyptian President Sisi, and Palestinian
Authority President Mahmoud Abbas. He will reiterate that Hamas does not
stand for the Palestinian people’s right to dignity and
self-determination and discuss the humanitarian needs of civilians in
Gaza.
