President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with President Abdel Fattah
Al-Sisi of Egypt.  The two leaders discussed ongoing coordination to
deliver humanitarian assistance to Gaza and mechanisms to ensure the aid
is distributed for the benefit of the civilian population. The two
leaders agreed to work together closely on encouraging an urgent and
robust international response to the UN’s humanitarian appeal.  They
agreed on the need to preserve stability in the Middle East, prevent
escalation of the conflict, and set the circumstances for a durable,
permanent peace in the region.  The two leaders reaffirmed their
commitment to the enduring, strategic partnership between the United
States and Egypt.
