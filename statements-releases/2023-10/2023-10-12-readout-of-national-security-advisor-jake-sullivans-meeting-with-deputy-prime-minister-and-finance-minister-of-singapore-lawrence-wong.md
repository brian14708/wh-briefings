National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan met today at the White House
with Deputy Prime Minister and Finance Minister Lawrence Wong of
Singapore.  Mr. Sullivan and Mr. Wong underscored the strength of the
U.S.-Singapore Strategic Partnership and reviewed opportunities to
expand the relationship, including through additional cooperation with
ASEAN and other Indo-Pacific partners.  Mr. Sullivan and Mr. Wong also
discussed regional and global issues and shared efforts to develop an
Indo-Pacific Economic Framework that advances inclusive and sustainable
prosperity.
