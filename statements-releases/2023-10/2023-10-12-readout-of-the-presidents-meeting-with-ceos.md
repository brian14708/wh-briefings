This afternoon, the President met with CEOs from a variety of sectors,
who shared how their businesses are thriving and contributing to our
economic growth. The CEOs described how Bidenomics is leading to a
resilient labor market along with an investment boom in construction,
manufacturing, and clean energy, creating new opportunities for
businesses and workers. The President and business leaders underscored
the importance of public sector-enabled, private sector-led investments
as the Biden-Harris Administration kicks off its third [Investing in
America
tour](https://www.whitehouse.gov/briefing-room/statements-releases/2023/10/05/fact-sheet-biden-harris-administration-to-kick-off-third-investing-in-america-tour-tout-historic-investments-benefitting-communities-across-the-country/).  
  
  
**Attendees:**

-   Brendan Bechtel, Chairman and CEO, Bechtel Group
-   Calvin Butler, CEO, Exelon
-   Kenneth Chenault, Chairman and Managing Director, General Catalyst
-   Brian Cornell, Chair and CEO, Target
-   Thasunda Brown Duckett, President and CEO, TIAA
-   Arvind Krishna, Chairman and CEO, IBM
-   Judy Marks, Chair, CEO and President, Otis Worldwide
-   Jeff Zients, Assistant to the President and Chief of Staff
-   Jared Bernstein, Chair, Council of Economic Advisers
-   Steve Ricchetti, Assistant to the President and Counselor to the
    President
-   Lael Brainard, Assistant to the President and Director of the
    National Economic Council
-   Stephen Benjamin, Assistant to the President, Senior Advisor to the
    President and Director of the Office of Public Engagement
