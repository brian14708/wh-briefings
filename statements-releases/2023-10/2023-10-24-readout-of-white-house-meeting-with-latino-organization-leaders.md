---
 
Last Wednesday, Senior Biden-Harris officials hosted a meeting with
Latino leaders ahead of the White House Hispanic Heritage Month
celebration. During the meeting leaders discussed President Biden’s
commitment to advancing opportunities for Latino families across the
country. Administration officials highlighted ongoing Administration
efforts including the implementation of President Biden’s “Investing in
America” agenda, Bidenomics, immigration, climate change, and Latino
representation in the Administration.

The Latino leaders praised President Biden’s efforts and shared their
experiences of working within Latino communities. The leaders emphasized
the importance of the Administration’s work to improve equity and
highlighted the significance of maintaining collaboration with Latino
organizations throughout the country. The group also reinforced the
great contributions that Latino communities have made to the country.
The group agreed to continue to working collaboratively and to ensuring
that Latino communities are supported and valued.

**Administration Participants Included:**

-   Stephen Benjamin, Assistant to the President, Senior Advisor to the
    President and Director of the Office of Public Engagement
-   Tom Perez, Assistant to the President, Senior Advisor to the
    President and Director of Intergovernmental Affairs
-   Emmy Ruiz, Assistant to the President and Director of the Office of
    Political Strategy and Outreach
-   Neera Tanden, Assistant to the President and Domestic Policy Advisor
