President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. met today with the People’s Republic of
China Director of the Office of the Foreign Affairs Commission and
Foreign Minister Wang Yi.   
  
The President emphasized that both the United States and China need to
manage competition in the relationship responsibly and maintain open
lines of communication.  He underscored that the United States and China
must work together to address global challenges.  
  
President Biden expressed his condolences on the passing of former
Premier Li Keqiang.
