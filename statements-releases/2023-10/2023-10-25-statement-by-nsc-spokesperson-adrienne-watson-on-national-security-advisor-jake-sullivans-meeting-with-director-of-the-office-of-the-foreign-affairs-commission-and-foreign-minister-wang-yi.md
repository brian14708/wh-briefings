National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan will meet with Chinese Communist
Party Politburo Member, Director of the Office of the Foreign Affairs
Commission, and Foreign Minister Wang Yi on October 27. This meeting is
consistent with commitments by both sides to maintain this strategic
channel of communication as part of ongoing efforts to responsibly
manage the relationship. It follows recent high-level diplomatic
interactions between National Security Advisor Sullivan and Director
Wang Yi in Malta in September and in Vienna in May.
