This week, the White House Office of Public Engagement convened over 100
Latino leaders from the Salvadoran community to discuss the Biden-Harris
Administration’s ongoing efforts and investments within the Latino
community.

During the briefing, participants discussed the United States-El
Salvador relation, including economic accomplishments of the
Administration pertaining to President Biden’s Investing in America
Agenda, the Justice40 Initiative, and affordable housing investments.

Speakers uplifted the historic investments secured by the Biden-Harris
Administration through private partnerships to address the root causes
of migration in Central America and praised the economic investments to
the Latino community as part of President Biden’s economic agenda
Bidenomics, and shared their successful stories on how their communities
have benefited from it.

Biden-Harris Administration officials will continue working with the
community to deliver on the President’s efforts to advance equity for
the Latino community.
