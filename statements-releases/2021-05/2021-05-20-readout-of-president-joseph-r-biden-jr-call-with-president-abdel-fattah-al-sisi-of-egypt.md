  \ Al Sisi of\_Egypt"

President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. spoke today with President Abdel Fattah
Al Sisi of Egypt. The two leaders discussed efforts to achieve a
ceasefire that will bring an end to the current hostilities in Israel
and Gaza. They agreed that their teams would stay in constant
communication toward that end and the two leaders would stay closely in
touch.
