title: President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Amends Nebraska Disaster Declaration
---
 
Today, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. made additional disaster
assistance available to the State of Nebraska by authorizing an increase
in the level of Federal funding for Public Assistance projects
undertaken in the State of Nebraska as a result of a severe winter
storm, straight-line winds, and flooding during the period of March 9 to
July 14, 2019.  
  
Under the major disaster declaration issued for the State of Nebraska on
March 21, 2019, Federal funding was made available for Public
Assistance, Hazard Mitigation, and Other Needs Assistance at 75 percent
of the total eligible costs.           
  
Under the President’s order today, the Federal share for Public
Assistance has been increased to 90 percent of the total eligible
costs.
