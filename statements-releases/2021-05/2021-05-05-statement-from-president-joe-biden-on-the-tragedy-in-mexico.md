title: Statement from President Joe Biden on the Tragedy in Mexico
---
 
The United States joins the people of Mexico in mourning the loss of
life due to the collapse of a rail overpass in Mexico City. We send our
deepest condolences to all those who lost a loved one and offer our best
wishes for the recovery of all those who were injured. As neighbors and
partners, our nations are closely linked, and the United States is ready
to assist Mexico as it rebuilds from this tragedy.
