title: "President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Amends Cahuilla Band of Indians Disaster\_\
  Declaration"
---
 
Yesterday, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. made additional disaster
assistance available to the Cahuilla Band of Indians by authorizing an
increase in the level of federal funding for Public Assistance projects
undertaken as a result of severe storms and flooding on February 14,
2019.  
  
Under the major disaster declaration issued for the Cahuilla Band of
Indians on March 28, 2019, federal funding was made available for Public
Assistance and Hazard Mitigation at 75 percent of the total eligible
costs.            
  
Under the President’s order today, the federal share for Public
Assistance has been increased to 90 percent of the total eligible
costs.
