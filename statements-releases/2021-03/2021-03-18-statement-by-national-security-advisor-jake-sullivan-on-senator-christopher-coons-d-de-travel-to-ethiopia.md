Senator Christopher Coons is traveling to Addis Ababa, Ethiopia to meet
with Prime Minister Abiy Ahmed Ali at President Biden’s request. Senator
Coons will convey President Biden’s grave concerns about the
humanitarian crisis and human rights abuses in the Tigray region and the
risk of broader instability in the Horn of Africa. He will also consult
with the African Union on how to advance the region’s shared interests
in peace and prosperity.
