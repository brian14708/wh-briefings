Today, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. made additional disaster
assistance available to the La Jolla Band of Luiseño Indians by
authorizing an increase in the level of federal funding for Public
Assistance projects undertaken as a result of severe storms, flooding,
landslides, and mudslides during the period of February 14 to February
15, 2019. 

Under the major disaster declaration issued for the La Jolla Band of
Luiseño Indians on March 26, 2019, federal funding was made available
for Public Assistance and Hazard Mitigation at 75 percent of the total
eligible costs.          

Under the President’s order today, the federal share for Public
Assistance has been increased to 90 percent of the total eligible costs.
