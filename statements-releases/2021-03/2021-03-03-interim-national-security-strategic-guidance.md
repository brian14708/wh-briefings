title: Interim National Security Strategic Guidance
---
 
Today, the Administration released the Interim National Security
Strategic Guidance, attached.  
   
This interim guidance has been issued to convey President Biden’s vision
for how America will engage with the world, and to provide guidance for
departments and agencies to align their actions as the Administration
begins work on a National Security Strategy.  
   
To view the guidance, visit
<https://www.whitehouse.gov/wp-content/uploads/2021/03/NSC-1v2.pdf>
