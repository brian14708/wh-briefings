WASHINGTON – Today, President Joe Biden announced Clare Martorana as his
choice to serve as the Federal Chief Information Officer (CIO) and
administrator of the Office of Electronic Government at the Office of
Management and Budget (OMB).

**Clare Martorana, Federal Chief Information Officer and administrator
of the Office of Electronic Government at OMB**

Throughout her career, Clare Martorana worked to improve and simplify
the digital experiences people have when interacting with businesses and
government. Martorana most recently served as Chief Information Officer
of the U.S. Office of Personnel Management, where for the past two years
she stabilized and secured agency operations to deliver better
digital-first services for the Federal workforce. Martorana began her
public service career as a member of the U.S. Digital Service team at
the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs, establishing the agency’s
enterprise-wide Digital Modernization effort to deliver for veterans the
21st-century digital experience they deserve. Prior to joining
government, Martorana was President at Everyday Health and Senior Vice
President and General Manager and editor-at-large at WebMD.
