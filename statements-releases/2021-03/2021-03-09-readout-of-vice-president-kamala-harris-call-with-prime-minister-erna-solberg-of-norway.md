title: Readout of Vice President Kamala Harris Call with Prime Minister Erna Solberg
  of Norway
---
 
   
Vice President Kamala Harris spoke today with Prime Minister Erna
Solberg of Norway, and affirmed her commitment to deepening the strong
alliance between Norway and the United States. Vice President Harris and
Prime Minister Solberg agreed on the importance of standing together to
support human rights and shared democratic values. They agreed to
cooperate bilaterally and multilaterally to advance global health,
particularly the well-being of women and girls.  Vice President Harris
and Prime Minister Solberg also discussed the need for close
coordination on COVID-19, climate change, and the Arctic. The Vice
President thanked the Prime Minister for Norway’s close security
partnership with the United States and generous contributions to
development and health security efforts around the world.
