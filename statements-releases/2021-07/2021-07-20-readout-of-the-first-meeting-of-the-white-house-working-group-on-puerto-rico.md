title: Readout of the First Meeting of the White House Working Group on Puerto Rico
---
 
Today, Domestic Policy Advisor Susan E. Rice, National Economic Advisor
Brian Deese, and Director of Intergovernmental Affairs Julie Rodriguez
chaired the inaugural meeting of the White House Working Group on Puerto
Rico, which President Biden committed to establishing to provide Puerto
Rico the resources and technical assistance it needs to recover and
prosper.  
  
Participants in today’s White House Working Group meeting discussed the
President’s plan for Puerto Rico grounded in dignity, equity, and
respect, and his commitment to supporting a full recovery for the island
from Hurricanes Maria and Irma, the 2020 earthquakes, as well as the
COVID-19 pandemic. This means not just rebuilding but investing in the
long-term resilience of the island so that its communities and
infrastructure can withstand future disasters.  
  
Participants received briefings from the Departments of the Treasury,
Housing and Urban Development, and Education, as well as the Federal
Emergency Management Agency on their efforts to advance a comprehensive
and holistic approach that sets the island on a course for future
prosperity.
