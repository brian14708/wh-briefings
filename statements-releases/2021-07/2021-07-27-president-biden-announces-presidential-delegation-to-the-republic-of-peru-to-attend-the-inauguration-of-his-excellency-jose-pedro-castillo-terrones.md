President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. today announced the designation of a
Presidential Delegation to the Republic of Peru to attend the
Inauguration of His Excellency Pedro Castillo on July 28, 2021, in Lima,
Peru.  
  
The Honorable Miguel Cardona, United States Secretary of Education, will
lead the delegation.

Delegation:</span>**
