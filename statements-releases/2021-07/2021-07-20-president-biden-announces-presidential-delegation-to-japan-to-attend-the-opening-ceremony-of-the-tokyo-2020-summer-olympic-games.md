President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. today announced the designation of a
Presidential Delegation to Japan to attend the Opening Ceremony and
events of the Tokyo 2020 Summer Olympic Games beginning Friday, July 23,
2021.

First Lady of the United States Jill Biden will lead the delegation.

<span style="text-decoration: underline">Member of the Presidential
Delegation:</span>

Mr. Raymond Greene, Chargé d’Affaires ad interim, U.S. Embassy Tokyo
