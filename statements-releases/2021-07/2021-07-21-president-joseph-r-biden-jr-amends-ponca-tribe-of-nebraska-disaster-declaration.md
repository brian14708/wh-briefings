title: "President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. Amends Ponca Tribe of Nebraska Disaster\_Declaration"
---
 
Today, President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. made additional disaster
assistance available to the Ponca Tribe of Nebraska by authorizing an
increase in the level of federal funding for Public Assistance projects
undertaken as a result of severe storms and flooding during the period
of March 13 to April 1, 2019

Under the major disaster declaration issued for the Ponca Tribe of
Nebraska on June 17, 2019, federal funding was made available for Public
Assistance and Hazard Mitigation at 75 percent of the total eligible
costs.         

Under the President’s order today, the federal share for Public
Assistance has been increased to 90 percent of the total eligible costs.
