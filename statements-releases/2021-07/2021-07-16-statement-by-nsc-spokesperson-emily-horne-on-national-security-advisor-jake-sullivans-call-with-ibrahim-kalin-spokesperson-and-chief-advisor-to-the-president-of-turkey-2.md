National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan spoke by phone today with
Ibrahim Kalin, Spokesperson and Chief Advisor to the President of
Turkey. Following the meeting between President Joe Biden and President
Recep Tayyip Erdogan on June 14, Mr. Sullivan and Dr. Kalin discussed a
range of regional issues.
