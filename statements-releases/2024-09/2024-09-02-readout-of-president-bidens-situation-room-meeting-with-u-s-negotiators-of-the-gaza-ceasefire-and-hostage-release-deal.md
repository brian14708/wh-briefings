Today, President Biden and Vice President Harris met in the White House
Situation Room with the U.S. hostage deal negotiation team following the
murder of American Hersh Goldberg-Polin and five other hostages by
Hamas. President Biden expressed his devastation and outrage at the
murder, and reaffirmed the importance of holding Hamas’s leaders
accountable.  

During the meeting, President Biden and Vice President Harris received
an update from the U.S. negotiation team on the status of the bridging
proposal outlined by the United States, Qatar, and Egypt. They discussed
next steps in the ongoing effort to secure the release of hostages,
including continuing consultations with co-mediators Qatar and Egypt.
