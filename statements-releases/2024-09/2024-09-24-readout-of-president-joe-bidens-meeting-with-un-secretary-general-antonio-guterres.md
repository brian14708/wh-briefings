President Joseph R. Biden Jr. met with UN Secretary-General António
Guterres September 24 at UN Headquarters in New York and underscored how
the United States and United Nations are working together to advance
peace, safeguard human rights, and help countries develop. The President
spoke about his life-long commitment to building alliances,
relationships, and multilateral solutions to solve global crises, and to
upholding international principles and norms, including those enshrined
in the Charter of the United Nations. The President also emphasized the
need to advance a stronger, more effective United Nations to tackle 21st
century challenges.  He also noted U.S. efforts to invest billions in
achieving the Sustainable Development Goals.
