President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. met today with Chief Adviser Muhammad
Yunus of Bangladesh to congratulate him on his recent appointment as the
head of the interim government.  Both leaders affirmed the close
partnership between the United States and Bangladesh, which is rooted in
shared democratic values and strong people-to-people ties. President
Biden welcomed further engagement between the two governments and
offered continued U.S. support as Bangladesh implements its new reform
agenda.
