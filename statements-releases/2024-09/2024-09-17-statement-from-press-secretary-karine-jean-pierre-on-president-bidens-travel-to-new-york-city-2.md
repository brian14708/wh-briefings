President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. will travel to New York City to
participate in the 79th session of the United Nations General
Assembly on September 23-25.  In addition to addressing the General
Assembly on September 24, President Biden will meet with world leaders
to discuss cooperation in tackling threats to international peace and
security, advancing global prosperity, and protecting human rights.
