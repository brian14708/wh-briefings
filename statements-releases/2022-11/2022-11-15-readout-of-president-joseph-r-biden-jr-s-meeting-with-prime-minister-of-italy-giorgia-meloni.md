President Biden met today with Prime Minister Meloni of Italy on the
margins of the G20 Summit in Bali to coordinate responses to a range of
global challenges, including those posed by the People’s Republic of
China, the climate crisis, and Russia’s use of energy as a weapon. The
two leaders also discussed their commitment to continue providing
Ukraine the support it needs to defend itself and to holding Russia
accountable for its aggression.
