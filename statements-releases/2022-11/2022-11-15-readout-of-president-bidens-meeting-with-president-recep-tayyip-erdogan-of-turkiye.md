  \ of\_Turkiye"
---
 
President Biden met with President Recep Tayyip Erdogan on the margins
of the G20 Summit. President Biden expressed his deep condolences to
President Erdogan and the people of Turkiye on the acts of violence in
Istanbul and made clear we stand with our NATO Ally. President Biden
expressed his appreciation to President Erdogan for his efforts to renew
the Black Sea Grain Initiative, which they both agreed has been critical
to improving global food security amid Russia’s war and that the
Initiative must continue. The two also discussed continued close
coordination on NATO Alliance issues, and other issues of regional and
global concern.
