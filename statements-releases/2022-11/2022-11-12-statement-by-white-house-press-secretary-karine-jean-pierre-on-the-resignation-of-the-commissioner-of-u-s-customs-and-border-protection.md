The President has accepted the resignation of Christopher Magnus, the
Commissioner of U.S. Customs and Border Protection.  President Biden
appreciates Commissioner Magnus’ nearly forty years of service and the
contributions he made to police reform during his tenure as police chief
in three U.S. cities.  The President thanks Mr. Magnus for his service
at CBP and wishes him well.

To view the resignation letter from Mr. Magnus to the President,
visit: [https://www.whitehouse.gov/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Resignation.pdf ](https://www.whitehouse.gov/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/Resignation.pdf)
