Dear Mr. Chairman:  (Dear Madam Chair:)

In accordance with section 1264(b) of the National Defense Authorization
Act for Fiscal Year 2018 (Public Law 115-91), as amended, 50 U.S.C.
1549(b), I am transmitting notice of changes to the legal and policy
frameworks for the United States’ use of military force and related
national security operations. 

I am enclosing a copy of the unclassified notice and its classified
annex, which includes the Presidential Policy Memorandum outlining the
changes.

                               Sincerely,

                               JOSEPH R. BIDEN JR.

View the unclassified report
[here](https://www.whitehouse.gov/wp-content/uploads/2022/11/1264-Report-Nov-15-2022-unclass-notification.pdf).
