  \ of the\_Philippines"

Vice President Kamala Harris today met with Vice President Sara Duterte
of the Philippines in Manila. The Vice Presidents underscored the
strength of the U.S.-Philippines Alliance and our nations’ shared
interest in strengthening ties across all areas of cooperation. The Vice
Presidents discussed education policy and U.S. support for ensuring all
children in the Philippines have access to quality education. They also
discussed efforts to advance skills training and internet connectivity.
The Vice President underscored that the United States is committed to
working closely with the Philippines to strengthen the economic
partnership and investment ties between our countries. They also
discussed our nations’ shared democratic values and the importance of
respect for human rights.
