President Biden will meet with President Xi Jinping of the People’s
Republic of China (PRC) in Bali, Indonesia on November 14, 2022. The
Leaders will discuss efforts to maintain and deepen lines of
communication between the United States and the PRC, responsibly manage
competition, and work together where our interests align, especially on
transnational challenges that affect the international community. The
two Leaders will also discuss a range of regional and global issues.
