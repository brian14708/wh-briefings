Biden-Harris Administration has hit the ground running to make major
progress. Today, the White House released updated state and territory
fact sheets that highlight the nationwide impact of the law, the largest
long-term investment in our infrastructure and competitiveness in nearly
a century.Â  In addition to announced funding to date, the fact sheets
include key project highlights.
