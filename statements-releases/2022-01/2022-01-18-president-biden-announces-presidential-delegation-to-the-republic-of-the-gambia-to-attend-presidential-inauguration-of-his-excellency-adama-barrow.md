title: President Biden Announces Presidential Delegation to the Republic of The Gambia
  to Attend Presidential Inauguration of His Excellency Adama Barrow
---
 
President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. today announced the designation of a
Presidential Delegation to Banjul, The Gambia to attend the Inauguration
of His Excellency Adama Barrow on January 19, 2022.  
  
The Honorable Richard Carlton Paschall III, United States Ambassador to
The Gambia, will lead the delegation.
