National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan spoke by phone today with
Ibrahim Kalin, Spokesperson and Chief Advisor to the President of
Turkey.  They discussed the ongoing efforts in coordination with Allies
and partners to avoid further Russian aggression toward Ukraine and
de-escalate tensions through diplomacy.  They exchanged views on a range
of regional issues, including the situation in Kazakhstan.  They agreed
on the importance of maintaining stability and unity in
Bosnia-Herzegovina, and on working toward de-escalation and
normalization in the Caucasus. Mr. Sullivan also noted the critical need
to support efforts to end the conflict in Ethiopia.
