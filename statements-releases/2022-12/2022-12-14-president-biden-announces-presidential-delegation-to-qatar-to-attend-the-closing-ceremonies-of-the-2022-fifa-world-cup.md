President Joseph R. Biden, Jr. today announced the designation of a
Presidential Delegation to the State of Qatar to attend the Closing
Ceremonies of the 2022 FIFA World Cup on December 18, 2022, in Doha.

The Honorable Linda Thomas-Greenfield, United States Ambassador to the
United Nations, will lead the delegation.

**<u>Members of the Presidential Delegation:</u>**

The Honorable Timmy T. Davis, United States Ambassador to Qatar

The Honorable Rick Larsen, United States Representative and Co-Chair of
the Congressional Soccer Caucus (D), WA

the Congressional Soccer Caucus (R), IL

The Honorable Amanda Mansour, Special Assistant to the President and
