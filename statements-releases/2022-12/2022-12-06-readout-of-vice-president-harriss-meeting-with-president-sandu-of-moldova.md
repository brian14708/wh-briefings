title: "Readout of Vice President Harris\u2019s Meeting with President Sandu of\_\
  Moldova"
---
 
Vice President Kamala Harris today met President Maia Sandu of Moldova
at the White House. The Vice President welcomed Moldova’s progress on
its reform agenda and reaffirmed U.S. support for Moldovan energy
security and self-defense.  The Vice President also congratulated
President Sandu on receiving the Madeleine K. Albright Democracy Award
and underscored strong U.S. support for President Sandu’s commitment to
advancing democracy and fighting corruption.  The two also discussed the
impact on Moldova of Russia’s brutal war against Ukraine, including
Moldova’s efforts to welcome Ukrainian refugees.
