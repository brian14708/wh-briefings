title: 'WEEK NINE WINS: President Trump Puts America First'
---
 
President Donald J. Trump marked week nine of his presidency with a host
of resounding wins and reaffirming his relentless focus on safety,
security, and opportunity for all Americans.

**Here is a non-comprehensive list of wins in week nine:**

-   President Trump’s economic agenda continued to deliver needed
    economic relief for Americans following years of Bidenflation.
    -   Wholesale egg prices
        [dropped](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/1902002562586292572)
        for the third straight week —
        [down](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/1902008840071135279)
        more than 50% since President Trump took office.

    <!-- -->

    -   The nationwide average for gas
        [declined](https://x.com/Pfeiffer47/status/1901623430044069932)
        for the fourth straight week, with average prices falling below
        $3/gallon in a majority of states.

    <!-- -->

    -   U.S. industrial production
        [surged](https://www.breitbart.com/economy/2025/03/18/u-s-industrial-production-surges-in-february-to-record-level-led-by-manufacturing-and-autos/)
        to its highest-ever recorded level, led by growth in
        manufacturing and the auto sector.

    <!-- -->

    -   Home sales
        [rose](https://www.wsj.com/economy/housing/home-sales-february-2025-rise-f17edbfd)
        4.2% in February — far surpassing expectations.
-   President Trump and his administration continued their unprecedented
    effort to secure the homeland.
    -   President Trump
        [invoked](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2025/03/invocation-of-the-alien-enemies-act-regarding-the-invasion-of-the-united-states-by-tren-de-aragua/)
        the Alien Enemies Act to
        [deport](https://www.washingtonpost.com/immigration/2025/03/16/alien-enemies-act-venezuela-el-salvador-prison/)
        brutal Tren de Aragua [gang
        members](https://www.aa.com.tr/en/americas/261-gang-members-deported-from-us-arrive-in-el-salvador/3511585)
        to El Salvador.

    <!-- -->

    -   The Federal Bureau of Investigation
        [captured](https://www.whitehouse.gov/articles/2025/03/another-most-wanted-criminal-captured-by-the-trump-administration/)
        a [key senior
        leader](https://www.fbi.gov/wanted/topten/francisco-javier-roman-bardales)
        of the brutal MS-13 gang — the FBI’s third fugitive capture from
        its “Ten Most Wanted” list since President Trump took office.

    <!-- -->

    -   The Department of Homeland Security
        [announced](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/1902742973579685999)
        “unprecedented” seizures of illicit drugs along the country’s
        borders.

    <!-- -->

    -   Customs and Border Protection
        [awarded](https://www.cbp.gov/newsroom/national-media-release/cbp-awards-first-border-wall-contract-president-trumps-second-term)
        the first border wall construction contract of President Trump’s
        second term.

    <!-- -->

    -   *[The Washington
        Times](https://www.washingtontimes.com/news/2025/mar/19/mexicos-cartels-boxed-trumps-success-border/)*:
        Mexico’s cartels ‘boxed in’ by Trump’s success at the border
-   President Trump’s [unrelenting
    pursuit](https://www.whitehouse.gov/articles/2025/03/president-trump-is-remaking-america-into-a-manufacturing-superpower/) of
    American manufacturing dominance continued drawing new investment.
    -   Nvidia [announced](https://www.whitehouse.gov/articles/2025/03/president-trump-positions-u-s-as-global-superpower-in-manufacturing/) it
        will invest hundreds of billions of dollars over the next four
        years in its U.S.-based manufacturing operations.
        -   [Nvidia CEO Jensen
            Huang](https://www.ft.com/content/3fd3a717-2fbf-42ef-bb08-5baecdeb1985#:~:text=%E2%80%9CHaving%20the%20support%20of%20an%20administration%20who%20cares%20about%20the%20success%20of%20this%20industry%20and%20not%20allowing%20energy%20to%20be%20an%20obstacle%20is%20a%20phenomenal%20result%20for%20AI%20in%20the%20US%2C%E2%80%9D%20he%20said.):“Having
            the support of an administration who cares about the success
            of this industry and not allowing energy to be an obstacle
            is a phenomenal result for AI in the U.S.”

    -   Johnson &
        Johnson [announced](https://www.whitehouse.gov/articles/2025/03/trump-effect-johnson-johnsons-55-billion-investment-in-american-manufacturing/) a
        new $55 billion investment over the next four years in
        U.S.-based manufacturing, research and development, and
        technology.
        -   [Johnson &
            Johnson](https://www.jnj.com/media-center/press-releases/johnson-johnson-increases-u-s-investment-to-more-than-55-billion-over-the-next-four-years#:~:text=25%25%20increase%20in%20investment%20compared%20to%20the%20previous%20four%20years%20and%20builds%20upon%20the%20Company%E2%80%99s%20already%20elevated%20U.S.%20investment%20levels%20resulting%20from%20the%20passage%20of%20the%202017%20Tax%20Cuts%20%26%20Jobs%20Act.):
            “This represents a 25% increase in investment compared to
            the previous four years and builds upon the Company’s
            already elevated U.S. investment levels resulting from the
            passage of the 2017 Tax Cuts & Jobs Act.”

    -   Prepac, a Canadian furniture
        manufacturer, [announced](https://www.furnituretoday.com/tariffs/furniture-manufacturer-shuts-down-canadian-operations-moves-production-to-n-carolina/) it
        will move production from Canada to the U.S.

    -   Honda
        [announced](https://www.reuters.com/business/autos-transportation/honda-procure-toyotas-us-made-batteries-tariff-response-nikkei-reports-2025-03-17/)
        it will procure U.S.-made batteries for its hybrid vehicles.

    -   Audi

        tariffs.

        Petro](https://www.nasa.gov/news-release/welcome-home-nasas-spacex-crew-9-back-on-earth-after-science-mission/):

        war” and “agreed that a future with an improved bilateral
        relationship … has huge upside.”
