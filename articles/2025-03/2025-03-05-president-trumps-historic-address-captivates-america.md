President Donald J. Trump’s
[address](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/1897187632385548369) to a
joint session of Congress last night was [widely
acclaimed](https://www.whitehouse.gov/articles/2025/03/what-they-are-saying-president-trumps-masterclass-before-congress/)
as among his best — in which he touted his record accomplishments after
just six weeks in office and laid out his compelling vision for four
years of peace, strength, and prosperity for all Americans.

**This morning, President Trump’s speech was the focus of newspapers
across America:**
