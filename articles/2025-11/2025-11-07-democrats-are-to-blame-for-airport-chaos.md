title: Democrats Are to Blame for Airport Chaos
---
 
**Make no mistake: Democrats’ shutdown tantrum — now dragging into its
38th day of misery — is a brazen, demented betrayal of the American
people.** Now, as FAA safety restrictions loom like a guillotine over
holiday flights, Democrats are inflicting their man-made catastrophe on
Americans just trying to make life-saving medical trips or get home for
Thanksgiving.

**Everyday travelers are pleading with Democrats to end the
hostage-taking:**

-   **“I’m more than a little concerned,”**
    [said](https://www.8newsnow.com/news/local-news/las-vegas-travelers-brace-for-delays-as-faa-orders-flight-reductions/#:~:text=%E2%80%9CI%E2%80%99m%20more%20than%20a%20little%20concerned%20for%20sure%2C%E2%80%9D%20traveler%20Angelo%20Mastrio%20said.)
    a traveler at Las Vegas Harry Reid International Airport in Nevada —
    where Sen. Jacky Rosen has voted 14 times to extend the shutdown.
-   **“I am so frustrated,”**
    [said](https://www.fox5atlanta.com/news/tracking-delays-cancellations-atlanta-airport-faa-reductions-begin#:~:text=%22I%20am%20looking%20for%20options%20to%20go%20back%20home.%20I%20am%20so%20frustrated%2C%22%20she%20said.%20%22The%20option%20that%20I%20have%20is%20to%20take%20a%20bus%20to%20Orlando%20and%20find%20a%20direct%20flight.%22)
    a traveler at Hartsfield–Jackson Atlanta International Airport in
    Georgia — where Sens. Jon Ossoff and Raphael Warnock have voted 14
    times to extend the shutdown.
-   **“I’m a little worried because I’m supposed to fly to see my mom on
    Thanksgiving and I don’t see this ending,”**
    [said](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fS3eDGlCsp4) a traveler at
    Detroit Metropolitan Wayne County Airport in Michigan — where Sens.
    Gary Peters and Elissa Slotkin have voted 14 times to extend the
    shutdown.
-   **“The government is playing with people’s lives,”**
    [said](https://www.nbcnews.com/news/us-news/live-blog/faa-flights-canceled-airports-government-shutdown-live-updates-rcna242483#:~:text=The%20nation%27s%20four,flights%20are%20affected.)
    a traveler at Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport in Virginia
    — where Sens. Tim Kaine and Mark Warner have voted 14 times to
    extend the shutdown.
-   **“It’s just not right. It’s very upsetting,”**
    [said](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RkzhNHIc9XE) a traveler at
    Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport in Arizona — where Sens.
    Mark Kelly and Ruben Gallego have voted 14 times to extend the
    shutdown.
-   **“We’re extremely nervous because we have to make it to the
    appointment… We can’t miss it. Time is running out,”**
    [said](https://kstp.com/money-matters/air-travelers-scrambling-with-cancellation-uncertainty-as-thousands-of-flights-impacted/)
    a traveler at Minneapolis-St. Paul International Airport in
    Minnesota — where Sens. Amy Klobuchar and Tina Smith have voted 14
    times to extend the shutdown.
-   **“It’s not just the traveling public, but it’s affecting the whole
    economy — and you people are to blame,”**
    [said](https://www.cnn.com/politics/live-news/trump-government-shutdown-flight-cancellations-delays-11-07-25?post-id=cmhox48ek000j356q7wed4nqs)
    a traveler at Newark International Airport in New Jersey — where
    Sens. Cory Booker and Andy Kim have voted 14 times to extend the
    shutdown.
-   **“It’s going to impact the everyday lives,”**
    [said](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=q58TQNmJZ7s) a traveler at
    Denver International Airport — where Sens. Michael Bennet and John
    Hickenlooper have voted 14 times to extend the shutdown.
