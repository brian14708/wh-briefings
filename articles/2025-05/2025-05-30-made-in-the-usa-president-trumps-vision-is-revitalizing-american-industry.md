President Donald J. Trump heads to Pennsylvania today, where he’ll
champion the partnership he
[brokered](https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/114558783827880495)
between U.S. Steel and Nippon Steel — a $14 billion investment that will
create at least 70,000 jobs and ensure steel is made in America for
decades to come.

**AMERICAN JOBS, AMERICAN STEEL.**

The landmark agreement comes alongside a host of companies from across
industries that are onshoring their production and investing in American
manufacturing as President Trump relentlessly pursues his America First
trade policies.
