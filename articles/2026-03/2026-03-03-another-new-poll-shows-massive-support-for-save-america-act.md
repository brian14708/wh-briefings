support from the American people — yet Radical Left Democrat politicians
continue to obstruct it, choosing partisan power plays over national
security and the will of the people.

**Fresh**
[**polling**](https://harvardharrispoll.com/press-release-february-2026/)
**underscores the SAVE America Act’s popularity with Americans:**

-   **71% support the SAVE America Act — including 69% of independents
    and half of rank-and-file Democrats.**
    -   81% favor requiring voter ID — backed by 79% of independents and
        70% of Democrats.

    <!-- -->

    -   80% want states to purge non-citizens from voter rolls.

    <!-- -->

    -   75% support proof of citizenship to vote.

    <!-- -->

    -   61% support sharing unredacted voting rolls with the Department
        of Homeland Security.
-   60% call the SAVE America Act a “commonsense way to stop fraud and
    protect the security of our elections.”
-   58% recognize at least some voter fraud exists in the U.S.
-   85% agree only U.S. citizens should vote in our elections —
    including 84% of independents and 82% of Democrats.

**These numbers align with** [**other
polling**](https://www.whitehouse.gov/articles/2026/02/voter-id-is-overwhelmingly-popular-with-literally-everyone-except-democrat-politicians/)
**that shatters Democrats’ tired narrative:**

-   **Pew Research Center: **83% of Americans —
    [including](https://www.pewresearch.org/politics/2025/08/22/majority-of-americans-continue-to-back-expanded-early-voting-voting-by-mail-voter-id/#:~:text=Support%20for%20photo%20ID%20requirements%20also%20remains%20widespread%20in%20both%20parties.%20More%20than%20nine%2Din%2Dten%20Republicans%20(95%25)%20and%20about%20seven%2Din%2Dten%20Democrats%20(71%25)%20favor%20requiring%20all%20voters%20to%20show%20government%2Dissued%20photo%20identification%20to%20vote.) [wide
    majorities](https://x.com/ForecasterEnten/status/2018727149050741020) of
    Democrats, independents, Whites, Blacks, and Latinos —
    [favor](https://www.pewresearch.org/politics/2025/08/22/majority-of-americans-continue-to-back-expanded-early-voting-voting-by-mail-voter-id/#:~:text=Requiring%20all%20voters%20to%20show%20government%2Dissued%20photo%20identification%20(83%25)) voter
    ID requirements.
-   **Gallup: **84%
    [support](https://news.gallup.com/poll/652523/americans-endorse-early-voting-voter-verification.aspx#:~:text=requiring%20photo%20identification%20to%20vote%20(84%25))
    voter ID,
    [with](https://news.gallup.com/poll/652523/americans-endorse-early-voting-voter-verification.aspx#:~:text=Republicans%20are%20more,the%20majority%20level).) 98%
    of Republicans, 84% of independents, and 67% of Democrats on board —
    while an equally robust
    83% [back](https://news.gallup.com/poll/652523/americans-endorse-early-voting-voter-verification.aspx#:~:text=providing%20proof%20of%20citizenship%20when%20registering%20to%20vote%20for%20the%20first%20time%20(83%25).) proof

-   **Rasmussen Reports:** 75%

    voter ID, with support
    [increasing](https://www.rasmussenreports.com/public_content/lifestyle/general_lifestyle/march_2021/75_support_voter_id_laws#:~:text=Support%20for%20voter%20ID%20laws%20has%20actually%20increased%20since%202018%2C%20when%2067%25%20said%20voters%20should%20be%20required%20to%20show%20photo%20identification%20such%20as%20a%20driver%E2%80%99s%20license%20before%20being%20allowed%20to%20vote.)

-   **Heritage Action:** Strong majorities across five battleground
