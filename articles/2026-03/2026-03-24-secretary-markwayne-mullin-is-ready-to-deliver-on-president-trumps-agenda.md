**Today, Markwayne Mullin was officially** [**sworn
in**](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/2036509762951479374) **as the
ninth Secretary of the Department of Homeland Security following his**
[**bipartisan
confirmation**](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/2036239847212335251)
**by the Senate.** A proven fighter with deep experience as a business
owner, rancher, and former Congressman and Senator, Secretary Mullin
brings unmatched energy and commitment to securing our borders,
protecting our homeland, and safeguarding our sovereignty.

Secretary Mullin will immediately get to work implementing President
Donald J. Trump’s agenda — building on the historic successes in
securing our borders, removing criminal illegal aliens, combating
terrorism and cartels, streamlining disaster response, and serving the
American people.
