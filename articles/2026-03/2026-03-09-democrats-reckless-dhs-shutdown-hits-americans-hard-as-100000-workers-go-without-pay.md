[**shutdown**](https://www.dhs.gov/news/2026/02/17/another-democrat-government-shutdown-dramatically-hurts-americas-national-security)

and
[hours-long](https://www.wwltv.com/article/news/local/travelers-experiencing-long-lines-at-tsa-checkpoint-at-new-orleans-international-tsa-government-shutdown-travel/289-7b812cb3-7f02-41fd-8717-69dc61b25952)

[lines](https://www.khou.com/article/travel/bush-hobby-airport-long-tsa-lines/285-c0db98ad-366b-48f8-9f76-6cd7596d51fe)
are gripping airports as millions of families head out for spring break.

**The chaos unfolded this weekend at airports across the country:**

-   **“There’s been a lot of frustration. A lot of people are very
    upset,”**
    [said](https://www.khou.com/article/travel/bush-hobby-airport-long-tsa-lines/285-c0db98ad-366b-48f8-9f76-6cd7596d51fe#:~:text=%E2%80%9CThere%E2%80%99s%20been%20a%20lot%20of%20frustration.%20A%20lot%20of%20people%20are%20very%20upset%2C%E2%80%9D%20traveler%20Leah%20Turner%20said.)
    a traveler at William P. Hobby Airport in Houston — where TSA wait
    times [neared four
    hours](https://x.com/KHOU/status/2030708265634451749).
-   **“This is ridiculous. This is crazy. We get here, we go through
    Customs — and it’s so packed in here, you can’t even find the
    direction they’re trying to give,”**
    [said](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sNX4vzLe0QQ) a traveler at
    Louis Armstrong New Orleans International Airport — where lines
    snaked through multiple floors and [spilled into the parking
    garage](https://www.nola.com/news/politics/tsa-lines-new-orleans-airport/article_2b82f544-0c26-47ea-8545-14aeb1bb83a7.html).
-   **“It’s the longest I’ve ever seen it. I’ve flown into Atlanta
    several times, and this is by far the longest I’ve ever seen it in
    my life,”**
    [said](https://www.atlantanewsfirst.com/2026/03/09/atlantas-airport-faces-fluctuating-long-wait-times-amid-partial-shutdown-ground-stop-issues/#:~:text=%E2%80%9CIt%E2%80%99s%20the%20longest%20I%E2%80%99ve%20ever%20seen%20it%2C%20I%E2%80%99ve%20flown%20into%20Atlanta%20several%20times%20and%20this%20is%20by%20far%20the%20longest%20I%E2%80%99ve%20ever%20seen%20it%20in%20my%20life%2C%E2%80%9D%20said%20Jared%20Sommer%2C%20who%20is%20traveling%20home%20to%20Houston.)
    a traveler at Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport —
    where passengers were
    [met](https://www.wsbtv.com/news/local/clayton-county/recent-ground-stops-staff-constraints-cause-tsa-delays-hartsfield-jackson/LJUBKML4UNAOFOVBK3LX2GTY2U/)
    with mounting wait times and ground delays.
-   **“We’re fed up. We’ve had enough. This is the third shutdown in a
    matter of six months,”**
    [said](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/2030995087618895987) a
    10-year veteran TSA officer in Phoenix.
