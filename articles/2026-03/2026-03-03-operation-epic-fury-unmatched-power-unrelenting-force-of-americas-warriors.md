military is unleashing overwhelming force in Operation Epic Fury —
proving once more why the U.S. possesses the most powerful, lethal, and
advanced fighting force the world has ever seen.** As America’s warriors
deliver crushing, devastating hits to eliminate the threat of the
Iranian regime once and for all, this is victory in action — decisive,
relentless, and unstoppable.

the sheer dominance of America’s military:**
