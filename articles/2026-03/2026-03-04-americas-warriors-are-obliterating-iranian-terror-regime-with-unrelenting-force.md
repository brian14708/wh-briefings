**Under the leadership of President Donald J. Trump, the United States
Armed Forces are executing Operation Epic Fury with unparalleled
precision, overwhelming power, and relentless effectiveness.** With
crushing dominance and devastating force, our warfighters are
systematically dismantling the Iranian regime’s ability to ever again
threaten America, our allies, and global security.

**Today, Secretary of War Pete Hegseth and Chairman of the Joint Chiefs
of Staff General Dan Caine provided an update from the Pentagon on the
extraordinary progress of Operation Epic Fury and the unrelenting
strength of America’s warriors:**

-   “Thus far, Operation Epic Fury has delivered twice the air power of
    ‘Shock and Awe’ of Iraq in 2003… We are accelerating, not
    decelerating. Iran’s capabilities are evaporating by the hour, while
    American strength grows fiercer, smarter, and utterly dominant.”
    ([Watch](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/2029184881494831296?s=20))
-   “Iran’s theater ballistic missile shots fired are down 86% from the
    first day of fighting, with a 23% decrease just in the last 24 hours
    — and their one-way attack drone shots are down 73% from the opening
    days.”
    ([Watch](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/2029187525739208862?s=20))
-   “Starting last night, and to be completed in a few days in under a
    week, the two most powerful Air Forces in the world will have
    complete control of Iranian skies.”
    ([Watch](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/2029183819484442979?s=20))
-   “In the Indian Ocean, an American submarine sunk an Iranian warship
    that thought it was safe in international waters. Instead, it was
    sunk by a torpedo, Quiet Death — the first sinking of an enemy ship
    by a torpedo since World War II.”
    ([Watch](https://x.com/DOWResponse/status/2029193589079613857))
-   “The leader of the unit who attempted to assassinate President Trump
    has been hunted down and killed. Iran tried to kill President Trump
    — and President Trump got the last laugh.”
    ([Watch](https://x.com/DOWResponse/status/2029188555424354767?s=20))
-   “Iran’s senior leaders are dead. The so-called ‘Governing Council’
    that might have selected a successor? Dead, missing, or cowering in
    bunkers, too terrified to even occupy the same room. Senior
    generals, mid-level officers, enlisted ranks — they can’t talk or
    coordinate, let alone mount a coordinated and sustained offensive…
    The Iranian Air Force is no more. Built for 1996; destroyed in 2026.
    The Iranian Navy rests at the bottom of the Persian Gulf.”
    ([Watch](https://x.com/DOWResponse/status/2029187209509634162?s=20))
