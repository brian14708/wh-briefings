title: President Trump Marks Six Months in Office with Historic Successes
---
 
**Today, President Donald J. Trump celebrates the most successful first
six months in office for any President in modern American history.**

-   Congress passed the One Big Beautiful Bill, thereby delivering the
    [largest tax
    cut](https://www.whitehouse.gov/articles/2025/04/icymi-president-trumps-largest-tax-cut-in-history/)
    in American history,
    [increasing](https://www.whitehouse.gov/wp-content/uploads/2025/03/Preserving-and-Expanding-Low-Tax-Rates-to-Create-American-Economic-Prosperity.pdf)
    Americans’ take-home pay by as much as $13,300, and
    [terminating](https://www.breitbart.com/politics/2025/05/15/democrat-messaging-falters-as-they-ask-constituents-to-foot-medicaid-costs-for-1-4-million-illegal-aliens/)
    benefits for at least 1.4 million illegal immigrants who were gaming
    the system.
-   Congress
    [passed](https://nypost.com/2025/07/18/us-news/house-passes-rescissions-package-slashing-9-billion-in-federal-funding-for-foreign-aid-npr-and-pbs/)
    President Trump’s historic rescissions package, which will save
    taxpayers $9 billion in wasteful, politically-motivated funding for
    leftwing foreign aid scams and biased NPR and PBS.
-   The wholesale price of a dozen eggs is down 53%, or $3.09, since the
    inauguration and is [down
    62%](https://www.ams.usda.gov/mnreports/ams_2843.pdf), or $5.08,
    from its [March](https://tradingeconomics.com/commodity/eggs-us)
    peak.
-   The U.S. economy has now
    [added](https://x.com/ChairmanWhatley/status/1940760835619803345) a
    net of 671,000 jobs since January 2025, with jobs numbers beating
    expectations four months in a row. Native-born workers have
    [accounted](https://www.whitehouse.gov/articles/2025/07/june-boom-jobs-report-shows-the-economy-continues-to-soar-under-president-trump/)
    for all job gains, with native-born employment increasing 2,079,000
    while foreign-born employment has fallen 543,000.
-   U.S. Customs and Border Patrol
    [encountered](https://www.whitehouse.gov/articles/2025/07/extraordinary-president-trump-drives-illegal-border-crossings-to-a-new-historic-low/)
    just 6,070 illegal immigrants at the southern border in June —
    setting a new record low (15% lower than the previous record set in
    March). Additionally, *zero* illegal immigrants were
    [released](https://www.cbp.gov/newsroom/national-media-release/most-secure-border-history-cbp-reports-major-enforcement-wins-june)
    into the U.S. on parole in June, compared to 27,766 a year prior.
-   The administration has ramped up deportations,
    [breaking](https://english.elpais.com/usa/2025-07-11/us-breaks-record-with-209-deportation-flights-in-june-after-first-major-increase-of-trump-era.html)
    a record for the number of deportation flights in a month in June.
    President Trump’s self-deportation push has also been a [massive
    success](https://x.com/theblaze/status/1945665572156641298).
    Additionally, over 600 known and suspected terrorists have been
    [removed](https://x.com/TrumpWarRoom/status/1946239003381895508)
    from the United States.
-   At President Trump’s direction, U.S. Immigrations and Customs
    Enforcement has
    [arrested](https://www.cbsnews.com/news/ice-arrests-under-trump-100k/)
    over 100,000 illegal alien criminals,
    [including](https://abc3340.com/news/nation-world/ag-pam-bondi-announces-arrest-of-2700-venezuelan-tren-de-aragua-gang-members-in-us-president-donald-trump-deportation-press-briefing-colorado-apartment-complex-border-policies-joe-biden-undocumented)
    over 2,700 members of the vicious Tren de Aragua gang.
-   Following President Trump’s declaration of an energy emergency, the
    U.S. has [reached](https://archive.ph/Hcubg) its fastest rate of new
    oil and gas drilling permits in years, exceeding the Biden
    administration by 44%.
-   Since President Trump took office, core inflation has
    [tracked](https://www.whitehouse.gov/articles/2025/07/inflation-remains-right-on-target-under-president-trump/)
    at just 2.1% — levels not seen since the first Trump Administration,
    when prices were low and stable — and has come in below or at
    economists’ expectations every single month. Meanwhile, wholesale
    inflation
    [remained](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/07/16/ppi-inflation-report-june-2025.html)
    flat in June, while import prices [came
    in](https://x.com/RealEJAntoni/status/1945825738227151333) far below
    expectations.
-   Summer gas prices
    [reached](https://lvb.com/gas-prices-exceptionally-low-for-summer-driving-season/)
    their lowest point since 2021, and, inflation-adjusted,
    [are](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/06/28/gas-prices-will-be-the-cheapest-theyve-been-in-years-expert-says.html)
    near a 20-year low.
-   President Trump’s deregulatory efforts have already
    [saved](https://www.whitehouse.gov/articles/2025/03/president-trumps-deregulation-effort-has-already-saved-families-thousands-of-dollars/)
    Americans over $180 billion, or $2,100 per family of four, with the
    rollback of automobile-related rules alone expected to save
    consumers more than $1.1 trillion.
-   President Trump
    [secured](https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/114738606142462442)
    a historic agreement for NATO members to raise defense spending to
    5% of GDP – a foreign policy feat long thought impossible.
-   Under President Trump’s strong and decisive leadership, the U.S.
    [obliterated](https://www.whitehouse.gov/articles/2025/06/irans-nuclear-facilities-have-been-obliterated-and-suggestions-otherwise-are-fake-news/)
    Iran’s nuclear program.
-   President Trump secured ceasefires between [India and
    Pakistan](https://www.npr.org/2025/05/10/nx-s1-5392335/trump-announces-ceasefire-agreement-between-india-and-pakistan)
    and [Israel and
    Iran](https://apnews.com/article/trump-iran-israel-ceasefire-agreement-terms-b5fc5cc8a8c32b4899646130b496798a),
    a [peace
    agreement](https://www.state.gov/releases/office-of-the-spokesperson/2025/06/peace-agreement-signing-between-the-democratic-republic-of-the-congo-and-republic-of-rwanda/)
    between Rwanda and the Democratic Republic of Congo, and a
    [pathway](https://www.whitehouse.gov/fact-sheets/2025/06/fact-sheet-president-donald-j-trump-provides-for-the-revocation-of-syria-sanctions/)
    to stability for Syria.
-   As a result of his historic peacemaking efforts, President Trump has
    [already](https://x.com/RepBuddyCarter/status/1937496308069064985)
    [received](https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cwyx5yw8y28o)

-   The U.S. Treasury has taken in [nearly $90

    U.S. Steel, and trade deals with the [United
