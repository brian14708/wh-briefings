The American people won big this week following President Donald J.
Trump’s historic announcements on restoring fair trade and more great
economic news.

**Here is a non-comprehensive list of wins in week 12:**

-   President Trump brought [more than 75
    countries](https://x.com/RapidResponse47/status/1910027418993143962)
    to the negotiating table following his [bold
    action](https://www.whitehouse.gov/fact-sheets/2025/04/fact-sheet-president-donald-j-trump-declares-national-emergency-to-increase-our-competitive-edge-protect-our-sovereignty-and-strengthen-our-national-and-economic-security/)
    on reciprocal trade — and stood up for American workers who have
    seen their jobs go overseas to China.
