It was another highly successful week for the American people as
President Donald J. Trump continues his relentless pursuit of strength,
prosperity, and peace — and lays the foundation for America to be the
global powerhouse for generations to come.

**Here is a non-comprehensive list of wins in week 11:**

    [hit](https://www.whitehouse.gov/articles/2025/04/icymi-illegal-border-crossings-hit-new-record-low-in-march/)
    a stunning [new record

    — down 95% over last year.

    -   [*Los Angeles
        Times*](https://www.latimes.com/politics/story/2025-03-30/with-few-migrants-arriving-at-california-mexico-border-nonprofits-border-patrol-pivot):
        “California-Mexico border, once overwhelmed, now nearly empty”
    -   [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-04-03/us-bound-migration-plunges-99-along-panama-jungle-route):
        “US-Bound Migration Plunges 99% Along Panama Jungle Route”

    immigrant criminals.

        successful arrests of three illegal immigrant MS-13 gang members

        U.S. to El Salvador.

        successfully
        [arrested](https://www.dps.texas.gov/news/joint-tda-operation-results-over-40-arrests-hays-co/)

        brutal Tren de Aragua gang.
    -   The Trump Administration
        [deported](https://nypost.com/2024/09/16/us-news/migrant-influencer-leonel-moreno-who-encouraged-migrants-to-be-squatters-is-ordered-deported/)
