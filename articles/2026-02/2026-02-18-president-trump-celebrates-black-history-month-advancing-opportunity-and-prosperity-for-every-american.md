  Prosperity for Every American'
---
 
**Today, President Donald J. Trump hosts leaders from the black
community at the White House to mark Black History Month and reflect on
the Administration’s ongoing efforts to expand economic opportunity,
strengthen education, and promote justice for every American.** Through
targeted policies and broad reforms, President Trump has prioritized
initiatives that deliver tangible benefits to black families while
ensuring prosperity is accessible to every American citizen — fostering
a stronger nation united by shared success for all.

**Here’s what you need to know about President Trump’s second term:**

-   President Trump
    [awarded](https://www.ed.gov/about/news/press-release/us-department-of-education-makes-historic-grant-investments-programs-bolster-educational-outcomes)
    hundreds of millions of dollars in additional funding to strengthen
    educational outcomes at Historically Black Colleges and Universities
    (HBCUs), building on longstanding commitments to enhance access to
    quality higher education for students nationwide.
-   President Trump’s [historic
    push](https://www.whitehouse.gov/saveamerica/) to pass the SAVE
    America Act will safeguard the voices of black Americans — and all
    Americans — by preventing illegal votes from disenfranchising any
    citizen.
-   Trump Accounts — a [new, historic savings
    tool](https://trumpaccounts.gov/) made possible by President Trump’s
    [Working Families Tax Cuts Act](https://www.whitehouse.gov/obbb/) —
    provides newborn black Americans and all children with early
    exposure to the stock market, helping to bridge generational [wealth
    gaps](https://www.pewresearch.org/short-reads/2024/03/06/a-booming-us-stock-market-doesnt-benefit-all-racial-and-ethnic-groups-equally/)
    through compounded growth.
-   President Trump’s landmark Working Families Tax Cut Act secured No
    Tax on Tips, No Tax on Overtime, No Tax on Social Security, and a
    long list of other major tax benefits, delivering relief to millions
    of eligible black workers and families across the workforce.
-   President Trump
    [secured](https://www.army.mil/article/290125/1776_warrior_dividend_tax_free_irs_confirms)
    a tax-free $1,776 Warrior Dividend bonus for U.S. service members —
    including the [350,000 black service
    members](https://news.syr.edu/2023/01/31/black-history-month-data-on-black-african-americans-in-the-military/#:~:text=There%20are%20over%20350%2C000%20Black%20and%20African%20American%20active%20duty%20and%20select%20reserve%20members)
    proudly serving our country.
-   President Trump is protecting the hard-earned benefits of the [2.4
    million black
    veterans](https://news.syr.edu/2023/01/31/black-history-month-data-on-black-african-americans-in-the-military/#:~:text=Over%202.4%20million%20Black%20and%20African%20American%20veterans%20in%20the%20U.S)
    in the U.S. by
    [reducing](https://news.va.gov/press-room/va-reduces-backlog-of-veterans-waiting-for-va-benefits-by-57/)
    the backlog of veterans waiting for VA benefits,
    [reforming](https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trump-signs-new-law-helping-veterans-avoid-foreclosure-partial-claims-payment-program)
    the VA loan home program to help veterans keep their homes, and
    [eliminating](https://x.com/SecVetAffairs/status/1991181745605996623)
    waste, fraud, and abuse in the system.

**In his first term, President Trump’s policies were equally
transformative:**

-   President Trump
    [relocated](https://trumpwhitehouse.archives.gov/briefings-statements/president-donald-j-trump-delivering-promise-empower-lift-nations-forgotten-communities/#:~:text=Barely%20a%20month%20after%20taking%20office%2C%20President%20Trump%20signed%20an%20Executive%20Order%20to%20move%20the%20Federal%20HBCU%20initiative%20back%20to%20the%20White%20House%20%E2%80%93%20making%20sure%20it%20is%20a%20key%20priority.)
    the Federal HBCU Initiative back to the White House — ensuring it
    remained a key priority of his Administration and elevating support
    for these vital institutions.
-   President Trump
    [signed](https://apnews.com/article/c4834e48841d97c5a93312b1bf75302a)
    the FUTURE Act into law, securing a historic [$255
    million](https://www.scott.senate.gov/newsroom/news-coverage/scott-leads-bipartisan-passage-of-the-future-act-to-annually-fund-american-hbcus-and-msis/#:~:text=C.)%2C%20and%20Chris%20Coons,driven%20repayment%20for%20nearly%208)
    in annual funding for HBCUs and increasing funding for Pell Grants
    to make college more affordable for low-income students from all
    backgrounds.
-   President Trump
    [authorized](https://www.nbcnews.com/news/nbcblk/trump-announces-support-certain-historically-black-colleges-n1052331#:~:text=Trump%20also%20used%20the%20speech%20to%20review%20assistance%20he%E2%80%99s%20provided%20to%20HBCUs%20since%20taking%20office%2C%20including%20signing%20a%20farm%20bill%20that%20included%20more%20than%20%24100%20million%20for%20scholarships%20and%20research%20at%20certain%20historically%20black%20colleges%20and%20universities.)
    more than $100 million for scholarships, research, and centers of
    excellence at HBCU land-grant institutions, fostering innovation and
    academic excellence.
-   President Trump
    [granted](https://hbcubuzz.com/57995/education-department-forgives-322-million-loans-help-hbcus-recover-hurricanes/)
    full forgiveness of $322 million in hurricane-related disaster
    relief loans to four HBCUs, allowing them to focus fully on
    educating their students without the burden of debt.
-   President Trump
    [lifted](https://www.nbcnews.com/news/nbcblk/trump-announces-support-certain-historically-black-colleges-n1052331)
    restrictions that barred HBCUs with religious affiliations from
    accessing federal funding for capital projects, expanding
    opportunities for faith-based education.
-   President Trump’s America First agenda drove [record-low
    unemployment](https://www.cnbc.com/2019/10/04/black-and-hispanic-unemployment-is-at-a-record-low.html)
    for Black Americans, with [over
    350,000](https://www.kff.org/other/state-indicator/poverty-rate-by-raceethnicity/?currentTimeframe=3&sortModel=%7B%22colId%22:%22Location%22,%22sort%22:%22asc%22%7D)
    lifting themselves out of poverty through booming manufacturing and
    smarter trade policies that benefited workers nationwide.
-   President Trump
    [created](https://opportunityzones.hud.gov/news/opportunity-zones-makes-economic-prosperity-a-reality-in-fort-myers)
    Opportunity Zones, a game-changing initiative that attracted $75
    billion in new private investment into economically distressed
    communities —
    [creating](https://trumpwhitehouse.archives.gov/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/The-Impact-of-Opportunity-Zones-An-Initial-Assessment.pdf)
    $11 billion in new wealth for minority neighborhoods and
    revitalizing areas long overlooked.
-   President Trump
    [signed](https://www.bop.gov/inmates/fsa/overview.jsp) the First
    Step Act into law, enacting historic criminal justice reforms to
    make the system fairer, reduce recidivism, and provide second
    chances for individuals reentering society — impacting families and
    communities across the country.

**As we celebrate Black History Month amid America’s 250th anniversary,
President Trump remains committed to policies that lift up all Americans
and secure the American Dream for generations to come.**
