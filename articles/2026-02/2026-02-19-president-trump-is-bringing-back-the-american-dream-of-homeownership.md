---
 
**President Donald J. Trump is delivering real progress in making
homeownership and housing more affordable for American families.** Now,
[fresh data](https://www.freddiemac.com/pmms) from Freddie Mac shows
that mortgage rates have fallen to their lowest level since September
2022 — underscoring the Trump Administration’s ongoing commitment to
lowering costs for hardworking Americans.

**“This lower rate environment is not only improving affordability for
prospective homebuyers, it’s also strengthening the financial position
of homeowners,”** Freddie Mac
[stated](https://www.freddiemac.com/pmms#:~:text=Mortgage%20rates%20dropped,thousands%20of%20dollars.)
in its latest Primary Mortgage Market Survey.

**These encouraging developments are part of a broader wave of positive
momentum in the housing market under President Trump’s leadership:**

-   The average 30-year fixed mortgage rate
    has [dipped](https://www.freddiemac.com/pmms) to a new [multi-year
    low](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-02-19/us-mortgage-rates-fall-to-lowest-level-in-more-than-three-years),
    driving monthly housing payments to their [most
    affordable](https://www.redfin.com/news/housing-market-update-housing-payments-lowest-level-2-years/) levels
    in over two years and mortgage affordability to a [four-year
    high](https://www.housingwire.com/articles/mortgage-rates-refinance-2026/).
-   The National Association of Realtors’ [Housing Affordability
    Index](https://www.nar.realtor/research-and-statistics/housing-statistics/housing-affordability-index) has
    climbed to its [highest
    level](https://www.nar.realtor/newsroom/nar-existing-home-sales-report-shows-8-4-decrease-in-january#:~:text=%E2%80%9CAffordability%20conditions%20are%20improving%2C%20with%20NAR%E2%80%99s%20Housing%20Affordability%20Index%20showing%20that%20housing%20is%20the%20most%20affordable%20it%E2%80%99s%20been%20since%20March%202022.)
    since March 2022 as family incomes rise and rates moderate, marking
    seven straight months of improvement.
-   Apartment rents have
    [declined](https://www.apartmentlist.com/research/national-rent-data)
    for six straight months, dipping to their [lowest
    level](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/29/apartment-rents-just-dropped-to-the-lowest-level-in-4-years.html)
    in four years.
-   In 2025, [62% of
    homebuyers](https://www.wsj.com/real-estate/the-housing-market-is-swinging-toward-buyers-7c24d78e?gaa_at=eafs&gaa_n=AWEtsqc7iz3xdMrHtdBjbTsrxKb-1znrEOF6Znn54-5o_8zaoMgTrU9p6WuQ&gaa_ts=69825fff&gaa_sig=5xkZNNQMzcBx0tL5xLbVUo3E4nQzuBb4Ahh5puGnALGdLiM3n7ppreCpkVw6WFvyhmU92L9pebnolKOTXNLqVQ%3D%3D)
    purchased properties at a discount to the original listing price —
    the highest share since President Trump’s first term.
-   Mortgage refinance applications have [surged
    132%](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/18/mortgage-rates-sink-to-the-lowest-level-in-a-month-sparking-more-refinance-demand.html#:~:text=6.17%25%20from%206.21%25-,Applications%20to%20refinance%20a%20home%20loan%20rose%207%25%20for%20the%20week%20and%20were%20132%25%20higher%20than%20the%20same%20week%20one%20year%20ago.,-Applications%20for%20a),
    allowing homeowners to lock in lower rates and save thousands
    annually.
-   Home purchase applications are up [nearly
    10%](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/18/mortgage-rates-sink-to-the-lowest-level-in-a-month-sparking-more-refinance-demand.html#:~:text=one%20year%20ago.-,Applications%20for%20a%20mortgage%20to%20purchase%20a%20home%20dropped%203%25%20for%20the%20week%20and%20were%20just%208%25%20higher%20than%20the%20same%20week%20one%20year%20ago.,-In%20an%20aerial)
    over last year, signaling growing confidence among buyers.
-   Builders are accelerating construction, with housing starts at a
    [five-month
    high](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-02-18/us-housing-starts-rise-to-five-month-high-in-broad-increase).

**President Trump is also taking decisive action to further drive down
costs and expand access to homeownership:**

-   President Trump
    [directed](https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/115861588073149023) Fannie
    Mae and Freddie Mac to purchase $200 billion in mortgage-backed
    securities to further [drive
    down](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/08/trump-mortgage-bonds-rates-fannie-freddie.html)
    borrowing costs.
-   President Trump [took executive
    action](https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump/posts/115855059527504524) to
    prohibit large institutional investors from buying single-family
    homes that should be available for everyday American families.
-   The Trump Administration
    [barred](https://nypost.com/2025/03/26/us-news/trump-admin-bars-illegal-migrants-and-non-permanent-residents-from-receiving-taxpayer-backed-mortgages/)
    illegal aliens from accessing taxpayer-backed mortgages, ensuring
    resources are directed to U.S. citizens.
-   The Trump Administration
    [eliminated](https://www.hud.gov/news/hud-no-25-034) the burdensome
    Affirmatively Furthering Fair Housing rule, saving Americans [tens
    of millions of
    dollars](https://www.cato.org/blog/what-secretary-carson-should-know-about-affirmatively-furthering-fair-housing-affh#:~:text=There%20are%20other%20problems%20with%20AFFH.%20For%20example%2C%20AFFH%20requires%20federal%20and%20local%20government%20to%20spend%20up%20to%20%2455%20million%20annually%20collecting%20information%2C%20a%20good%20deal%20of%20which%20is%20unhelpful%20to%20its%20objective%20of%20understanding%20why%20racial%20segregation%20occurs.)
    annually and streamlining local housing decisions.

**The Trump Administration remains fully committed to building a strong,
accessible housing market that works for every American family.** This
includes continued efforts to combat inflation, support job creation,
increase housing supply, and promote long-term prosperity — so that all
Americans can thrive and own their piece of the American Dream.
